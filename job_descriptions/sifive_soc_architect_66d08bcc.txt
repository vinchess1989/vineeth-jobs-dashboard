Job Description:

The Role:

SiFive, a leader in RISC-V CPU IPs, is looking for an experienced leader in helping SiFive to research and develop next generation high performance SoC. The SoC architect will guide the team to develop system IPs such as IOMMU, interrupt controllers, memory controllers and a wide variety of IPs to be used in datacenter and networking SoCs . He will also be deeply involved in the platform security and will help to develop groundbreaking new security features.

What you will do:

Define features


Architect system IPs like IOMMU and system level solutions.
Define the platform security requirements and define the architecture of the needed security features.
Work with Product Management, Product Architect to define architecture / product specifications
Design and carry out experiments to research and develop architecture / micro-architecture features
Work with the design team to implement and deliver system IPs


Interface with customers


The SoC architect will have a chance to interact directly with customers to understand their needs, use cases and to help resolve their concerns/issues.


Interface with RVIA


The SoC architect will participate in various SIG/TG/HC related to the system architecture.


Requirements:


MS or PhD in Computer Science / Computer Architecture
10+ years of experience in SoC architecture
5+ years involved in the engineering team to deliver HW products.
Has experience in SoC design flow, including spec definition, microarchitecture design, and performance modeling.
Knowledgeable in CPU architectures, power management and SoC design.
Deep understanding in platform hardware, operating systems, device drivers and firmware interactions.
