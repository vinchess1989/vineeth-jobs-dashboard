Manager | Semiconductor, Embedded Systems, CPU, GPU

Digital Hardware Design Engineer - ASIC / SoC / RISC-V

This is with a rapidly growing global semiconductor company with a huge R&D Center in the south of Ireland.




Requirements / Skills Needed

CPU Microarchitecture experience
BsC in Science, Engineering, or related field.
RTL design, System Verilog, Tcl/Perl/Python shell-scripting skills
3+ years of RTL design experience on-chip with custom digital logic
Knowledge of sensor hardware for ASIC / SoC
Experience working with RISC-V (ISA) and hardware architectures.
Experienced with ASIC RTL Synthesis, LEC, Power Extraction tools (PTPX), Primetime, RTL Linting tools and extensive usage of simulation tools.
Experience with ASIC flow, RTL sanity tools specific to Design Rule Checking and Clock Domain Crossing checks is a plus
Experience with C++/SystemC is a plus
Familiarity with MBIST and DFT flow




Keywords: Digital / Design / System Verilog / SoC / RTL / TCL/Perl / ASIC /
