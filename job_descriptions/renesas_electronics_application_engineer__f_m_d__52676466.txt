Job Description



Principal Accountabilities:



Perform silicon device evaluation at chip and board level, documenting results and identifying potential issues or errata.
Support evaluation planning and execution for assigned products or features.
Assist in the development of evaluation collateral, including reports and test setups.
Contribute to development and maintenance of software for automated measurements under guidance.
Follow established evaluation methodologies and contribute incremental improvements.
Support customer design-in and ramp activities by assisting in the resolution of application and device-related issues.
Collaborate with Design, Test, Product, and Quality Engineering teams to support issue investigation and resolution.
Build effective working relationships and contribute to team objectives.


Qualifications


Degree in Electronics Engineering or a related discipline.
2+ years of relevant experience.
Working knowledge of silicon evaluation techniques and lab practices.
Ability to solve moderately complex problems using established methods.
Exercises judgment within defined procedures; seeks guidance when needed.
Able to work independently on routine tasks and collaboratively on complex issues.
Good analytical, communication, and teamwork skills.
Willingness to learn and develop technical expertise.
Flexible to undertake occasional international travel if required
