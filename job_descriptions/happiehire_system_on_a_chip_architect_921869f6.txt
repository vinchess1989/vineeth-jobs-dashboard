Designation: SOC Chip Lead/ Design Implementation Manager

Experience: 12–15 Years

Reports To: CEO/VP Engineering

Location: Bangalore, India




Job Description:

This role combines full chip ownership with functional team leadership, ensuring first-time-right

silicon, scalable architecture, and a strong RTL organization for future platforms.




Key Responsibilities:

• Own SoC execution from spec → architecture → RTL/Mixed Signal design → MPWs → mass

production

• Act as single technical owner (Chip Lead) for the SoC

• Drive PPA, quality, cost, and schedule trade-offs

• Lead execution across RTL, DV, DFT, PD and Validation

• Interface with Architecture, IP, FW/SW, Product, Business teams, and the PE team

• Own pre-silicon sign-off and post-silicon bring-up & validation

• Lead and grow the design team

• Ensure high-quality micro-architecture, coding, reuse, and integration

• Balance hands-on technical leadership with people management




Educational Qualifications

• Bachelor's/Master's degree in Electrical Engineering or equivalent

• 12+ years of experience in complex SoC/ASIC development

• Leadership experience across multiple SoC domains

• Strong expertise in ARM-based systems, interconnects, low-power design, and clock/reset/power architecture and wireless experience.

• End-to-end SoC lifecycle exposure, including RTL → PnR → timing closure

• Strong pre- and post-silicon debug skills, with multiple successful tape-outs

• Excellent communication and cross-team leadership skills




Preferred Experience:

• Prior experience in a Chip Lead / SoC Lead role

• Industrial SoC exposure

• Post-silicon bring-up experience
