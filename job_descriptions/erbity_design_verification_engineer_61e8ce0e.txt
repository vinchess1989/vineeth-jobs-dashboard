Erbity Private Limited is looking for an experienced Design Verification Engineer to join our dynamic engineering team. The successful candidate will play a key role in verifying complex digital designs to ensure functionality, reliability, and performance before production.

Responsibilities:


Develop and execute comprehensive verification plans and testbenches using industry-standard methodologies
Implement test cases and automate verification processes to improve efficiency
Analyze verification results, identify issues, and collaborate with design teams to resolve defects effectively
Maintain verification documentation, including test reports and coverage analysis
Participate in design reviews to provide feedback on testability and implementation
Ensure compliance with project schedules and quality standards


Requirements

Technical Skills:


Strong proficiency with SystemVerilog and UVM verification methodology
Solid understanding of digital design concepts and hardware description languages like Verilog or VHDL
Experience with simulation and debugging tools such as Synopsys VCS, Cadence Xcelium, or Questa
Familiarity with scripting languages like Python or Tcl for automation purposes
Good knowledge of verification techniques including constrained-random testing, coverage-driven verification, and assertions


Qualifications & Experience:


Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or related fields
Proven experience as a Design Verification Engineer or similar role in semiconductor or electronics industry
Strong problem-solving skills with attention to detail
Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
