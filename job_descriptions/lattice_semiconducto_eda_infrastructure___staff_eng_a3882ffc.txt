About Lattice Semiconductor

Lattice Semiconductor is the low power FPGA leader. We solve customer problems across the network from the Edge to the Cloud with our industry-leading FPGA, video ASSP, millimeter wave, and IP products. Our technology powers a wide range of applications — from smart factories and 5G infrastructure to automotive systems and edge AI.
