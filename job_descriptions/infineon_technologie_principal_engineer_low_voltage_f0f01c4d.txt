Are you in?

Your Role

Key responsibilities in your new role

Our Villach team is looking for an experienced technology developer with a strong drive to lead a cross-functional project team in a multi-site environment.


Lead technology development and innovation projects in the field of Power MOSFETs with significant impact on Infineon’s future technologies
Drive innovation from device concept definition and process flow development through validation to mass production release
Take ownership of designing novel device and process concepts, including cost estimations and decision-making matrices
Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to plan, execute, and evaluate cleanroom experiments
Lead reliability investigations, electrical characterization, and application testing to validate product performance and robustness
Ensure process stability, high quality standards, and cost optimization of manufacturing flows for successful mass production release


Your Profile

Qualifications And Skills To Help You Succeed


University degree (Master’s or PhD) in Physics, Electrical Engineering, Materials Science, or a related field
5+ years of experience in semiconductor device engineering and process integration, including device physics and electrical behavior, unit process modules, cleanroom operations, design of experiments, reliability testing, electrical characterization, failure analysis, and IP generation
Proven track record in managing technology development projects; Project Management certification is a strong advantage
Strong leadership skills, including coaching and decision-making, combined with excellent interpersonal abilities such as negotiation and conflict resolution
Strong problem-solving mindset, passion for innovation, resilience, strategic thinking, and excellent communication and presentation skills
Excellent written and spoken English skills; German language skills are considered a plus


This position is subject to the collective agreement for workers and employees in the electrical and electronics industry, at least employment group I (https://www.feei.at/leistungen/informations-service/mindestlohne-und-gehalter). The monthly salary is paid 14 times p.a. A higher payment is negotiable depending on your expertise and skills. Furthermore, Infineon offers additional employee benefits.
