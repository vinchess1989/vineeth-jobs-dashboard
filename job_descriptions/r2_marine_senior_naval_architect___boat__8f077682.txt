About R2 Marine

R2 Marine is a design and naval architecture company specialized in commercial and pleasure craft design. We are a team of naval architects, engineers, and designers. We solve problems using creativity as a tool. Our extensive knowledge of production processes and versatile project experience allow us to approach every project holistically and ensure an agile, solution-centered design process.

We are committed to building a great place to work and to grow as individuals and professionals.

Our office is located in Hernesaari, Helsinki.

Job Description

We are looking for an experienced Naval Architect to strengthen our naval architecture team. In this role, you will take a leading position within projects, with responsibility for the full naval architecture scope, from concept development to detailed design and production support.

You are comfortable leading projects and taking ownership, while working hands-on on naval architectural tasks. You enjoy coordinating technical work, guiding others, and acting as a technical point of contact within multidisciplinary teams.

This is a full-time, permanent position that will be filled as soon as a suitable person is found.

Responsibilities


Lead the naval architecture scope within projects, from concept to production support
Take ownership of naval architectural deliverables and technical quality
Coordinate and guide naval architecture work within multidisciplinary project teams
Perform and review hull design, stability analyses, regulatory compliance, and related calculations
Act as a technical interface towards clients, partners, and other stakeholders
Support shipyards and production teams with naval architectural expertise
Mentor and support junior team members as the team grows
Contribute to the development of internal standards, tools, and working methods


Preferred Requirements


Bachelor or Master degree in Naval Architecture or equivalent professional experience
Proven experience in naval architecture with small craft and vessels under 40 meters
Proven experience in leading projects or taking a coordinating technical role
Ability to take ownership and responsibility for the full naval architecture scope within a project
Strong understanding of hull design, stability, rules & regulations, and production processes
Fluent in English (spoken and written); Finnish language skills are considered a plus
Strong self-management and organisational skills
Proactive, responsible, and solution-oriented way of working
Motivation to grow into a leadership role and lead a team
