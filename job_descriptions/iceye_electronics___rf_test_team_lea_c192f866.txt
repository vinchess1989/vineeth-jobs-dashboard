Why This Role Matters

As an Electronics and RF Test Team Lead you will support the company's growth as we continue to scale our engineering department. The team supports the qualification of new satellite technologies and the validation and testing of our next-generation satellite systems. Responsibilities also include developing test jigs, constructing test setups, and preparing documentation for our production line.

Who We Are

ICEYE is the world leader in sovereign intelligence from space. We deliver persistent monitoring capabilities to detect and respond to changes in any location on Earth.

ICEYE owns the world's largest and most advanced SAR (synthetic aperture radar) satellite constellation. To our customers we provide intelligence with unmatched quality, latency and revisit times, in any weather, day or night. To governments who choose to operate their own constellation we provide this proven capability as a sovereign system.

ICEYE-built constellations serve customers in defence and intelligence, environmental monitoring, insurance and emergency management. We enable fast decisions that contribute to a safer future.

Founded and headquartered in Finland, ICEYE operates globally with over 1000 employees across Europe, North America, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific.

Your Day-to-day Responsibilities


Lead, manage, and mentor the Electronics and RF Test Team
Interpret the team OKRs and delegate tasks in order to achieve the domain goals
Lead and contribute to trade studies, design reviews, and verification and qualification documentation
Ensure smooth and timely product delivery from concept through qualification
Mentor and support the professional development of team members, helping them grow in their careers and take on new challenges.
Support integration, validation, and issue resolution across internal and external teams
Report progress, risks, and key metrics clearly to upper management and program stakeholders.
