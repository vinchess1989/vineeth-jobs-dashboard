STA Design Engineer - ASIC / Static Timing Analysis / Semiconductor / Physical Design




We are currently partnered with a leading semiconductor technology company developing next-generation high-performance, low-power ASICs and System-on-Chip (SoC) solutions for advanced computing applications. As part of their continued growth, they are looking to hire an STA Design Engineer to play a key role in timing sign-off, timing closure, and design optimisation across complex silicon products.










Key responsibilities

Perform static timing analysis (STA) and timing closure across complex ASIC blocks and full-chip SoC designs
Develop, validate, and maintain timing constraints throughout the ASIC design lifecycle
Analyse gate-level post-place-and-route (PnR) netlists to identify and resolve timing violations
Collaborate with RTL, synthesis, physical design, and implementation teams to optimise performance, power, and area (PPA)
Develop and implement timing ECOs to resolve setup, hold, and other timing issues
Drive timing sign-off activities to ensure designs meet performance, power, and quality targets
Improve STA methodologies, design flows, and automation to increase efficiency and reduce turnaround times







Key requirements

Strong commercial experience in Static Timing Analysis (STA) within ASIC or SoC development
Hands-on expertise with industry-standard STA tools such as PrimeTime, PrimeShield, Tempus, or equivalent
Experience performing timing closure and implementing timing ECO methodologies
Strong understanding of ASIC implementation flows from RTL through GDSII
Experience analysing post-layout timing and collaborating with synthesis and physical design teams




Keywords

STA Design Engineer / Static Timing Analysis / STA / ASIC Design / SoC Design / Physical Design / Timing Closure / Timing Sign-Off / PrimeTime / PrimeShield / Tempus / PrimeClosure / PTECO / Tweaker / RTL / RTL-to-GDS / Gate-Level Netlists / Place and Route / PnR / Physical Implementation / ASIC Implementation / Python / Tcl / Perl / Semiconductor / Silicon Design / Low Power Design / High Performance Computing / Chip Design / Timing ECO / Design Automation / VLSI
