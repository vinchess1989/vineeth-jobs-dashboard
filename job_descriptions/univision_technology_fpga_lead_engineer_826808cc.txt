🚀 Hiring: FPGA Lead Engineer | Bangalore




We are looking for an experienced FPGA Lead Engineer to join our team in Bangalore.




📍 Location: Bangalore

⏳ Notice Period: Immediate to 15 Days

💼 Experience: 10+ Years




🔹 Key Skills & Responsibilities:

• Strong experience in Intel/Altera FPGAs (Agilex, Stratix-10, Arria-10)

• Expertise in FPGA system architecture and design

• Hands-on experience with PCIe Gen4/Gen5 Hard IP

• Strong knowledge of Quartus Prime Pro and Platform Designer

• Experience in timing closure, floorplanning, and transceiver configuration

• Good understanding of NVMe/PCIe protocols, DMA engines, and high-speed digital design

• Ability to lead FPGA design reviews, integration activities, and performance optimization

• Expertise in CDC analysis, SDC constraints, SignalTap debugging, and RTL quality improvement

• Experience mentoring and guiding FPGA development teams




📩 Interested candidates can share their updated resume to:

gunashree.k@univisiontechnocon.com
