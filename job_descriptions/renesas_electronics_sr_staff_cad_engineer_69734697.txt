Job Description



We are seeking a Sr Staff Engineer – CAD to develop and deploy Frontend and Backend tools and flows for Digital Designs done in the company. This role is part of the global CAD team.



Job Description



Own some part of the overall CAD solution and be responsible for WW engagement with customers.
Good understanding of Digital Implementation Methodologies.
Hands-on in development & debug of CAD flows and with industry leading EDA tools in Digital Designs
Proactively engage with customers through regular follow-ups, tool training sessions, and consistent support throughout the entire deployment process.
Hands-on in mature software development practices, leading programming languages for potential AI/ML development tasks
Expected to work in a dynamic design environment
Evaluate EDA tools and flows for improved design efficiency and turnaround time
Experienced in leading small team.


Qualifications


BTech/MTech in Electrical/Electronic Engineering, Computer Engineering or Computer Science with 10+ years of experience in EDA/CAD.
Proven experience in supporting mature-node technologies (e.g., 22nm, 12nm).
Expertise in Synopsys/Cadence RTL to GDS digital design flow.

(e.g., synthesis tools, implementation tools and STA tools)
Strong scripting skills (Tcl, Perl, Python) for automation of RTL to GDS digital design flow (e.g., synthesis, implementation tools and STA flows)
Strong communication skills (written and verbal), problem solving, teamwork, attention to detail, commitment to task, and quality focus
Can – do attitude, openness to new environment, people and culture
Ability to propose innovative and leading-edge solutions
Strong drive & ability to coordinate work across highly experienced global teams
