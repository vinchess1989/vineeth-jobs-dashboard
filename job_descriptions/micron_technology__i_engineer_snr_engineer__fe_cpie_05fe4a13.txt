Micron Technology is a world leader in innovating memory and storage solutions that accelerate the transformation of information into intelligence, inspiring the world to learn, communicate and advance faster than ever.

Responsibilities and Tasks
Drive front‑end yield analysis at wafer, lot, and process‑step levels to identify systematic yield detractors and improvement opportunities.
Perform cross‑site yield benchmarking across multiple fabs to identify performance gaps, best‑in‑class behaviors, and transferable best‑known methods (BKMs).
Lead and support yield‑focused business process optimization and transformation, including standardization of yield analysis methodologies, metrics, dashboards, and operating models across sites.
Partner with Fab, Central YE, TD, and Product Engineering teams to align yield strategies, priorities, and execution frameworks across the network.
Conduct data‑driven root cause analysis using manufacturing, SPC, and test datasets to accelerate learning cycles and enable sustainable yield improvement.
Act as a bridge between site execution and central alignment, ensuring local yield learnings are captured, standardized, and scaled across the Micron network.
Support new technology introduction and yield ramp by defining front‑end yield readiness criteria, monitoring mechanisms, and escalation models.
Drive continuous improvement of yield‑related business processes, including issue tracking, escalation, closure, and learning loop effectiveness.
Communicate yield insights, benchmark results, and process improvement proposals clearly and effectively to technical and management audiences.
Build and maintain strong working relationships with cross‑functional partners and site stakeholders in a global, matrixed environment.
Travel to Micron fab sites as needed to support yield analysis, benchmarking, and business process alignment.
Requirements
Strong background in front‑end semiconductor manufacturing and yield engineering, including wafer fabrication processes, SPC, parametric data, and yield analysis techniques.
Proven experience in yield data analysis, statistical methods, and systematic problem solving.
Experience with cross‑site or network‑level benchmarking and translating insights into actionable improvements.
Demonstrated capability in business process optimization, standardization, or transformation, particularly in engineering or manufacturing environments.
Familiarity with DRAM device operation and front‑end integration is highly desirable.
Strong analytical, troubleshooting, and critical‑thinking skills with a high attention to detail.
Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to present complex technical topics clearly.
Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a global, cross‑functional organization.
Strong organizational skills and ability to manage multiple priorities effectively.
Intermediate to advanced PC skills, including Microsoft Office; experience with data analytics tools or scripting languages is a plus.
Self‑motivated, proactive, and comfortable driving change and alignment across teams.
Travel Required: Yes
Travel Frequency: Approximately 20–40%
Education

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Microelectronics, Physics, Materials Science, Chemistry, or a related engineering discipline.
