Signal Integrity (SI) and Power Integrity (PI) Engineers – Bangalore (Electronic City)

Experience: 2–7 Years

Location: Electronic City, Bangalore

Job Description

We are looking for an experienced Signal Integrity (SI) Engineer to support high-speed interface development and validation for next-generation semiconductor products. The candidate will work on advanced technologies including LPDDR6, PCIe Gen5, USB3.2 Gen2, MIPI CSI, UFS 4.0, HDMI, and 10G Ethernet.

Key Responsibilities


Perform channel modeling, extraction, and eye analysis for high-speed interfaces.
Conduct pre-layout and post-layout SI simulations to ensure compliance with interface standards.
Analyze crosstalk, reflections, jitter, insertion loss, and return loss.
Collaborate with design, package, and PCB teams to optimize signal integrity performance.
Generate technical reports and provide design recommendations based on simulation results.
Support debugging and validation activities for high-speed interfaces.
All SI tasks described above (channel modeling, extractions, eye analysis).
Perform power integrity extractions and simulations for high-speed interfaces.
Model and analyze package/board PDN
Define decoupling strategy and validate against system requirements.
Provide design guidelines balancing both SI and PI constraints .
Required Skills & Qualifications


Strong understanding of signal integrity concepts for high-speed serial and memory interfaces.
Hands-on experience with:
HFSS / Clarity
Siwave / PowerSI
SystemSI / ADS / AEDT
Familiarity with JEDEC, LPDDR5/LPDDR6, PCIe, and UCIe standards.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
Strong background in both signal and power integrity .
Hands-on experience with SI tools (listed above).
Proficiency with PI extraction/simulation tools (e.g., PowerSI, SIwave, AEDT, HSPICE, equivalent).
Knowledge of DDR, PCIe, UCIe standards and PDN design best practices.
Strong analytical and communication skills.
Ability to work effectively in cross-functional engineering teams.
Preferred Skills


Experience with advanced package and PCB analysis.
Exposure to high-speed interface validation and compliance testing.
Good communication and documentation skills.
