About GF

GlobalFoundries® Inc. (GF®) is one of the world's leading semiconductor manufacturers. GF redefines innovation and semiconductor manufacturing by developing and delivering feature-rich process technology solutions with leading performance in all growth markets. GF offers a unique mix of design, development and manufacturing services. With a talented and diverse team and manufacturing locations in the U.S., Europe and Asia, GF is a trusted technology provider to its global customers. GF employs approximately 13,000 people, including more than 3,000 in Dresden, Germany.

For more information, visit www.gf.com.

Introduction

You will be part of the advanced technology development team turning state of the art research into innovative volume production ready technology features. Our technology development team is embedded in the GlobalFoundries’ state-of-the-art 300 mm wafer fab (Fab1) in Dresden, Germany.

This position is established to strengthen our Next-Generation FDX technology development team, with the objective of advancing our FDX platform toward the next-generation FDSOI node.

In the role of Experienced Process Integrator, you will serve as a critical interface between research and manufacturing, assuming technical ownership for the development and integration of process flow modules (FEoL, MoL, BEoL). Your contributions will be essential in delivering industry-leading performance and enabling competitive differentiation for our customers.

Your Job


Physical and electrical characterization of advanced semiconductor features.
Design and analysis of experiments to improve specific performance parameter.
Coordinate technology development tasks in a volume production infrastructure.
Collaborate with cross functional teams to address and solve highly cross-linked technology challenges for optimized quality, performance, and manufacturability.
Networks with research institutes explore opportunities bridging leading scientific research into industry applications.
Collaborate with customers and drive technical solutions to support customer needs.
Define and run technical feasibility studies.
Provide technical support to other teams and departments.
Communicates and presents project results and updates to key stakeholders, both internal and external to GlobalFoundries.


Required Qualifications


M.Sc. in Physics, Electrical Engineering, Materials Science, Chemistry, or other relevant technical discipline is required; PhD will be beneficial.
Profound knowledge of modern CMOS process integration and its key techniques, such as CVD, lithography, RIE, etc.
In–depth experience in FDSOI.
Knowledge in Test Chip Design, T/O, Design Rules, Technology-Design Interactions.
Solid foundation in statistics, data analysis techniques and tools.
Both, the ability to work in high-performance cross-functional teams as well as to work independently is a prerequisite.
Excellent English skills are required; proficiency in the German language is preferred.
Strong interpersonal skills.
