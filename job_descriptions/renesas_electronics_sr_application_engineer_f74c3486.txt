Job Description



Are you an experienced engineer passionate about bringing cutting-edge silicon products to life? We’re looking for a driven Applications Engineer to lead complex mixed-signal IC evaluations, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and support customers through design-in and ramp phases. If you thrive in a fast-paced environment and enjoy owning technical challenges from concept to delivery, this role is for you.



Perform physical silicon device evaluation at chip and board level and document errata to support necessary device modifications.
Take on product lead role. This includes planning evaluation, supporting collateral development, reporting to management and customers, and reviewing evaluation documents.
Support the development of software for automated measurements.
Independently develop ideas to improve the silicon evaluation flow.
Interface directly with customers during their design-in and ramp phases to resolve application and device-related issues.
Drive interactions with Design Engineering, Test Engineering, Product Engineering and Quality Engineering to resolve product issues.
Mentor Junior Applications Engineers and assist with the hiring and interview process.


Qualifications


Typically 5 – 8 years experience
Ability to work with minimal instruction – either independently or collaboratively
Able to confidently share own expertise and work to others
Able to make significant contribution to projects, programs and business initiatives with creativity and ingenuity
Ability to support and mentor junior team members
Cross cultural awareness and sensitivity
Results-oriented and able to deliver on-time under a tight schedule
Flexible to undertake occasional international travel at short notice
Excellent command of verbal and written English

Essential Technical skills



Experience in python for use in test scripts
Ability to produce evaluation plans for complex mixed signal ICs
Familiar working with test equipment; scopes, source meters, function generators, network analysers etc.
PCB schematic generation and understand good layout practises

Desirable Technical skills



Knowledge of switcher topologies
Knowledge of battery management systems, such as fuel gauge and charger
