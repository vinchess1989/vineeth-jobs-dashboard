Overview




Keysight is at the forefront of technology innovation, delivering breakthroughs and trusted insights in electronic design, simulation, prototyping, test, manufacturing, and optimization. Our ~16,800 employees create world-class solutions in communications, 5G, automotive, energy, quantum, aerospace, defense, and semiconductor markets for customers in over 100 countries. Learn more about what we do.




Our award-winning culture embraces a bold vision of where technology can take us and a passion for tackling challenging problems with industry-first solutions. We believe that when people feel a sense of belonging, they can be more creative, innovative, and thrive at all points in their careers.




Keysight Technologies Finland Oy seeks a senior hardware engineer with experience in digital PCA designs.




As an R&D Hardware Engineer, You will be a key member of R&D team who research and develop world leading solutions.




Responsibilities




Main responsibility is working with digital board designs
Define and drive specifications, component selection, and design strategies
Design activities across entire product lifecycle —from concept, schematic design, PCB layout, prototyping, validation, to production support, ensuring performance, reliability, and manufacturability
Ensure all electronic designs meet regulatory standards
Close collaboration with firmware and mechanical engineer team throughout the product lifecycle
Work with order fulfillment, suppliers, manufacturing partners, and technology providers to deliver cost optimized designs and components
In a dynamically changing technology environment, continuous learning is expected and supported throughout your career




Qualifications




University degree with more than 7 years of experience in industry
Experience in high-speed data converters, modern FPGAs, SoCs, and system theory
Hands-on experience with mixed-signal electronic and PCB design (layout & schematic is a must)
Understanding of signal integrity and power integrity principles
Ability to work independently and under pressure
Proactive and willing to develop oneself, and to learn about new things related to AI and tool development
Practical experience working in an industrial R&D environment
Strong problem-solving
Good communication in English (written and spoken)
