Renesas is one of the top global semiconductor companies in the world. We strive to develop a safer, healthier, greener, and smarter world, and our goal is to make every endpoint intelligent by offering product solutions in the automotive, industrial, infrastructure and IoT markets. Our robust product portfolio includes world-leading MCUs, SoCs, analog and power products, plus Winning Combination solutions that curate these complementary products. We are a key supplier to the world’s leading manufacturers of electronics you rely on every day; you may not see our products, but they are all around you.





Job Description



We are looking for a passionate and driven Entry Level Analog Design Engineer to join our hardware development team. This is an exciting opportunity to work on cutting-edge analog and mixed-signal designs and learn from experienced engineers in a collaborative and innovative environment. You will contribute to the design of complex ASIC systems and gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and methodologies.







Responsibilities:



Take part in the design and verification of CMOS analog blocks for custom ASICs, ensuring performance, area, power, and schedule requirements are met.
Collaborate with an experienced mentor who will guide your early career development.
Prepare clear and accurate documentation for designed circuits.
Learn the importance of cross discipline collaboration with other teams such as layout, digital design, DV, test to ensure successful delivery of the product to mass production


Qualifications


Minimum Bachelor’s degree in Electronics, Microelectronics Engineering, or a related discipline.
Understanding of analog circuit fundamentals (e.g., amplifiers, oscillators, LDOs, comparators).
Familiarity with EDA tools and Cadence design environment is a plus.
Ability to work collaboratively in a multi-disciplinary team.
Good problem-solving skills and willingness to learn new techniques.
Strong verbal and written communication skills.
Results-oriented and able to manage tasks under guidance.
Flexibility for occasional international travel.
Enthusiasm for innovation and continuous improvement.
