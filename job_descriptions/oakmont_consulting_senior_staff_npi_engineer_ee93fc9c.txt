Senior & Staff Level NPI Product Engineer roles

Bristol (Hybrid – 4 days onsite Mon–Thurs)

Salary: depending on experience (competitive), plus 20% bonus and great benefits package




I’m working with a high-growth semiconductor company developing advanced analog/mixed-signal and optical technologies used in next-generation connectivity systems.

They’re building out their NPI/Product Engineering capability and are looking for 2 engineers, someone who wants to take real ownership across the full product lifecycle - not just sit in a single function.




The role

This is a genuinely end-to-end product engineering position, supporting new silicon from early concept through to high-volume production and beyond.

They’ll be working closely with design, test, validation and operations teams, helping to shape how products are tested, characterised and ultimately manufactured at scale.

It’s a strong opportunity for someone who wants to move beyond pure test or validation and develop into a well-rounded product engineer.




What they’ll be doing

Supporting new product introduction from concept through to production release
Contributing to test strategy, DfT and ATE development
Driving product characterisation across PVT and validating datasheet performance
Analysing data to define test limits, improve yield and optimise test time
Supporting manufacturing ramp, yield improvement and failure analysis
Collaborating cross-functionally to ensure smooth transfer into production




What they’re looking for

Background in semiconductor engineering (product, test, validation or design)
Understanding of analog/mixed-signal ICs
Experience or exposure to ATE and/or lab-based characterisation
Strong data analysis mindset (Excel, JMP or similar)
An interest in how products move from development into manufacturing




Why this role stands out

Full visibility across the product lifecycle - not siloed
Strong technical mentorship and development into a true product owner
Exposure to complex yield, test and manufacturing challenges
Collaborative, high-performance engineering environment
Opportunity to work on advanced semiconductor technologies with real-world impact







If they’re someone who enjoys understanding the whole system, not just their piece of it, this is the kind of role that can accelerate their career quickly.




For more information, please contact Hannah from Oakmont Consulting - hannah@oakmontconsulting.co.uk
