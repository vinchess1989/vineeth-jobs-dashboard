***NOTE - A LOT OF SUITABLE CANDIDATES WILL WORK AS A PROJECT MANAGER FOR DATA CENTER VENDORS




Are you an organised and driven project professional with experience delivering mission-critical infrastructure projects?




We are seeking an OFCI Package Manager to support the delivery of large-scale hyperscale data centre projects across Europe. This role offers the opportunity to manage the coordination, tracking and successful integration of key owner-furnished equipment throughout the project lifecycle, working alongside contractors, vendors, client teams and commissioning stakeholders.




As OFCI Package Manager, you will play a critical role in ensuring equipment packages are delivered, installed and handed over in line with project programme requirements. This is an excellent opportunity for candidates looking to develop their career within one of the fastest-growing sectors in construction and technology infrastructure.







Key Responsibilities

• Manage assigned OFCI equipment packages from procurement through installation and handover.

• Coordinate with vendors, contractors, client teams and commissioning stakeholders to ensure programme milestones are achieved.

• Track equipment manufacturing, delivery schedules and site readiness requirements.

• Monitor package progress and proactively identify risks, delays and mitigation actions.

• Support logistics planning, material management and delivery coordination.

• Ensure accurate reporting on package status, risks and key project metrics.

• Review technical documentation, installation requirements and vendor deliverables.

• Support quality inspections, testing, commissioning and closeout activities.

• Maintain strong stakeholder relationships across project and client teams.

• Ensure all activities are delivered in accordance with project safety, quality and compliance requirements.




Candidate Requirements

• Degree, diploma or equivalent qualification in Engineering, Construction Management or a related discipline.

• Experience working on data centre, industrial, pharmaceutical, semiconductor or other large-scale construction projects.

• Understanding of project delivery, logistics coordination and stakeholder management.

• Strong organisational and reporting skills with the ability to manage multiple workstreams.

• Experience coordinating suppliers, subcontractors or equipment vendors.

• Knowledge of commissioning, turnover and handover processes is advantageous.

• Excellent communication and relationship-building skills.

• Proficient in Microsoft Office and project management software.

• Detail-oriented, proactive and capable of working in a fast-paced project environment.




What's On Offer

• Opportunity to work on industry-leading hyperscale data centre projects.

• Exposure to complex mission-critical infrastructure and global technology clients.

• Clear progression opportunities within a rapidly growing sector.

• Competitive salary and benefits package.

• Collaborative and high-performing project teams.
