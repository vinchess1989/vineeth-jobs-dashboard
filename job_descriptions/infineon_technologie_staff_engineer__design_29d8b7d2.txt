Design Implementation and a solid understanding of SoC Design/Integration who can own and drive SoC /Synthesis and Front End Signoff topics.

Your Role

Key responsibilities in your new role


Be responsible for defining state-of-the-art SoC integration for mixed signal designs, including participation in related tool evaluations
Own and drive SoC Integration/Synthesis and Front End Signoff topics.
Drive best practices and lessons learnt from evaluations/benchmarks and customer interactions back into Methodology development
Share knowledge with other analog/digital integration experts and contribute to Methodology Expert Groups.
Closely cooperate with analog circuit and physical design engineers, as well as with the digital design counterparts.


Your Profile

Qualifications And Skills To Help You Succeed


A university degree in Electrical Engineering, Microelectronic or comparable
A strong understanding of SOC Design and Low power, CDC/LINT tools.
5 to 8+ years of experience of direct Industry experience.
Experience with Cadence or Synopsys tools in Digital Implementation and Verification (Synthesis, PnR, STA)
Hands on with Analog Block Integration at SoC .
In-depth knowledge of Low Power Implementation and UPF.
Strong experience in scripting skills (PERL, TCL, Python).
