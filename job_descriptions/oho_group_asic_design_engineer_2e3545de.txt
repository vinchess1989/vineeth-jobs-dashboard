ASIC Design Engineer - California Startups - $250k+ equity




Leading startups across California are currently hiring for ASIC design engineers to pioneer development in revolutionary technology in the fields of AI and GPU technology. This is perfect for an engineer who wants to feel like their individual contributions are recognised and integral, wants to work in small but high-quality teams and design novel solutions to new problems from scratch.




What you need

Strong experience across ASIC and SoC design principles
Proven to have worked on the full project lifecycle from prototyping to production
Ready to get hands-on in a fast-moving and high-ownership startup environment
Desire to work with industry leaders from the world's top semiconductor companies
Prior work with AI/LLM systems and memory/fabric interconnect is a plus, alongside familiarity with high-speed protocols.




Alongside high ownership from day one, these roles promise an appealing equity package and massive opportunities for personal and professional growth!
