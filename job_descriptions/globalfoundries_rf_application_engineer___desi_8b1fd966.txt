Title: PrRF Application Engineer - Design

About GlobalFoundries:

GlobalFoundries is a leading full-service semiconductor foundry providing a unique combination of design, development, and fabrication services to some of the world’s most inspired technology companies. With a global manufacturing footprint spanning three continents, GlobalFoundries makes possible the technologies and systems that transform industries and give customers the power to shape their markets. For more information, visit www.gf.com

Introduction

RF Application Engineering team is responsible for providing direct technical support on the Design Enablement tools and Technologies to the customers who are engaged in designing RF and millimetre wave integrated circuits (RFIC’s) in GLOBALFOUNDRIES' state-of-the-art RF SiGe and RFSOI technologies use.

 Your Job will need

Design experience with RF/mmWave designs like LNA, PA, Switches etc.
Experience with industry standard RFIC simulation tools (Keysight ADS, Spectre/RF, Momentum, EMX etc.), design checking and parasitic extraction tools such as Cadence, Mentor and Synopsys DRC, LVS and PEX, and/or silicon technology process development is required
To be responsible to drive client requests and insights (voice of customer) into internal Design Enablement and Technology development teams, resulting in increased competitiveness of GF’s Design Enablement and Technologies in the marketplace, and higher client satisfaction.
To be able to debug any reported bugs in the Design Kit and document clearly to the development team, and work with Design Kit development teams to implement fixes and enhancements
To present Design Kit and Technology trainings to customers

Required Qualifications

Education - B.S., M.S. in E.E, PhD ( Electronics, RF, RFGaN)
Experience – Minimum of 5+ years’ experience
Travel 10%  (flexible)
Fluency in the English Language – Written & verbal – Required
Strong fundamental knowledge with active and passive device FOM like ft, fmax, gm, nfmin, Q, SRF etc
RF, mmWave or Analog Integrated Circuit Design experience
Debugging skills, good documentation and effective communications skills is a must

Preferred Qualifications

Exposure to any semiconductor foundry PDKs specially on the RF technologies is an added advantage for this role
Program management skills would be benefical
