Senior Physical Design Implementation Engineer




Would you like to work for one of the global semiconductor leaders? Now hiring for a Senior Physical Design Implementation Engineer to join the team.




Key experience needed for the Senior Physical Design Implementation Engineer role:




4+ years of hands-on experience working in Physical Design.
Strong grasp of the full PD lifecycle, from netlist handoff through GDS, including floorplanning, power planning, placement, CTS, routing, and associated optimization.
Solid background using advanced-node physical design tools (10nm and below) for implementation and signoff.
Deep understanding of STA concepts, timing-closure strategies, and how timing impacts congestion, routing quality, and power.
Familiarity with power analysis and power-integrity evaluation techniques.
Working knowledge of Physical Verification flows and achieving DRC-clean layouts.




Keywords: ASIC / Physical Design / SoC / RTL / GDS / Place & Route / STA / RTL-to-GDS / IP Design / Digital Design / Cadence / Synopsys / Semiconductor / Timing Closure / PPA Optimization
