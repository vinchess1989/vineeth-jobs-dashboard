Role : Memory/Analog Layout Engineer

Job Location : Bangalore

Notice : Immediate to 30 days




We are looking for a highly motivated Layout Engineer to join our VLSI design team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing high-quality IC layouts for analog, mixed-signal, and custom digital blocks while ensuring compliance with design and manufacturing requirements. The role requires close collaboration with circuit designers, verification engineers, and process teams to deliver robust silicon solutions.




Job Description :




2–5 years of experience in semiconductor layout design 
Design and implement transistor-level and block-level layouts for analog, mixed-signal, and custom digital circuits.
Perform floorplanning, placement, routing, and layout optimization to achieve area, performance, and reliability targets.
Execute and resolve physical verification checks including DRC, LVS, ERC, and antenna violations.
Knowledge of layout techniques such as:

Common centroid matching

Interdigitation

Guard rings

Shielding

ESD-aware layout practices

Hands-on experience with EDA tools such as:

Cadence Virtuoso

Cadence Innovus

Synopsys IC Compiler II

Mentor Calibre

Knowledge of DRC, LVS, ERC, PEX, and EM/IR analysis.
Scripting skills in Tcl, Python, or Perl are desirable.
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Microelectronics, or a related field.




If interested, Please dm with updated Resume with Subject Line "Layout Engineer"
