Silicon Labs (NASDAQ: SLAB) is the leading innovator in low-power wireless connectivity, building embedded technology that connects devices and improves lives. Job description:

As a Research Engineer, you will contribute to the research, design, simulation, and prototyping of advanced wireless locationing algorithms for Silicon Labs’ wireless products, with a focus on Bluetooth Channel Sounding technology. You will work in a collaborative innovation-focused environment to explore next-generation positioning technologies and develop high-quality algorithmic solutions for Global wireless IoT markets.

Responsibilities:


Contribute to the research and development of advanced wireless locationing algorithms with emphasis on Bluetooth Channel Sounding technology
Conduct feasibility studies, simulations, and proof-of-concept implementations for emerging wireless positioning features and technologies
Apply signal-processing, statistical modeling, and machine-learning techniques to improve ranging accuracy, robustness, and overall system performance
Explore and evaluate algorithmic approaches for real-time distance estimation, phase-based ranging, and multipath mitigation
Collaborate with cross-functional engineering teams to help translate research concepts into scalable embedded software solutions
Provide technical contributions to wireless specifications and participate in relevant Bluetooth SIG working group activities where applicable
Document experimental findings, simulation results, and technical recommendations for internal and external stakeholders
Contribute ideas and innovations that enhance Silicon Labs’ wireless locationing technologies and future Bluetooth product capabilities



Skills/Experience:


Strong foundation in Digital Signal Processing, Statistical Signal Processing, Machine Learning, or related algorithmic domains
Demonstrated interest in applied research, experimentation, and innovation-driven development
Experience developing and evaluating algorithms through simulation, modeling, or prototyping
Proficiency in Julia, MATLAB, or Python for algorithm development and analysis
Good understanding of embedded systems, microcontrollers, and implementation considerations for real-world deployment
Ability to investigate complex wireless and signal-processing problems using data-driven and analytical approaches
Familiarity with Bluetooth Low Energy, wireless communication systems, RF concepts, antennas, or audio engineering is a strong plus
Positive attitude toward continuous technical learning, innovation, and collaborative problem solving
Strong communication, presentation, and technical documentation skills
Familiarity with patent generation or innovation disclosure processes is a plus
Experience with artificial intelligence (AI) powered tools and technologies used to enhance productivity, analysis, and decision-making
Fluent written and spoken English



Education requirements:

Preferably Master or PhD level degree in Electrical Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science or similar area.
