Bela Recruitment is recruiting a Packaging Manager in Co. Down, Northern Ireland




Salary £50,000 - £55,000 plus flexitime and amazing perks.




The Company

If you work in food manufacturing or packaging, this is the No1 business for innovation and pushing the sector forward! Already a super advanced business, they are now part of a multi-billion empire, with sites across the globe who share how to innovate and become better. They offer industry leading working conditions, exposure to advanced innovation, career pathways, development and working with super talented people.




Key Responsibilities

As the Packaging Manager, this is more about managing packaging solutions and relationships, than managing staff. You will have several key responsibilities, including -

Group Packaging & Supplier Management – drive improvements in all areas and manage relationships.
Site Support & Operations Collaboration – support internal teams on all packaging matters and support schedulers and buyers with forward planning.
NPD & Technical Collaboration – work closely with NPD, Technical and Process teams. Identify and advise on packaging risks, lead times, tooling and costs, as early as possible.
Production & Trails Support – support production on packaging issues, attend trials and signed off by relevant stakeholders.
Pricing, Costing & Governance – maintain & update packaging standards, vendor pricing etc.
Cost Savings & Value Engineering – identify & lead cost saving and Value Engineering opportunities.

Full job spec is available on request.




Essential Skills & Experience

Proven packaging procurement or packaging management, ideally in a large food manufacturing setting.
Strong knowledge of packaging materials – board, trays, films, labels and the supply chain dynamics.
Experience working in a matrix or group structure, balancing central strategy with site-level delivery.
Strong stakeholder management skills across – Procurement, Operations, NPD and Technical teams.
Commercially astute with experience in pricing mechanisms, cost modelling and savings delivery.
Organised, analytical and capable managing multiple priorities across sites.
Confident influencing suppliers and internal stakeholders at all levels.
