About Us:

At Dabster, we specialize in connecting top talent with leading global companies. We are currently seeking experienced Design Verification Engineers and Leads to join our client's team. Our mission is to be the foremost recruitment specialist in securing exceptional talent for a diverse range of global clients.




Who Will You Work With:

Our client is a leader in technology solutions, and you will be working with teams across the UK and EU. This is an opportunity to be part of a cutting-edge design verification team working on high-performance technologies such as Ethernet, PCIe, AXI, Chip2chip Multilink (CML), and Coherent Hub Interface (CHI).




About the Role:

We have multiple vacancies for Design Verification Engineers and Leads to work on various projects. You will be involved in the verification of designs using advanced tools and methodologies. The initial contract is for 6 months, with a strong possibility of extension.




Key Responsibilities:

For Leads:

Develop test plans and test benches using System Verilog and UVM.
Execute test development, simulation, debugging, and coverage analysis.
Lead the verification process, ensure design coverage, and provide sign-off.
Collaborate with engineers to ensure seamless execution of project tasks.

For Engineers:

Work on test bench development and test execution using System Verilog and UVM.
Perform simulation, debugging, and coverage analysis to ensure verification quality.
Support leads in achieving verification signoff by contributing to key technical tasks.




Preferred Skills:

For Leads:
10+ years of experience in design verification.
Proficiency in System Verilog, UVM, test planning, test bench development, and coverage analysis.
For Engineers:
5+ years of experience in design verification.
Strong skills in System Verilog, UVM, test bench development, and debugging.




What We Offer:

Impactful Work: Contribute to the development of advanced technologies for a global leader.
Fully Remote: This position is 100% remote and open to candidates located in the UK or EU.
Contract Type: Initial 6-month contract with potential for extension.




Interview Process:

Expect one or two rounds of technical interviews followed by a business-focused discussion.




How to Apply:

If you have relevant experience as a Design Verification Engineer or Lead, we'd love to hear from you.

Submit your resume detailing your qualifications via LinkedIn or send it to raj.singh@dabster.net.
