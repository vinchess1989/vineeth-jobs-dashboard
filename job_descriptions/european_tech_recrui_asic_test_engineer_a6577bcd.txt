ASIC Test Engineer




We are currently partnered with an innovative semiconductor technology organisation seeking an experienced ASIC Test Engineer to support the successful qualification, ramp-up, and volume production of advanced ASIC devices. This position offers the opportunity to work closely with customers, test houses, and cross-functional engineering teams to deliver high-quality semiconductor products from prototype through to high-volume manufacturing.




You will play a key role in defining test strategies, managing production ramp activities, coordinating ASIC qualification programmes, and driving continuous manufacturing and test cost improvements.




Key responsibilities

Define and plan ASIC test activities across multiple project stages in collaboration with Project Managers and engineering teams
Establish and manage ASIC test solutions in partnership with external test houses
Coordinate prototype-to-production ramp-up activities, including characterization, qualification, and manufacturing readiness
Work closely with customers to define production strategies and ensure successful product introduction
Manage the transition from engineering samples and prototypes into high-volume manufacturing







Key requirements

Master's degree in Electronics, Microelectronics, Semiconductor Engineering, or a related discipline
5+ years of experience in ASIC Test Engineering, Semiconductor Manufacturing, or Product Engineering environments
Strong understanding of ASIC production test methodologies and semiconductor manufacturing processes
Experience working with external test houses and outsourced semiconductor assembly and test (OSAT) providers
Experience managing prototype, qualification, and volume production transitions
