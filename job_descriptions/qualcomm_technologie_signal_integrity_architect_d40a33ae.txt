General Summary:

Qualcomm Data Center team is developing High performance, Energy efficient server solution for data center applications. We are looking for highly talented, innovative, teamwork-oriented individuals for our cutting-edge technology work! 

Our Mission

We are dedicated to transforming the industry by reimagining silicon and developing next-generation computing platforms. By joining our team, you’ll collaborate with world-class engineers to create innovative solutions that push the limits of performance, energy efficiency, and scalability. Our focus is on developing reference platforms based on Qualcomm's Snapdragon SoC, delivering a comprehensive solution that includes hardware, software, reference designs, user guides, SDKs, and more. 

Position: Signal Integrity Architect

We are seeking an experienced Signal Integrity Architect (individual contributor) to develop high-performance signal integrity (SI) solutions tailored for advanced server applications. In this role, you will work closely with the platform team and collaborate with cross-functional teams, including software, firmware, packaging, product management, and component suppliers. You will contribute to the definition, analysis, and verification of SI for high-speed interconnects — with a primary focus on CXL and GPIO/LSIO interfaces — across PCB and system levels for data center servers. 

Key Responsibilities

Own SI analysis, simulation, and design closure for CXL 2.0/3.0 interfaces, including channel budget development, , margin analysis and lab methodology support. 
Collaborate with SoC, applications engineering, packaging, and system design teams to define and implement system interconnects that meet the unique demands of high-end server environments. 
​Contribute to SI guidelines development, provide feedback on PCB stack-ups, routing constraints, via structure design, and material selection. 
Develop   full-topology SI models, simulations, and final designs across assigned interface domains. 
Perform SI analysis on 2.5D/3D structures and contribute to the establishment of design guidelines for advanced packaging integration. 
Own SI analysis and design guidelines for GPIO/LSIO interfaces such as SPI, I3C, and proprietary paralle interface including signal integrity characterization, termination, and compliance. 
Support customer-facing SI collateral development — guidelines, application notes, and simulation models for CXL interfaces.  
Develop design automation tools and methodologies. Set technical direction for SI aspects of design within your area of ownership and drive innovating design methodologies 
Prepare and present technical documentation and reports to stakeholders, including engineering teams, senior management, customers, and suppliers. 

Qualifications

Master’s degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering, with a specialization in Signal Integrity (SI). 

8+ years of experience in SI analysis, simulation, and measurement of high-speed digital systems. 

Strong knowledge of SI fundamentals, including power distribution networks (PDN), EMIR, transmission line theory, crosstalk, S-parameters, and channel simulations. 

Expertise in CXL 2.0/3.0 specifications and/or PCIe Gen5+ SerDes standards, including channel simulation and compliance methodology. 

Experience with GPIO, LSIO, and low-speed interface SI characterization and design guidelines. 

Familiarity with SerDes standards (PCIe, CXL, USB, Ethernet, etc.). 

Experience owning end-to-end methodologies, encompassing packaging, PCBs, connectors and cabling 

Proficiency with 2D/2.5D/3D EM simulation tools such as ANSYS HFSS/HFSS3DL, Cadence Sigrity, Dassault Systèmes CST, Keysight ADS, and Synopsys' HSPICE. 

Hands-on experience creating complex SI models and performing both frequency and time-domain simulations. 

Proficiency in Unix/Linux environments and experience with EDA tools through scripting. 

Strong knowledge of packaging and PCB technologies, design, and manufacturing processes. 

Proven ability to work independently and collaboratively within a cross-functional team environment. 

Strong technical documentation skills and excellent written and verbal communication. 

Preferred Qualifications

Ph.D. in Electrical or Computer Engineering, specializing in SI. 

12+ years of experience in SI analysis, simulation, and measurement of high-speed digital systems. 

In-depth understanding of computational electromagnetics and transmission line theory.  

Hands-on experience with CXL 2.0/3.0 channel simulation, compliance testing, and debug on server-class platforms. 

Hands-on experience with electrical validation and compliance testing using tools like VNAs, oscilloscopes, spectrum analyzers, TDR, probing and fixture design to ≥60GHz  

Familiarity with 2.5D/3D packaging technologies, such as CoWoS, InFo, EMIB, and RDL. 

Proficiency in DDR5 and LPDDR5X timing budgets, jitter analysis, and PHY design/link analysis. 

Experience with SI simulation for PCIe Gen6+, multi-gigabit serial buses (112Gbps+), UCIE, etc. 

Experience with package and PCB design tools such as Cadence Allegro. 

Proficiency in scripting with MATLAB, Perl, and Python. 

Prior experience contributing to customer-facing SI reference documentation or application notes for server or networking platforms.
