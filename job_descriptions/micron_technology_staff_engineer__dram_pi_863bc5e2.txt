Our vision is to transform how the world uses information to enrich life for all.

Micron Technology is a world leader in innovating memory and storage solutions that accelerate the transformation of information into intelligence, inspiring the world to learn, communicate and advance faster than ever.

Work with process owners for DRAM integration. Establish structural requirements for DRAM devices in current and future products. Analyze the data related to DRAM integration such as SEM, TEM, inline film thickness, Inspections, and Electrical measurements. Work on resolutions for deviations. Assess DRAM CMOS structure and layout and define the requirements for DRAM CMOS. Lead and coordinate joint technical efforts with the process teams to resolve complex process-related issues that impact CMOS performance or yield. Debug process related issues in current/future technology nodes: Planar and wafer bonding and beyond. Continuously monitor and analyze inline fab process data to ensure the health of the line and to identify and troubleshoot issues related to yield, performance, and reliability. Lead structured DOE for yield, reliability and process window characterization May telecommute part-time.

Employer will accept a Master’s degree in Materials Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Physics, Mathematics or related field and 4 years of experience in the job offered or related occupation.

Position Requires


Semiconductor devices, processes and characterization techniques
High performance logic technologies (14nm and beyond)
Data analysis, device characterization and process integration
Performing electrical characterization of CMOS devices and CMOS circuits to identify device improvement directions
CMOS technology and its impact on overall product figures of merit
Design, TCAD and modelling groups
Python, JMP, and SPC
