THE ROLE:  

As a Server Customer Platform Application Engineer, you are responsible for customer server system development program technical support from concept to end of life. This includes concept design, schematic and layout review, signal integrity and power in-tegrity, electrical validation, system integration, manufacture, etc. You will provide technical support across platform design, platform debug and system validation to meet program goals of quality platform delivery on schedule at target performance.

  

THE PERSON:  

As a Server Customer Platform Application Engineer, you must have experience as a domain expert in at least one or more areas include system design, schematic, layout, SI/PI, EV, validation and have basic understanding of the general system design process (preferably Server), from concept to gerber-out and through production.

  

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:  

Work with MDC/OEM/ODM partners to enable successful deployment of AMD server platforms by augmenting customer solutions with technical collateral including whitepaper, application note, user guide on customization and tuning, reference design materials, and debugging/profiling tools
Perform design reviews include concept, block-level, schematic, layout for customer platorms and/or referene platforms
Support bring-up, validaiton and debug of platform related issues
Engage directly on key technical topics, issues, architecture discussions, deisgn optimizations with customers and internal teams and steer of multi-party disscussion to convergence
Analytic ability to address technical issues and generate creative solutions
On-site support to speed up issues closure to meet overall project schedule

  

PREFERRED EXPERIENCE:  

3+ years of server/client SOC or system development experience
Board Design Fundamentals pertaining to Mixed signal design, High Speed board design, and Power Design
Hands-on with debug, triage, and validation procedures for all the interfaces and subsystems of a platform
Experience with common lab equipment, including protocol/logic analyzers, oscilloscopes, etc.  
Knowledge of server system architecture, technical debug, and validation strategy
Fair knowledge of HW development life-cycle end to end from Design Conception, Execution, Fabrication & Assembly, board Bring-up, Test & Validation, Reporting and Customer support.

  

ACADEMIC CREDENTIALS:  

Bachelors and/or higher degree in EE/CS or related majors
