Engineering Director




Engineering Director required for an early-stage deep-tech company developing next-generation precision sensing technology based on advanced materials and semiconductor processes. This Engineering Director role will lead the transition from an R&D-focused environment into a scalable product development and manufacturing organisation. The Engineering Director will provide technical leadership across hardware, test, process, and product engineering activities while helping shape the future direction of the business.




Responsibilities

Lead multidisciplinary engineering teams across electronics, test and measurement, process development, and manufacturing
Drive the transition from prototype development to scalable product production
Define engineering strategy, development roadmaps, and product release plans
Establish DFM, DFT, quality, and manufacturing processes
Work closely with research teams to convert laboratory technology into commercial products
Build and develop engineering teams as the company grows
Provide technical leadership and executive-level input to company strategy




Requirements

Degree in Electronics Engineering, Physics, Semiconductor Engineering, or a related discipline
Experience taking complex hardware technologies from development through to production
Strong background in semiconductor, electronics, photonics, advanced materials, or high-technology hardware environments
Experience leading multidisciplinary engineering teams
Knowledge of manufacturing, industrialisation, DFM, DFT, and product qualification
Ability to operate both strategically and technically within a growing company




Opportunity pathway to VP / CTO at the next funding round (Series A)




Location: Lund, Sweden
