Your Role

Key responsibilities in your new role


Infineon developed strategy and an infrastructure supporting that significantly boosts design productivity
Create data structure to hold design data, fill them with formalizeddata (e.g.XML, HDL-Models), and generate code out of it
Write maintainable and scalable code by making efficient use of Datastructures, algorithms and design patterns in C#
Understand the EDA domain
Develop with design engineers methods, that could further automate their jobs and help them to implement the methods quickly
Generalize implementation and structure it following state-of-the-art software concepts
Define architecture, coding principle, QA strategy and documentation plans for the implementations mentioned abov


Your Profile

Qualifications And Skills To Help You Succeed


Approximately 10+ years of experience in Software industry
Strong experience in architecting and developing complex scalable software tools right from idea-to-production with a very good design architecture/pattern
Hands on expereince in Electronic Design Automation (EDA) area or software design and implementation or a related technical area
Very good programming skills with focus on Java/Eclipse/Python in UNIX/Windows environments
Very good problem-solving skills
Familiarity with agile software development techniques is a plus
Understanding of semiconductor development process from concept tohardware design is a plus
Familiarity with Eclipse plugin development is a plus
Good hold on data structures and algorithms
Solid Understandingof OOPS/UML
