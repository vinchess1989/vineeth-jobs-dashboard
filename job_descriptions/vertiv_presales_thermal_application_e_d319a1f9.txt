Job Description

Presales Thermal Application Engineer

Vertiv are recruiting for a Presales Thermal Application Engineer to join our UK & I team. The Presales Thermal Application Engineer will identify customer requirements & specifications. The Presales Thermal Application Engineer will review RFQ, evaluate the best trade off solution according to customer needs, budget, efficiency and project timeframes. The Presales Thermal Application Engineer will work closely with Engineering and SFAs linked to thermal products.

Who We Are

At Vertiv, we build products that keep critical technologies running 24/7. We are proud we were the first to protect mainframes with precision cooling systems. We were the first to introduce an integrated enclosure system to distributed networks. We help some of the largest names in the industry bring new capacity online faster and at a lower cost when search and social media increased demand for storage and computing. Our portfolio spans power, thermal and infrastructure management products, software and solutions, and is complemented by a network of nearly 250 service centers worldwide.

Responsibilities and Measurement Criteria with Time investment Needed on Each: -

(This will describe the overall core responsibilities of the role, decision making responsibilities etc.)


Development of technically and price competitive cooling solutions working closely with colleagues and/or external partners when needed.
Provide support on hydronics, especially on projects where there's a coexistence of high density products together with chillers and other CW units.
Provide Sales with pre-sales technical support when building proposals, quotes and tenders
Provide Sales with pre-sales technical support when attending technical meeting, aiming to become a reference point in EMEA, being able to explain the whole thermal chain, from servers till heat rejection/heat recovery.
Create and review application based documentation (drawings, specifications, electrical layout, presentations) enabling AE colleagues and Sales to secure the business
Share ideas and detailed design proposals to Product Management and Engineering teams for product improvement/development by applying a continuous improvement mindset.
Provide product and application based training to the EMEA AE community and Sales to strengthen the know-how across the Vertiv's group
Support WT activities and factory visits when needed.
Attend in person meeting with clients when necessary supporting colleagues and Sales.
Support that any activity, task and responsibility required by role in terms of the International Trade Compliance Regulation is managed and performed in full compliance with the Company ITC certification program itself


Qualifications

Required/ Minimum Qualifications:


School Background: engineering background or equivalent education with proven field experience
Professional experience within the Datacenter industry with focus on High Density applications
Excellent know-how of Data Center High Density trends, applications and related thermal
management complexities
Excellent know-how of Uptime Insititute Tier levels and related Thermal Management
infrastructure design
Language skills: fluent English written and verbal required
Excellent IT skills (Word/Excel/PowerPoint) and Customer/Sales orientation


Additional / Preferred Qualifications


High focus on customer needs
Open minded
Experience in quoting/proposing/ managing projects
Organized, self-motivated and proactive
Able to combine long term and short-term goals by setting priorities with high level directions
Organizational skills and familiar with a decentralized environment but at the same time able to work in a team
Ability to interact with all levels within the organization from entry level to executive
the Fly, Integrity and Trust, Working in Teams, Communicate Effectively, Work/Life Balance.
