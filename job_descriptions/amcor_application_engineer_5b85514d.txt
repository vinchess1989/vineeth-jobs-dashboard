Position Job Title: Application Engineer - Proteins

BG and BU: Amcor Flexible Europe, Middle East and Africa

Function: Research and Development

Location: Open in Europe

Line Manager’s Job Title: Product Development Manager

Contract Type: Permanent

Be part of something great

We are looking for an experienced Application Engineer to join our Flexible Packaging team in EMEA. In this role, you will work closely with customers and internal teams to turn packaging challenges into robust, value‑adding solutions. You will be a trusted technical partner, supporting existing products and enabling new developments that drive profitable growth.

What You Will Do

As an Application Engineer, you will sit at the intersection of customers, sales, and R&D, translating application needs into the right packaging solutions.

Your key responsibilities include:


Act as a technical expert for packaging applications at customers manufacturing in Meat and Cheese markets across EMEA
Support customers with product selection, application optimization, and effective product commercialization
Support product enhancements and new developments in collaboration with R&D and Product Management
Work closely with Sales and Field Technical Service to solve complex application issues
Lead and support product qualifications, trials, and scale‑ups at customer sites
Conduct packaging line, shelf‑life, and performance evaluations to demonstrate quantifiable customer value
Capture customer and market insights to identify portfolio gaps, innovation opportunities, and competitive trends
Share knowledge internally and contribute to building strong application capabilities across the organization


What Makes This Role Exciting


High level of customer interaction and real‑world problem solving
Strong influence on product success and customer satisfaction
Exposure to leading packaging technologies, market trends, and sustainability challenges
Daily collaboration with Sales, R&D, Operations, Marketing, and global teams


What You Bring


10+ years of experience in application engineering, technical sales, or similar roles within flexible packaging in Meat and Dairy.
Strong understanding of packaging materials and customer manufacturing processes
Proven ability to assess customer needs and translate them into practical, scalable solutions
Solid project management, problem‑solving, analytical, and communication skills
Comfortable working independently in a cross‑functional, international environment
Willingness to travel more than 50%


Market expertise (one or more of the following):


Hard Cheese: grated, sliced, and block cheese applications
Fresh Meat: shrink films and bags; forming films and bags


Who You Are


Customer‑focused and solution‑oriented
Curious, proactive, and hands‑on
Credible technical partner who builds trust quickly
Organized, pragmatic, and able to manage multiple priorities


Why join us

You will join a global packaging leader where technology, customer value, and collaboration matter. This is a role with real impact on customers, products, and the future of flexible packaging.

About Amcor

Amcor is the global leader in developing and producing responsible consumer packaging and dispensing solutions across a variety of materials for nutrition, health, beauty and wellness categories. Our global product innovation and sustainability expertise enables us to solve packaging challenges around the world every day, producing a range of flexible packaging, rigid packaging, cartons and closures that are more sustainable, functional and appealing for our customers and their consumers. We are guided by our purpose of elevating customers, shaping lives and protecting the future. Supported by a commitment to safety, over 75,000 people generate $23 billion in annualized sales from operations that span over 400 locations in more than 40 countries.
