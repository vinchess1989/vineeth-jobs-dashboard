Key Responsibilities

Act as primary technical interface for customers on post-silicon validation and debug
Enable and execute SoC/IP bring-up, validation, and feature enablement
Drive issue triage, root cause analysis, and closure across HW/FW/SW layers
Support customer setup for validation environments, tools, and automation
Translate customer requirements into actionable inputs for internal teams
Collaborate with design, firmware, and validation teams for issue resolution and delivery
Provide status updates, tracking, and escalation management

 

 

Required Qualifications

Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Electrical/Electronics/Computer Engineering or related field
8+ years of experience in post-silicon validation (SoC or IP level)
Strong experience in silicon bring-up, validation, and debug
Hands-on experience with lab equipment (oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, protocol analyzers, etc.)Experience with scripting/programming (Python, C, Tcl or similar)
Solid understanding of system architecture and hardware-software interaction
Experience working with emulation platforms or validation environments is a plus

Preferred Skills

Experience in customer-facing roles (FAE / onsite / customer support engineering)
Strong knowledge of high-speed interfaces (PCIe, Ethernet) and/or SoC subsystems
Familiarity with firmware, drivers, and system-level debug
Experience working across pre-silicon and post-silicon environments

 

Soft Skills

Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
Strong customer orientation and problem-solving mindset
Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
Proven ability to work independently and collaborate across global teams
