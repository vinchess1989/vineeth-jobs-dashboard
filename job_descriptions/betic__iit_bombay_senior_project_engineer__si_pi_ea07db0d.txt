Hiring: Senior Project Engineer (SI/PI & EMI/EMC)




BETIC, IIT Bombay | Mumbai, India




About BETIC

The Biomedical Engineering Technology Innovation Centre (BETIC) at IIT Bombay is a leading research facility focused on creating high-quality, affordable medical devices through innovation. By bringing together experts from various disciplines, BETIC excels in identifying medical needs, developing solutions, and delivering tested and qualified products to the market




Role Overview

Work with product development teams to understand requirements for Signal Integrity (SI), Power Integrity (PI), EMI/EMC, and motor simulation
Perform system-level and PCB-level simulations using industry tools
Work on multi-domain simulations including electronics, power systems, motors, thermal systems, and batteries
Use simulation tools such as Ansys HFSS and Ansys SIwave
Support simulation-driven design, validation, and performance optimization of medical electronic systems.




Key Responsibilities




Perform SI/PI & EMI/EMC simulations using HFSS, SIwave and Keysight ADS
Develop simulation models and perform pre-layout and post-layout analysis
Interpret simulation results and provide design insights and recommendations
Validate simulation results against standards, analytical models, and experimental data
Ensure convergence, accuracy, and reliability of simulation outcomes
Analyze Signal Integrity issues including parasitics, crosstalk, noise coupling, grounding, and shielding
Analyze Power Integrity issues including IR drop, decoupling, and power distribution network (PDN) design
Perform system-level simulations including radiated emissions, conducted emissions, ESD, and motor simulations (PMDC, BLDC)
Conduct multi-domain simulations including thermal and battery analysis
Participate in design reviews, technical discussions, prototype evaluations, and cross-functional engineering meetings
Prepare simulation reports, validation summaries, design recommendations, and technical documentation
Develop automation scripts using Python, MATLAB for simulation workflows and post-processing
Maintain simulation libraries, material databases, and reusable design templates
Support verification & validation (V&V) activities and assist in regulatory documentation (CDSCO, ISO 13485, CE)
Participate in internal workshops, technical forums, and knowledge-sharing activities

Required Qualifications

Education

 Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, or related discipline




Experience

Bachelors with 8 years or Masters with 6 years of experience in SI / PI / EMI / EMC simulation or related domains.
Experience in multi-board system simulation and analysis
Strong knowledge of Signal Integrity issues (parasitics, crosstalk, noise, grounding, shielding)
Strong knowledge of Power Integrity concepts (IR drop, decoupling, PDN design)
Understanding of EMI/EMC concepts (radiated, conducted emissions, ESD)
Experience in motor simulation (PMDC, BLDC)
Familiarity with lab instruments such as oscilloscopes, spectrum analyzers, EMI receivers, and LCR meters
Experience working in cross-functional engineering environments (electronics, embedded, mechanical, testing teams)




Why Join BETIC?

Work on life-saving indigenous medical technologies that directly impact patient care and healthcare outcomes
Gain hands-on experience in medical device development, working closely with real clinical needs and healthcare environments. Exposure to national programs and funding ecosystems such as BIRAC, ICMR, DBT, etc.
Contribute to the development of safe, reliable, and robust medical electronic systems that meet real-world healthcare challenges
Be part of India’s leading med-tech innovation ecosystem at IIT Bombay, working alongside researchers, clinicians, and industry partners




Type of Appointment: Contractual basis – The initial appointment will be for a period of 89 days after which you may be considered for a one-year appointment, based on your performance.




Gross Salary Range: 102552-123891* The selection committee may offer lower or higher designation and lower or higher salary depending upon the experience and performance of the candidate in the interview/test.
