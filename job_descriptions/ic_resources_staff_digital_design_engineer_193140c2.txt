If you enjoy technically challenging RTL and mixed-signal integration work, but also want to be part of a collaborative, engineering-led team where your experience genuinely carries weight. This opportunity is worth exploring.

A well-established semiconductor design team in Edinburgh is looking for a Staff Digital Design Engineer to work on advanced mixed-signal and power management products in cutting-edge process nodes.

This is a senior technical role offering high levels of ownership, complex design challenges, and the opportunity to influence both product development and the growth of the wider engineering team.

The work combines low-power digital design with complex mixed-signal integration challenges, working closely with Analog, Verification, and Physical Design teams throughout the full chip development lifecycle.

Why Engineers are interested


Strong engineering culture with experienced technical leadership
Opportunity to mentor and support junior engineers as the team grows
High level of technical ownership and visibility
Long-term product development work on complex semiconductor devices
Collaborative, low-bureaucracy environment
Hybrid working with flexibility around core office hours
Edinburgh-based design centre within one of the UK’s strongest semiconductor hubs


Key experience


10+ years’ experience in Digital IC/SoC Design
Strong Verilog/SystemVerilog RTL design expertise
Experience with ASIC or complex SoC development
Low-power digital design experience
RTL integration, simulation, and debug experience
Mixed-signal semiconductor experience beneficial


The role


Low-power RTL design and digital integration
Full chip development from architecture through to silicon
Power, area, and performance optimisation
Collaboration with Analog, Verification, and Physical Design teams
Technical leadership, reviews, and mentoring


The team works onsite together three days per week, helping maintain close collaboration across engineering while still offering flexibility around working hours and commuting times.

This role would suit a senior engineer looking for challenging silicon development work, strong technical peers, and the opportunity to make a visible impact within a respected semiconductor design team.

Worth exploring? Contact me directly Steph Hutchinson.
