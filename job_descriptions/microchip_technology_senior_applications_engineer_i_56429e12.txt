Job Description

In this position, you will be part of the Applications Engineering team, acting as a key interface between customers, product marketing, design, validation, and verification teams. You will contribute across the entire product lifecycle – from initial concept development to customer feedback.

You will work in a dynamic, international, and collaborative environment, playing a key role in the development of innovative and market-leading SPE (Single Pair Ethernet) products.

Key Responsibilities


Develop and evaluate new concepts for SPE products, including simulation, prototyping, and validation
Collaborate closely with design teams to transform concepts into robust and scalable solutions
Support validation and compliance processes to ensure products meet customer and industry requirements
Create, review, and maintain technical documentation and application notes
Provide technical support to customers and ensure alignment with design recommendations
Drive high product quality by contributing expertise in signal integrity and EMC throughout the design and validation phases
Perform analysis, testing, and debugging using internal and external lab equipment
Collaborate cross-functionally and communicate complex technical topics to stakeholders with varying levels of expertise


Requirements/Qualifications


Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field (e.g. Mechatronics, Physics) with 8+ years of relevant experience; Master’s degree preferred
Experience with Ethernet / SPE technologies (100BASE-T1 / 1000BASE-T1)
Experience with Ethernet compliance and certification processes
Strong background in analog and digital circuit design
Experience in system- and circuit-level simulation and analysis
Hands-on experience with modern measurement equipment (oscilloscope, spectrum/signal/logic analyzer, VNA)
Experience in signal integrity and EMC testing and debugging
Strong technical documentation skills (reading and authoring)
Programming / scripting skills (Python, C, C++, C#, Java, uPython)
Independent, structured, and solution-oriented working style
Fluent in English (business level)


Preferred Skills


Experience in the automotive industry
Experience in the semiconductor industry
Familiarity with AI-supported workflows
RF design and debugging experience
PCB design experience (Altium)
Firmware development experience
Soldering skills
German language skills
