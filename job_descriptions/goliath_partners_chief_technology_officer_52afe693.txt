A high-growth AI startup is hiring a Chief Technology Officer to lead engineering and help shape the future of AI-powered enterprise software. This is a rare opportunity to join a rapidly scaling business that already works with many of the world's leading investment firms and has achieved strong commercial traction in its first year.




What You'll Do

• Define the company's technical vision, architecture, and engineering strategy

• Build and scale the engineering organization while remaining hands-on with development

• Lead the design of AI-powered document intelligence, workflow automation, and enterprise data platforms

• Partner directly with enterprise customers to solve complex technical and product challenges

• Recruit, mentor, and develop a high-caliber engineering team




What You Bring

• Experience building products from 0→1 in a startup or high-growth environment

• Current or previous CTO, Principal Engineer, Staff Engineer, or senior engineering leader with deep technical expertise

• Hands-on experience building production systems with Python, TypeScript/React (Go is a plus)

• Proven experience leading technical architecture, engineering strategy, and team growth

• Entrepreneurial mindset with the ability to balance executive leadership and hands-on engineering




Why Join

• Executive-level opportunity with significant ownership and influence over the company's future

• Join a venture-backed business experiencing rapid revenue growth and strong enterprise adoption

• Build and scale the engineering organization from an early stage

• Work directly with sophisticated enterprise customers on mission-critical AI products

• Competitive executive compensation, meaningful equity, and exceptional long-term upside




If you're interested in building and scaling a category-defining AI company as both a technical leader and executive, I'd be happy to share more details.




Comp: Up to $230K base + meaningful equity!
