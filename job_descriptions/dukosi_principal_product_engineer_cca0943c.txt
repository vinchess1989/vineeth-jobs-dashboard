Dukosi is a leading-edge technology company developing revolutionary technologies that dramatically improve the performance, safety, and efficiency of battery systems, and enable a more sustainable battery value chain. Our unique cell monitoring platform based on chip-on- cell technology and proprietary contactless communication protocol is transforming battery systems for electric vehicles (EV), industrial transportation and stationary energy storage markets. Our environmental purpose is at the heart of our business and drives our search for new ideas and approaches to improve the technologies that can help transition to net-zero emissions and a sustainable clean energy economy.

We are seeking a dynamic and experienced Product Engineer to lead our NPI, yield and cost reduction activities in our semiconductor manufacturing operations. This role will be play a key role in new product introduction (NPI), product yield & cost optimization and ensure outgoing quality. The ideal candidate will have a broad understanding of semiconductor manufacturing processes, product engineering, ATE test and back-end logistics.

KEY DUTIES


Responsibility for all product engineering activities and will report to the Director of Production Engineering
Owner of statistical analysis of data to support NPI, Production and Customer Return activities
Ownership of end-to-end product yield across Dukosi product lines
Central role in the quick turnaround of customer return issues, working within cross functional teams, to contain and implement corrective actions, and ensure appropriate communications on status internally within Dukosi and to end customers
Develop Yield Management Systems and tools to deliver best in class functionality
Co-Work with Test Development Team to approve release to production of test programs, by determining production readiness, using GR&R methodology
Disposition products through an MRB process to ensure all products released to customers meets appropriate quality requirements
Co-work with reliability team to determine appropriate stresses applicable to Dukosi products and drive root cause on any failures generated during reliability stresses
Lead the analysis of characterization data to confirm ability to meet datasheet limits, determine appropriate spec limits to be used on ATE and root cause any results that do not match simulations
Operate test equipment, debug test programs on ATE, create and execute DoE’s.


Experience


Bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or a related field
10+ years of experience in high volume semiconductor manufacturing, operations & product engineering, with strong knowledge of manufacturing processes, test equipment, FA techniques and data analysis best practices
Demonstrated leadership, communication and influencing skills and can communicate with ease to customers, senior management and across internal groups
Knowledge and experience of continuous improvement and structured problem solving such as 8-D, fishbone, and other tools
Knowledge and experience of yield management tools and statistical analysis packages
Experience in creating and maintaining SOPs for manufacturing and production
Experience with Automotive standards, AEC-Q100 and zero-defect initiatives
Knowledge of supply chain digitalization, automation, and advanced manufacturing technologies.


Desired Skills


Demonstrated leadership skills with the ability to drive cross-functional collaboration
Confident communicator with excellent presentation skills
Strong skills with using statistical analysis packages, such as SAS JMP
Scripting skills using JSL, Python, PERL etc.
Strong diagnostic and analytical skills with excellent numerical ability
Ability to plan and manage own workload and that of others
Test and Reliability hardware design skills are desirable.
Previous experience of implementing custom wafer map handling with suppliers to accommodate defect reduction initiatives such as DPAT & GDBC would be advantageous
Previous experience working with SiP packages would be advantageous.
