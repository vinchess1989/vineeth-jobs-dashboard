Applied Materials develop complex multidisciplinary systems for metrology and quality control processes in the semiconductors industry.

Our systems contain high-end Physics , HW , Algo and SW all combine into unique system allowing our customers extend and improve their existing technologies.

System Qualification Engineer day-to-day work contain examine new/obsolete modules and system improvements, assess their integration complexity to existing / new system and how they might affect the system overall performance. Work involves close collaboration with various RnD disciplines and conducting complex activities on real tools or dedicated simulators until approval or rejection of the suggested design

Roles & Responsibilities


Close collaboration with R&D disciplines such as System Eng. , Physics , SW Eng., Mechanics etc.
Perform detailed risk assessment from module to system level and assess the impact on system performance and integrity
Define set of experiments to validate the design, while considering efficient risk reduction
Lead comprehensive testing on simulators & real tools and perform detailed analysis on collected data
Share conclusions on the module performance and existing gaps to address
Provide recommendations for performance specs and promote corrective actions for future designs
Approve final design and specs to be “field ready”
Develop validation tools and capabilities for modules and system performance evaluation
20 % travel for new designs implementation and for complex system issues troubleshooting
