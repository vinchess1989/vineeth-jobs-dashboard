Physical Design & Implementation Engineer | Cutting-Edge SoCs




We are looking for a talented individual to join our global implementation team in Cork, Ireland, and take full ownership of the physical implementation for products using bleeding-edge technology nodes. This is a chance to push the boundaries of Power, Performance, and Area (PPA) with innovative methodologies.




We are hiring from junior up to staff level.




Key Responsibilities:

End-to-End Implementation: Executing the complete physical implementation flow from floorplan to GDSII for highly complex, high-speed designs with extremely aggressive PPA targets.
PPA Innovation: Researching and developing innovative methodologies, flows, and scripts (primarily using Perl and Tcl) to push the limits of PPA in advanced process nodes.
Design and Optimisation Mastery: Leading complex analyses using statistics and data prediction to track benchmarks, optimise timing, congestion, and power, and identify critical areas for improvement.
Cross-Functional Collaboration: Working closely with cross-functional teams (e.g., RTL, CAD) to understand design context, align on PPA strategies, and ensure the delivered design meets the highest standards of innovation and compatibility.
Industry Leadership: Staying ahead of the curve by researching industry trends and innovations in physical design to ensure our solutions align with best practices.




Qualifications:

Hands-on, relevant experience in Physical Design.
Expert knowledge of the entire PD flow from netlist to GDSII (Floorplanning, Placement, CTS, Routing, and optimisation).
Strong experience with PD implementation tools in advanced technology nodes (10nm and below).
Excellent understanding of Static Timing Analysis (STA) and timing closure methodologies.
Good working knowledge of Power Analysis and Power Integrity (PI) analysis.
Solid understanding of Physical Verification (DRC/LVS) and closure techniques.
Expert-level automation skills in Perl and Tcl for developing and supporting PD flows.
Exceptional communication skills and a strong desire to thrive within a collaborative, global team.
