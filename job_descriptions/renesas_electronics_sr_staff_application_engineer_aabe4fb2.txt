Company Description

Renesas is a global semiconductor leader shaping the backbone of next‑generation automotive, industrial, cloud, and IoT innovation. With over $9.3 billion in annual revenue (2024) and a world‑class team of 22,700+ employees, Renesas operates at scale while retaining the agility to drive category‑defining breakthroughs. The company’s footprint spans more than 30 countries, enabling deep customer proximity and a uniquely global innovative engine. As Japan’s semiconductor powerhouse and a top global player in microcontrollers, analog, and power technologies, Renesas delivers real‑world impact across EVs, smart factories, renewable energy infrastructure, and edge intelligence. For ambitious problem‑solvers who want to work on meaningful, systems‑level challenges with huge industry leverage, Renesas offers the scale, stability, and strategic clarity of a market leader—paired with the transformative momentum of a company reinventing how the world computes, connects, and electrifies.

Renesas has a growing presence in India with HC approaching 1000+ and significant presence with active government and university collaboration as well as OSAT footprint (JV with CG India). Renesas is a leading electronics supplier globally, and this is a unique opportunity to directly influence the future products which will be offered to our customers in a new, fast growing and large Indian market with specific needs and applications.

Job Description

We are seeking a Sr. Staff Applications Engineer with deep expertise in power electronics and power management systems to support Renesas’ India‑focused growth initiatives. This role is hands‑on, customer‑facing, and technically authoritative.

The successful candidate will act as the technical backbone between customers, field teams, and internal design groups, owning application architecture, reference designs, competitive analysis, validation methodology, and critical customer debug. This role strongly influences product P&L or roadmap decisions through real system insight.

You will be expected to operate autonomously, mentor other applications engineers, and represent Renesas as a trusted technical authority at key customer engagements

Responsibilities


Customer‑Facing Applications Leadership
Serve as the primary technical interface for key customers in power-related applications (industrial, automotive light EV, inverter/UPS, consumer power, energy storage).
Lead design‑in, bring‑up, and debug activities at customer sites or Renesas labs.
Diagnose complex system‑level issues across power stage, control loop, sensing, firmware interaction, EMI/thermal behaviour.
Provide structured technical feedback to product and design teams based on real customer usage.
Reference Designs & Application Enablement
Architect and develop reference designs, evaluation platforms, and application demonstrators using Renesas power products (e.g., MOSFETs, DC/DC, PMICs, gate drivers, BMS ICs).
Own schematic review, component selection, PCB guidance, and layout best‑practice recommendations.
Generate application notes, design guides, and technical collateral that materially improve customer adoption.
Define validation plans that reflect real-world corner cases, not just datasheet conditions.
System‑Level Technical Expertise
Understand and analyse full power system behaviour, including:
Control loop stability and transient response
Thermal and efficiency trade‑offs
Protection mechanisms and fault behaviour
Interaction with MCUs/SoCs and firmware
Perform and guide system modelling and simulation (SIMPLIS, PLECS, SPICE).
Evaluate design trade‑offs and clearly articulate why one solution works better than another keeping BOM cost in mind
Lab Ownership & Technical Depth
Hands‑on operation of lab equipment: oscilloscopes, Bode/network analysers, electronic loads, power supplies, thermal setups.
Develop automation scripts (Python/C) to accelerate validation and characterization.
Support failure analysis and root cause investigation for field issues and RMA trends.
Technical Leadership & Mentorship
Act as a technical mentor for junior and mid‑level applications engineers.
Help define best practices, review standards, and debug methodologies across the power applications team.
Influence hiring by helping assess deep technical capability (not surface‑level


Qualifications

Required skills & Qualifications:


Comfort and ability to collaborate and be effective in an entrepreneurial (fast-paced and ambiguous) environment
Excellent interpersonal, communication and presentation skills and comfort in presenting to a wide range of audiences. Capable to communicate issues in a concise and effective way.
Desire and drive to interact with, as well as ability to build and maintain excellent relationships with internal and external stakeholders.
Product ownership mentality
12+ years of experience in power management space, ideally including customer facing technical problem solving with semiconductor companies
Travel will be required
Bachelor or Masters of Electronics Engineering related discipline
Ability to build a strong team and motivate individuals to work out of their comfort zone
Self-driven, strongly motivated individual capable to take full ownership of their activities, and to involve other functions in the company or management when needed to bring them to a successful outcome.
Understanding of power management applications and products.


Technical Proficiency


Deep understanding of Power topologies (e.g., buck, boost, buck boost, isolated where relevant), Control loop theory and compensation, Thermal design and power loss modelling (using FEA tools), and EMI/EMC considerations at board and system level
Hands-on experience and ability in lab debugging, probing and characterizing circuit and silicon using a mixture of custom-made and standard equipment such as power supplies, e-load, digital oscilloscopes, spectrum analyzers, etc.
SIMPLIS or similar ckt simulator
Programming proficiency Python or C for automation and data analysis
Expertise in board level analog and digital circuit design expertise and PCB schematic design using PCB CAD tools
Proficiency with a full complement of lab equipment including network analysers, oscilloscopes and waveform generators. Experience with automation of lab testing.
Data analysis using Excel or JMP
Understanding of AECQ / IATF /ISO26262 (FuSa) (At least basic level)
