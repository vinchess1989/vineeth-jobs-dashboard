Job Description

Design Engineer for MCU design group in Bangalore. This candidate will work on ICs for future generations of

Microcontrollers. Job Responsibilities Are


participate in a global team setting on the development of advanced microcontroller products.
Support design and verification of Microcontroller products.
Perform RTL design and verification of complex digital macros, working from an architectural description.
ASIC Design flow through backend verification and stream out.
Debug of complex silicon issues down to transistor level.
Technical Mentoring and supervision of Junior people.
Project Management.


Requirements/Qualifications


The candidate must have B.E. (Electronics) degree. M.E. / M. Tech (Electronics/Microelectronics) preferred
Candidate must have 5 to 10 years of experience working in CMOS VLSI development capacity, preferably on products that incorporate microcontrollers for embedded controlapplications.
Strong digital design skills and experience in verilog RTL coding for synthesis is essential.
Experience with Synopsys tools (Design Compiler, IC Compiler, Power Compiler, Primetime) and formal verification tools is highly desirable.
The candidate will be working as a part of global VLSI design team. Strong communication skill & the ability to interface effectively with US team members is essential
