Purpose of role

 

Working closely with the Project Manager and the Bid Lead, responsible for the setting of the project technical delivery philosophy to enable efficient and successful project execution of agreed technical requirements while supporting the project schedule and budget requirements.

Ensuring a strong technical systems engineering approach delivering against the engineering project delivery process.

Ensures all technical teams on the project deliver the agreed technical requirements, the agreed technical timeline, with the required quality, and through the appropriate communication channels, thereby safeguarding the technical lifecycle from risks and issues yielding first time silicon.

Supports and where appropriate initiates technical discussion and reviews with the customer/internal teams on project requirements, execution and milestone signoff.

 

Responsibilities

 

Responsible for ensuring a strong technical systems engineering approach to project delivery

Ensures all teams deliver the agreed technical requirements, the agreed technical timeline, with the required quality, and through the appropriate communication channels, thereby safeguarding the technical lifecycle from risks and issues

Ensuring project goals and schedules are cascaded and clearly understood by the engineering team

Being, and ensuring project technical team, are risk and change alert and escalating new technical risks and opportunities for CCNs

Ensuring the inter-functional team interfaces are managed to ensure alignment on expected deliverables, quality and schedule

Driving managers and engineers to execute tasks in line with required quality and agreed schedules for the assigned discipline

Day to day management, task assignment and prioritisation for project engineers in the assigned discipline

Displaying a customer focused approach by demonstrating clear and competent communication, issue resolution & delivery to expectation and sharing this approach with the team

Taking ownership and responsibility of your tasks, and wider participation in Aion's strategic goals

Work with Project Manager to identify resource requirements (engineers and infrastructure), risks, issues, and solutions and to recommend priorities to deliver to schedule

Makes and enforces technical decisions in the assigned discipline for the project. E.g., flow, tools, guidelines, procedures etc.

Lead by example to encourage best practice to and to create a culture of knowledge sharing within the team

Assist with creating/updating project plans where necessary

Track and report on progress versus plan for the assigned discipline

 

Skills & Experience

 

Essential

 

Demonstrable experience of technically leading SoC and ASIC projects across multiple disciplines (e.g. Architecture, Front-end Design, Physical Design, Verification & DfT)

Experience of clearly reporting on issues, risks and KPIs

Ability to confidently challenge thinking to ensure clear procedures and correct decisions are taken

Proven capability as a problem solver with an ability to coach others

Good leadership skills; coaching, empowering, delegating and developing teams of people to deliver on projects

Proven technical people management experience in a similar role

Detailed understanding and proven experience of sharing best practices and the ability to train

Awareness of working with external customers., including development of technical sales documents such as a Statements of Work and interacting with customers at all stages of sales process

 

Attributes

 

Able to build trust through open and transparent communication

Reliable and dedicated to deliver on promises and commitments

Able to be Risk and Change alert

Ability to work under pressure with solid organisational and creative problem-solving skills

Positive mindset, demonstrates Aion Silicon values and embraces and promotes Aion’s culture 

Self-organisation and ability to respond to changing priorities quickly with excellent time management skills

Team player with the ability to guide and mentor more junior team members

Self-motivated and able to work under own initiative with excellent attention to detail

Passionate and committed to delivering a high standard of work

Resilient and always finds a way to succeed
