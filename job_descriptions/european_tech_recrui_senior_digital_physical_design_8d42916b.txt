Senior Digital Physical Design Engineer — Place & Route / Timing Closure / Remote




We are partnered with a global leader in the semiconductor industry. The team is looking to hire a Senior Digital Physical Design Engineer to own end-to-end physical design and drive sign-off closure on complex SoC designs.




This is an initial 10-month contract opportunity, available fully remote within Europe.




Key responsibilities for this Senior Digital Physical Design Engineer position:

Execute the full P&R flow — floorplanning, power planning, placement, CTS, and routing.
Drive timing closure across all corners using advanced STA techniques.
Debug physical verification flows including DRC, LVS, and ERC using Calibre.
Analyse and resolve timing, SI, IR drop, and EM issues.
Collaborate with RTL, DFT, PD, and verification teams to achieve design closure.




Key requirements:

10+ years in digital physical design.
Strong expertise in Cadence (Innovus, Tempus) and Synopsys (ICC2, PrimeTime).
Proficient with Calibre for physical verification and sign-off.
Deep knowledge of STA, timing constraints, and closure methodologies.
Experience with advanced nodes and low-power design techniques.
TCL or Python scripting for flow automation advantageous.




Keywords: Senior Digital Physical Design Engineer / Physical Design / Place & Route / P&R / Floorplanning / CTS / Timing Closure / STA / Cadence Innovus / Tempus / Synopsys ICC2 / PrimeTime / Calibre / DRC / LVS / Low Power / SoC / Tape-out / TCL / Python / Remote / Europe / Contract
