CTO, Advanced Packaging & Thermal Integration

Location: San Jose, CA

Company: Destination 2D Inc.




Destination 2D is pioneering CMOS-compatible graphene technologies to redefine semiconductor performance—from advanced 2.5D/3D packaging and thermal management to beyond-copper interconnects and heterogeneous integration.

We are seeking a senior packaging leader to translate these materials innovations into scalable, manufacturable solutions for next-generation AI and high-performance computing systems.




What You’ll Do

Architect and evaluate advanced packaging solutions leveraging materials with superior thermal, electrical, and mechanical performance.
Develop integration pathways for graphene and other 2D materials in next-generation packaging platforms.
Analyze and optimize thermal transport, power delivery, and package reliability.
Lead cross-functional efforts to transition materials from prototype to high-volume manufacturing.
Contribute to patents, publications, and long-term technology roadmaps.




Who You Are

PhD in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Materials Science, or related field.
15+ years of experience in advanced semiconductor packaging or heterogeneous integration.
Experience with emerging materials for thermal management or packaging innovation.
Proven track record of technical leadership, publications, or patents.
Deep understanding of heat transfer, power delivery, reliability, and system-level integration.
Experience working with leading foundries or OSATs; demonstrated success scaling technologies to production.




Join us in redefining semiconductor packaging using atomically engineered materials for the AI and HPC era.
