Our vision is to transform how the world uses information to enrich life for all.

Micron Technology is a world leader in innovating memory and storage solutions that accelerate the transformation of information into intelligence, inspiring the world to learn, communicate and advance faster than ever.

Seeking an experienced Hardware Engineer to architect and deliver next-generation server, mobile, and HPC platforms across the full product lifecycle. The role leverages AI-Enabled engineering for design space exploration, workload analysis, predictive validation, automated debug, and system performance optimization, enabling faster development cycles and higher-quality silicon/platform solutions.

Key Responsibilities

End-to-End Ownership


Own complete hardware lifecycle: architecture → schematic → PCB layout → bring-up → validation → production
Drive system-level design decisions for complex hardware platforms
Leverage AI-Enabled or Agentic AI to design next generation solutions and drive efficiency.


System Architecture & Design


Architect multi-layer, high-speed PCBs, including partitioning, power domains, and signal topology
Design and review systems involving FPGAs, SoCs, memory, and power management circuits
Ensure scalability, performance, and reliability through rigorous design reviews


Memory & High-Speed Interfaces


Design and validate DDR5 / LPDDR memory subsystems (timing, topology, SI/PI compliance)
Integrate high-speed interfaces such as PCIe Gen4/5, USB, SATA, I²C, SPI, and UART


Bring-Up, Validation & Debug


Lead board bring-up from initial power-on to system-level qualification
Define validation strategies, test plans, and debug methodologies
Drive root-cause analysis across hardware, firmware, silicon, and PCB issues
Perform advanced debug using oscilloscopes, logic/protocol analyzers, JTAG, and power tools


Cross-Functional Collaboration


Partner with SI/PI teams on signal integrity, stack-up, and PDN design
Collaborate with mechanical teams on thermal and form factor requirements
Work with manufacturing and supply chain on DFM/DFT, BOM optimization, and production readiness


Technical Leadership


Lead design reviews, milestone checkpoints, and architecture discussions
Mentor engineers and drive best practices in hardware design and validation


Required Expertise


12+ years in hardware design for server, storage, networking, embedded, or HPC systems in an IC role
Strong background in digital and mixed-signal design
Expertise in FPGAs, SoCs/microcontrollers, and power management (LDOs, buck/boost regulators)
Deep understanding of PCB design, PDN, controlled impedance, and manufacturing processes
Experience in hardware validation, bring-up, and system debugging
Familiarity with signal integrity considerations for high-speed systems


Qualifications


Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering or related field


Proven track record of delivering production-quality hardware platforms
