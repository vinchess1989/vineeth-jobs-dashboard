Job Description



As a Digital Design Engineer, you will be responsible for the digital design and RTL coding using Verilog or System Verilog. Integrating modules at SoC and work hands-on in the development cycle, especially in frontend domain until tapeout, this includes RTL coding, CDC, Lint, synthesis, STA, DFT. Debug and fix functional issues in RTL and develop scripts to automate frequently used processes. You will be expected to validate all the digital design functionality on silicon and debug and find innovative solutions.





Qualifications


MS or above in Electronics or Electrical Engineering with focus on digital IC design.
Experience with a full design cycle from architecture to place and route.
Hands on experience with HDL. Verilog, system verilog or VHDL.
Excellent logical reasoning and a quick learner with good communication skills.
Good in scripting using TCL, Perl. Comfortable with Unix, Linux environment.
Exposure to the industry standard tools in VLSI digital design will be preferred.
