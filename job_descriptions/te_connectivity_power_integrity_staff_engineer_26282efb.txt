Job Overview

Data communications applications change at an exponential pace and the Digital Data Networks Business Unit is at the heart of a continuously expanding connected life. Accordingly, we provide responsive, iterative, and fundamentally agile capabilities to support today's demands. Our customers are building the devices and the infrastructure that is redefining what information technology means. Our products push markets and meet the ever-changing needs of the evolving, more connected, data-driven world. We are enhancing the way we do business, focusing on the most important things and staying agile and responsive to the needs of a faster, more connected world.

Are you a self-starter, an influencer and have a passion for evolving high-speed data communication technology? At TE Connectivity, you will find a culture that rewards such hands-on engineering spirit. We empower our engineers to work collaboratively supporting our customer’s next generation of high-speed data communication products. This is a great opportunity to join our team that is seeking a like-minded person to develop relationships with key technology individuals at customers to understand technology pain points and direction.

Job Responsibilities

As a Power Integrity Engineer within our Digital Data Networks Business Unit, you will play a key role in simulating, developing, implementing, and verifying the power integrity of our innovative power solution portfolio. Collaborating with a multidisciplinary team of engineers, you will contribute to the design, validation, scaling, deployment, and support of cutting-edge data center power solutions.

We are seeking individuals who excel at turning ambiguous requirements into precise, highly constrained solutions. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in Signal Integrity (SI) and Power Integrity (PI) design, exceptional simulation and lab measurement skills, and proven experience in designing and manufacturing power products.

As the Power Integrity Engineer, a typical day may include:


Design, validate, and characterize power conversion and delivery systems for large compute infrastructure
Develop power distribution products, such as cables, connectors, and bus bars, for 800+V, 20+kA server racks and data center applications
Create simulation models, plan and execute simulation suites
Correlate simulation results with measured lab data to validate models.
Work with vendors to specify, procure, and validate new designs
Measure and characterize AC response, parasitics, and impedance of the power products.
Identify and resolve electrical issues by performing hands-on testing, debugging, and root cause analysis.
Prepare detailed documentation and reports to support product development and validation
Drive high-value development projects that will require you to lead and interface with large groups
Manage technical collaboration with customers and align internal development efforts to meet technical and functional requirements.


Qualifications


Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Physics, or equivalent experience.
Generally requires 10 years of electrical engineering work experience and 5+ years experience in power conversation and distribution
Experience using electrical design tools and software packages such as Spice, Allegro, PowerDC, Ansys Q3D
Knowledge of voltage converter architectures
Familiarity with High Voltage, 3-phase electrical systems is a plus
Comfortable using different instruments such as network analyzer, oscilloscope, signal generator, scope and other related test equipment to measure the eye diagram
The geographically dispersed group requires that the candidate be self-motivating with the ability to take the initiative to accomplish tasks with limited direction
Ability to communicate technical concepts to diverse audiences
Effectively able to build relationships with external customers and industry leaders as well as internally across all levels, disciplines, and geographies
Ability to spend 25% of your time traveling
