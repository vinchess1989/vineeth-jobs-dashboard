Our Mission

At Vertical Aerospace, we are pioneering the way for electric aviation. The Valo, our eVTOL (electric, vertical, take-off and landing), 'zero emissions' aircraft will set a new safety standard for how we will navigate the sky.

We won't realise our mission following the same legacy processes and traditions our predecessors followed, instead, we want to 'redefine' aerospace best practices. We are growing quickly from a prototype business to a scaling SME, and the next few years will be critical to our success and delivering on our ambitious goals. Valo is targeting airliner-level safety certification in 2029 ahead of entering service with our airline and operator customers.

We’re now looking for an Advanced Thermal Engineer to play a key role in shaping the thermal performance of our aircraft. This role sits at the heart of our battery and powertrain development, combining high-fidelity modelling with system-level thinking to ensure safe, efficient and certifiable performance.

What you’ll do


Lead battery thermal CFD analysis, supporting design verification across key programme milestones
Develop and apply high-fidelity and system-level thermal models across battery and wider vehicle systems
Contribute to powertrain and avionics thermal analysis, supporting overall vehicle performance
Support design decisions through simulation, trade studies and engineering insight
Work cross-functionally with design, test and systems teams to align modelling with real-world outcomes
Correlate simulation with test data to validate models and improve confidence in results
Define appropriate modelling approaches to answer complex engineering questions
Communicate findings clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
Contribute to the development of tools, methods and best practices in thermal modelling
Produce high-quality technical documentation to support design and certification
Engage with external partners including universities and research organisations
Provide technical oversight and guidance to junior engineers, reviewing their work in detail and supporting the development of robust, high-quality analysis across the team


What you'll bring


Degree in Mechanical, Aerospace or a related engineering discipline
Strong experience in thermal CFD analysis (e.g. Ansys Fluent, Star-CCM+)
Experience in battery thermal modelling strongly preferred but Modelling experience in alternative Powertrain systems will be considered
Knowledge of heat transfer, fluid dynamics and thermal management systems
Experience using MATLAB / Simulink / Simscape
Proven ability to correlate simulation results with test data
Understanding of how system-level requirements (performance, safety, durability) influence modelling
Strong analytical mindset with the ability to work from first principles
Ability to work autonomously and deliver in a fast-paced environment
Clear and confident communication skills


What can you expect from us?

We're on a mission. Where others see limits, we see opportunity, and we work at pace. Working at Vertical isn't your average role but for those seeking a challenge, a flexible, supportive organisation and an incredible team; working here is an opportunity to do the best work of your career.

Our approach promotes ingenuity and courage, while our environment builds success through diligence in safety and being open in the way we work. The only way we're going to assure the next chapter of aviation history is by working as a team, relentlessly, towards our goal.
