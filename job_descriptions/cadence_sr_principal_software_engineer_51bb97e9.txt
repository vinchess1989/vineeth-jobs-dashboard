Tallenna
At Cadence, we hire and develop leaders and innovators who want to make an impact on the world of technology.

Cadence Design Systems is a world leader in providing computational software for all aspects of intelligent system design.

The Circuit Simulation Engineer is responsible for designing, implementing and maintaining software

designed to perform analog and RF circuit simulation.

Candidate should have proven track-record on computer aided methods for numerical problem solving.

Requirements


MSc/PhD in electrical engineering, applied mathematics/physics, computer science, or similar
5+ years of work experience on numerical analysis on some related field (circuit, EM, thermal, fluid, photonics)
Experience on algorithms related to e.g., sparse matrix solvers, iteration, optimization etc.
Proficiency in C/C++ programming on Win and/or Linux environment
Team player with good communication skills


Experience on parallel computing and/or GPU utilization as well as knowledge on circuit simulation (e.g., Spectre, HSpice, APLAC) is a strong plus.

The engineer should have ability to work with an engineering and cross-functional team to deliver innovative technologies in a production environment.
