Hiring: Power Electronics Engineer

📍 Location: Chennai

🕒 Experience: 5–15 Years

We are looking for a Power Electronics Engineer with strong experience in server board power design, including power supply and motherboard power architecture.

🔧 Key Responsibilities:

Design and develop power solutions for server motherboards and power supply units (PSUs)
Work on DC-DC converters, VRM, POL converters, and power distribution networks
Perform schematic design, circuit simulation, and validation for power electronics
Conduct power integrity (PI) analysis, thermal considerations, and efficiency optimization
Collaborate with cross-functional teams for board bring-up, debugging, and testing
Ensure compliance with industry standards and reliability requirements

✅ Required Skills:

Strong experience in Power Electronics design for server boards / motherboard power systems
Expertise in SMPS, DC-DC converters, VRM design
Hands-on with schematic tools and simulation tools (e.g., Cadence, LTspice, etc.)
Knowledge of power integrity, signal integrity basics, and PCB layout considerations
Experience in testing, debugging, and validation of power circuits

🎯 Preferred Qualifications:

Experience in high-current, low-voltage power design (server/compute platforms)
Familiarity with Intel/AMD power specifications
Understanding of thermal management and reliability testing

📩 Apply Now:

If you’re passionate about power electronics and server hardware design, we’d love to connect!Please drop your CV to mareeswari.cm@hcltech.com
