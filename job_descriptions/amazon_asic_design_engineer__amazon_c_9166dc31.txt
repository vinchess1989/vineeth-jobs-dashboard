Description

Join the team which delivers highly differentiated silicon into Blink and Ring battery powered devices. Our team works on state-of-the art SoCs in a vertically integrated team environment to deliver products our customers love. Innovators will be delighted with our integrated verification/validation environment that is used to perform architectural modeling to post-silicon validation. The team works backwards from customer requirements to build super-low power, energy efficient designs that include the latest in AI, video processing, low power communications and CMOS fabrication technology.

Key job responsibilities


Write micro-architecture specs and solicit feedback/link to KPIs from architecture team
Execute on design specifications to deliver high quality RTL
Evaluate 3rd party IP blocks
Estimate power, performance, and area for significant IPs early in design cycle
Ensure quality by running and tracking results of front-end tools including: Synthesis, Lint (RTL, DFT, UPF), Power Analysis and STA
Work with pre-silicon verification teams to assist in defining test-plans/test-benches
Work with post-silicon validation teams to define and execute on test-plans


Basic Qualifications


Experience identifying bugs in architecture, algorithms, functionality, and performance with strong overall debugging skills
Experience with front-end design tools (Lint, CDC, RDC and lint)
Ability to write detailed micro-architectural specs which work through the design problems before coding (System Verilog, Verilog).


Preferred Qualifications


Bachelor's degree in Electrical or Communications Engineering or a related field
Experience with industry standard tools and scripting languages (Python or Perl) for automation
Experience with hardware-software interfaces
Using formal verification tools to enhance RTL quality before testing.
