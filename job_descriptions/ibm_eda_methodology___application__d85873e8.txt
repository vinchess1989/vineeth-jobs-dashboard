Introduction

At IBM Infrastructure & Technology, we design and operate the systems that keep the world running. From high-resiliency mainframes and hybrid cloud platforms to networking, automation, and site reliability. Our teams ensure the performance, security, and scalability that clients and industries depend on every day. Working in Infrastructure & Technology means tackling complex challenges with curiosity and collaboration. You’ll work with diverse technologies and colleagues worldwide to deliver resilient, future-ready solutions that power innovation. With continuous learning, career growth, and a supportive culture, IBM provides the opportunities to build expertise and shape the infrastructure that drives progress.

Your Role And Responsibilities

We are looking for a passionate and experienced EDA Methodology Engineer to join our Backend EDA team, focusing on Physical design flow development using Synopsys DC/DCNXT. In this role, you’ll work closely with design teams and EDA vendors to develop and implement robust PD flows used by IBM ASIC design teams.

Key Responsibilities


Develop and maintain Synthesis & physical design flows using Synopsys DC/DCNXT.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to align synthesis & physical design methodology with design goals.
Drive methodology improvements.
Coordinate with internal stakeholders and external vendors to deliver high-quality solutions.


Preferred Education

Master's Degree

Required Technical And Professional Expertise


Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Electronics Engineering, or VLSI Design.
5–8 years of hands-on experience with Synopsys DC/DCNXT.
Expertise in Tcl and Python scripting.
Deep knowledge of Physical design flow starting from Synthesis to GDS.
Proven ability to troubleshoot and resolve complex physical design issues.
Strong communication and collaboration skills.


Preferred Technical And Professional Experience


Experience with Synopsys DC/DCNXT, Synopsys Fusion Compiler, Cadence Innovus, Synopsys ICC2
