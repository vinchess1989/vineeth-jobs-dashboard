Asia Account Manager (Korea based)
Company
TSMC Korea Limited
Location
Korea
Career Area
Business Development
Job Type
Manager / Executive
Employment Type
Regular
Posted
Mar 18, 2026
Job Responsibilities
1. Account management of Asia customers with focus in Korea.
2. New business development of Asia customers in various applications segments.
3. Coordinate and lead the sales support team to achieve business targets and metrics.
4. Conduct business trips as required.
Job Qualifications
1. Bachelor’s degree or higher in Electrical Engineering (EE), Computer Science, Communication Engineering, Control Engineering, Physics, or related fields.
2. At least 3 years of experience in direct sales or business development, with a minimum of 8 years of work experience in large semiconductor companies.
3. Comprehensive IC product and market knowledge with strong analysis capability. IC design or application engineering experience is preferable.
4. Fluently in Korean and English is required, and proficiency in Chinese is a plus.
5. Based in Korea local office.
