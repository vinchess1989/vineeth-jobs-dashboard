Responsibilities:

Define and realize digital functions on IPs, subsystem or IC level based on required specifications
Perform design, verification and evaluation of digital functions
Develop RTL using Verilog/SystemVerilog
Support verification activities, including developing simple testbenches and debugging issues
Participate in ASIC front-end design flow activities
Analyze and debug design issues during development
Collaborate with cross-functional teams
Document design and related work

Requirements:

BSEE or equivalent is required; MSEE is a plus
Strong Verilog/SystemVerilog coding skills
Knowledge of ASIC front-end design flows
Experience in developing simple SystemVerilog/Verilog testbenches
Around +2 years of working experience

Strongly desired:

Knowledge of UVM is desirable
Knowledge of C/C++, Matlab, Unix/Perl scripting, or Python
Knowledge of digital signal processing and telecom is a plus
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as hands-on lab debugging experience
Self-motivated, with excellent communication skills and ability to excel in a team environment
Ability to work with teams at remote locations across different time zones
