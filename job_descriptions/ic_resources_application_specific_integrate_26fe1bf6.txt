Rachel Mason
I recruit ASIC Design Engineers, Design Verification Engineers and Formal Verification Engineers across the UK.

Join a company who are building a next-generation design house focused on AI, advanced compute, and emerging semiconductor technologies.




This is a rare opportunity to join at an early stage and define how digital design is done, technically and culturally. You’ll support and lead the development of AI and compute-focused ICs, working from architecture through RTL to silicon and have the opportunity to support and mentor the 6 graduates within the group.




This role is well suited to someone motivated by the opportunity to give back to the industry sharing their experience, mentoring the next generation, and helping shape the growth of an emerging company. It will appeal most to individuals driven by impact, ownership, and legacy, rather than those primarily focused on compensation.




Why This role is different

Foundational impact – shape the technical direction and standards from day one
Real ownership – no layers of management, no waiting for instructions
End-to-end exposure – architecture → RTL → tapeout → silicon validation
Variety of work – customer projects + in-house IP development
Startup agility – fast decisions, minimal bureaucracy, high trust




What you’ll be doing

Define digital architectures for AI accelerators, data paths, and SoC subsystems
Develop high-quality, synthesizable RTL (Verilog/SystemVerilog).
Run synthesis, STA, and DFT using industry-standard digital design tools
Lead design reviews and help establish best practices and design flows
Support tapeout and bring-up, including hands-on silicon validation
Mentor junior engineers and contribute to building team capability
Contribute to internal IP development alongside customer delivery




What they’re looking for

Strong background in digital IC design (senior-level experience)
Experience with a tape out.
Solid understanding of SoC design and PPA trade-offs
Confident working across the full design lifecycle
Comfortable taking ownership and making technical decisions
Strong communication skills and team mindset
DSP, CPU/GPU/NPU is advantageous but not essential.




What you’ll get

The chance to build something from the ground up
Work on cutting-edge AI and compute silicon
High autonomy and real technical ownership
A collaborative, engineering-led culture
Opportunity to shape both technology and team

T

here is a hybrid working model, but the company can be creative with onsite work.

Eg 2 days in the office every other week. On offer is a base salary up to £80,000 + shares + bonus + an excellent pension scheme.




You must have UK working rights.

Please contact Rachel Mason for more information.
