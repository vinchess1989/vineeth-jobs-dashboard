About this Opportunity

Aspire For Her is leading this search on behalf of a client — a global investment management firm that is scaling its technology capability in India. To protect confidentiality, the client's name is not disclosed at this stage; it will be shared with shortlisted candidates during the interview process.




Role Overview

We are seeking a Technology Transformation Lead, India – Global Technology to bring operational discipline, enterprise Agile maturity, global program management, technology-finance alignment, and transformation execution to a fast-growing India technology organization. This is a senior individual contributor role that operates with significant influence across a complex global matrix, partnering closely with the Head of India Technology and the Global Head of Technology Transformation. It carries a clear build vs. run mandate — sustaining stable, well-controlled operations while helping scale India from a delivery centre into a strategic global technology capability hub.




Key Responsibilities

• Own global program management for India-linked transformation initiatives — scope, milestones, dependencies, risks, financials, RAID logs, executive reporting, and benefits realization.

• Drive adoption and maturity of Enterprise Agile (release/PI-style planning, backlog governance, value-stream alignment) and delivery health metrics such as velocity, cycle time, and release predictability.

• Align technology execution with portfolio priorities and technology financial management — budgeting, forecasting, cost transparency, chargeback/allocation models, and value realization.

• Support scalable India operating models (product-aligned squads, PODs, platform teams) with demand intake, capacity, and workforce planning.

• Operate as a senior IC integrator across India and global technology, finance, delivery, and governance forums — influencing without direct authority.

• Advance India GCC maturity, including AI-enabled engineering, intelligent automation, AIOps, and continuous improvement.

• Ensure governance, risk, security, compliance, and operational resilience across initiatives; drive strategic vendor and managed-services management.




Candidate Profile

• 15–20+ years in technology transformation, enterprise program management, technology operations, and portfolio governance.

• Proven experience as a senior individual contributor in complex, global, matrixed technology organizations, ideally within India-based Global Capability Centres.

• Strong technology financial management, Enterprise/Scaled Agile, and GCC operating-model expertise.

• Financial services, investment banking, asset management, banking, or insurance experience strongly preferred; GCC / global delivery and global program management background essential.

• Working knowledge of cloud, data, AI, cybersecurity, platform engineering, DevSecOps, and automation.




Compensation & Engagement Details

• CTC: per industry standards

• Shift Timings: 1:30 PM – 10:30 PM IST

• Location: Mumbai (On-site)

• Reporting: Head of India Technology, with a matrix reporting line to the Global Head of Technology
