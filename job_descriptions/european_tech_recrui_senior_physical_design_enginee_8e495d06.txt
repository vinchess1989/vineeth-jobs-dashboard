Senior Physical Design Engineer




Want to work for a global leading semiconductor company? ... We're scaling the GPU Silicon team and they are now looking for a Physical Designer Engineer to come on board




This is an amazing opportunity to work on industry leading Graphics and GPU Cores with a leading company that are massively expanding - No previous GPU experience needed.




Responsibilities

Clock, power delivery network design and analysis
Deep knowledge of Physical Implementation
Physical synthesis
Place and route
Power analysis and optimizations
Semi-custom and custom block integration.
Involved in both implementation and development/automation of tasks for block implementation flow in addition to new methodology development and vendor tool evaluations and integration.
Work is in a dynamic team environment with aggressive schedule, chip power consumption and area targets.




Skills and Experience

Experience in Physical design / Circuit design.
Experience with physical synthesis and implementation tools
Good knowledge of timing analysis and power analysis
Understanding of CMOS circuit design.




If this is interesting for you - click apply / send me your CV on sk@eu-recruit.com




Key Words: Physical Design Engineer / Implementation / RTL / Synthesis / CMOS / Circuit Design / Synthesis
