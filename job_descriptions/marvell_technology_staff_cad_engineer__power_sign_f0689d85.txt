About Marvell

Marvell’s semiconductor solutions are the essential building blocks of the data infrastructure that connects our world. Across enterprise, cloud and AI, and carrier architectures, our innovative technology is enabling new possibilities.

At Marvell, you can affect the arc of individual lives, lift the trajectory of entire industries, and fuel the transformative potential of tomorrow. For those looking to make their mark on purposeful and enduring innovation, above and beyond fleeting trends, Marvell is a place to thrive, learn, and lead.

Your Team, Your Impact

You will be part of Marvell's Central CAD and Design Services (CCDS) organization — the engine that powers every business unit at Marvell. Our team develops and maintains world-class design flows and infrastructure that enable Marvell's semiconductor products across Data Center, Networking, Automotive, and Storage markets.

About PSI CAD team: CCDS CAD PSI team is responsible for driving Power Signal Integrity signoff methodology across Marvell's worldwide business units. The team develops and maintains PSI flows within M1DP, supports design teams through tapeout, and collaborates with EDA vendors to continuously improve tool capabilities and methodology. The team operates with a "One Marvell" mindset

What You Can Expect


Work on block-level and full-chip static IR/EM analysis flow and methodology
Work on dynamic IR analysis methodology (vectorless and vector-based using VCD/FSDB) at block and full-chip levels, with and without package models
Involved and tuning flows for Out-of-Context (OOC) and In-Context block-level IR analysis
Work on Left Shifted IR Analysis like Early Rail Analysis (ERA) to detect power grid weaknesses at floorplan stage
Interface with EDA vendors to evaluate new tool features, report bugs, and drive tool improvements.
Develop automation scripts (Tcl, Python) to streamline IR/EM flow execution, debug, and reporting.
Support tapeout activities by ensuring IR/EM signoff closure across blocks and full-chip designs.
Contribute to knowledge sharing, documentation, and training of IR?EM CAD flows across global teams.


What We're Looking For


B.E./B.Tech or M.E./M.Tech in Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field.
6+ years of hands-on experience with Cadence Voltus for IR drop and EM analysis in production ASIC/SoC flows
Proven track record of running block-level and full-chip PSI signoff on advanced technology nodes (7nm, 5nm, or 3nm)
Solid understanding of static/dynamic power and IR Analysis flow & Methodology (including hierarchical)
Good understanding of DC & AC-EM analysis and its impact
Understanding of package aware IR simulation and chip power model generation flows
Understanding of thermal flows is a plus
Understanding of multi-die IR/EM analysis is a plus
