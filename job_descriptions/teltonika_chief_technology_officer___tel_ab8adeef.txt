We are looking for a Chief Technology Officer to strengthen Teltonika Group at the headquarters level in Vilnius. This is a senior, group‑wide leadership role responsible for defining and guiding the technological vision and engineering excellence across multiple companies within the group.

This position goes beyond traditional technical leadership and focuses on strategic technology governance, process maturity, and scalable technology leadership. You will work closely with executive leadership and technology teams to ensure that efficiency, innovation, and long‑term technical sustainability are aligned with Teltonika's growth ambitions.

The headquarters, run by the parent company Teltonika IoT Group, coordinates the activities of Lithuanian high‑tech companies operating under the Teltonika brand as well as all representative offices worldwide. It plays a central role in shaping group‑level strategy, technological direction, and long‑term organizational structure.

Teltonika offers a unique opportunity to influence and shape technology development across a rapidly growing group of IoT‑focused companies, currently consisting of approximately five companies and expected to expand further.

In this role, you will:


Define and drive the group‑level technology strategy aligned with business objectives
Provide high‑level technical leadership and guidance across multiple companies within Teltonika Group
Evaluate and improve engineering efficiency, methodologies, and processes across the organization
Advise on strategic technology direction changes, balancing innovation, scalability, and sustainability
Assess current tools, platforms, and workflows (e.g., project management and development tools) and recommend improvements or alternative solutions where needed
Establish a clear balance between innovation, development, and maintenance activities, targeting an effective mix
Design future‑oriented organizational and technology structures that support group growth, including risk management and scalability considerations
Ensure consistent engineering standards, best practices, and governance across multiple entities
Act as a strategic partner to executive leadership on all technology‑related decisions
Support integration and technological alignment as the group grows from approximately five companies toward a larger, more complex structure


What we expect of you:


Extensive senior‑level experience in technology leadership roles such as CTO, Head of Engineering, or similar
Exceptional technical competence with the ability to assess and guide complex engineering organizations
Strong strategic mindset with the ability to connect technology decisions to long‑term business value
Experience designing and scaling engineering organizations, processes, and governance models
Ability to evaluate tools, workflows, and development practices critically and pragmatically
Understanding of balancing innovation, product development, and maintenance at scale
Proven experience working with executive leadership and multi‑company or complex organizational structures
Strong leadership, communication, and decision‑making skills
Fluent English, both spoken and written


What we offer:


Great opportunities to grow in a strong and rapidly expanding international company
A group‑level leadership role with significant influence on long‑term technology strategy
Dynamic, challenging work environment and a cooperation‑based internal culture
Care for your emotional and physical well‑being through additional health insurance, including psychologist consultations
Company‑paid sports activities (basketball, football, volleyball, yoga) and other sports events together
Celebration of achievements and quality time together during team‑buildings, summer festivals, and other important occasions
Opportunity to receive solid bonuses by participating in the employee recommendation program
Birthday gifts and a day off on your birthday, as well as surprises during various festive occasions
Recognition and acknowledgements on work anniversaries
Snacks provided at the office for lunch or dinner


Salary:

We appreciate everyone's efforts and experience, therefore we offer an attractive salary matching your skillset and expertise. The salary for this position ranges from 50 EUR/h up to 70 EUR/h (8000 EUR - 11200,00 EUR pre‑tax), depending on your experience and qualifications.

In the employment agreement, a fixed (tariff) hourly gross salary rate is specified and defined.

If you have questions, do not hesitate to contact our Talent Acquisition Senior Partner Jolita Reut, jolita.reut@teltonika.lt.
