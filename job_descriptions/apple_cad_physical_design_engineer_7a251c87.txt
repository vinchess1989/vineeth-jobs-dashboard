Do you love working on challenges that no one has solved yet? As a member of our dynamic group, you will have the unique and rewarding opportunity to craft upcoming products that will delight and inspire millions of Apple’s customers every single day.

Apple’s CAD Hardware team are responsible for automating processes in designing Apple’s chips which power everything from Apple Watch and Apple TV to iPhone and iPad. We have outstanding career opportunities for engineers interested in applying their software knowledge towards developing Apple’s ground breaking methodologies. Working among the industry's best, we’re looking for those with talent and ambition to innovate the way we design computer chips, to provide the next technological leap and improve customer experiences.

Description
As a PNR-CAD team member, you will have an impact a wide range of activities and domains in the PD world, The team develops and supports utilities that enable efficient work environment for the physical design teams across the world.

The role includes exploring new technologies and methodologies of first of its kind designs, complex implementation of designs from small ones to largest possible. The job includes hands-on work & impact on all layers of the design cycle, from infrastructure related automation for design efficiency through developing internal pnr flows which will enable efficient designs - floor-planning, clocking & optimization, custom route utilities, power grid creation & eco implementation, through developing signoff flows & verification scripts.

The job includes constant work with different teams and disciplines that interface with the PNR world, that includes Timing, power delivery, synthesis, Physical Verification and more. Along with the different discplines you will work with different teams, from vendors, pd team members and other CAD team members and discpline owners across the world to enable and improve the productivity of the PD flows.

Minimum Qualifications

BSc/MSc in Electrical Engineering or Computer Science


Preferred Qualifications

5+ years experience in ASIC P&R and flow development
Experience with all aspects of ASIC physical design including floorplanning, power-distribution, multi-voltage design, placement, CTS, and routing.
Strong TCL/Python scripting skills and LLM/GenAI implementation methods. Candidate should have experience developing complex algorithms, managing, and regressing P&R flows.
Candidate should be familiar with design signoff issues.
