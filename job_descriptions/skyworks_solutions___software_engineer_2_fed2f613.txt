Requisition ID: 77473

Description

Skyworks Solutions is seeking a highly motivated multidisciplinary individual to drive software/AI development and workflows automation to accelerate innovation across our semiconductor products. This role sits at the intersection of muti-tier software product and platform development and EDA tools, silicon design flows, and scalable software systems. Your work will make a direct impact on companies top and bottom line.

The ideal candidate has hands‑on experience building innovative products from ground up, automate processes, and thrives in a collaborative environment.

Responsibilities

What you will do


Build 'Software products and platform' for semiconductor design automation supporting design, tapeout and manufacturing
Develop software / AI solutions to improve productivity, quality, reliability and design reuse.
Integrate commercial EDA tools via API/CLI into Skyworks design flows
Collaborate with engineering teams to define automation requirements.
Debug design flow issues and improve tool reliability and scalability.
Define and develop good software development practices
Support semiconductor tape‑out schedules and continuous improvement initiatives
Use AI/ML to optimize process, built more predictability, reliability across the process flows.


Required Experience And Skills

What you bring

Qualifications


Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field
3+ years of relevant solid industry Full-stack software development experience
Stong experience with software tech-stack - User Interface. Backend (Python, C++, Java), Databases (SQL)
Experience with workflow automation
Experience using Machine learning models to solve complex challenges.
Experience developing custom AI agents, MCP servers
Experience leading DevOps (github, jenkins)
Experience hositng application on web server
Confortable working with both Linux and Windows environment
Experience with version control systems such as Git and/or Perforce/CI/CD
Familiarity with batch processing systems, job scheduling, and flow orchestration
Strong system design expert and a critical thinker


Software Engineering & Operations


Ability to host, operate, and scale web‑based software tools in Unix/Linux environments
Experience setting up and maintaining Apache (or similar) web servers in on‑premises environments
Working knowledge building software product which are secure by design


Cloud & Modern Tooling


Experience or familiarity with at least one cloud platform (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
Experience using AI‑assisted development tools (e.g., Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, or similar) to improve productivity and automation


Desired Experience And Skills

Nice to Have


Domain experience in semiconductor
Exposure to large‑scale EDA compute environments or regression infrastructures
Experience supporting hybrid on‑prem + cloud engineering platforms
Familiarity with CI/CD concepts applied to EDA flows and tooling
