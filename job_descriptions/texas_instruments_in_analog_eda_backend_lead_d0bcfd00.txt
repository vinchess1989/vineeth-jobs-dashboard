Job Description

Change the world. Love your job.

As an Analog EDA Lead in the team, you will be working on below EDA solutions.


Work with cross-functional teams within EDA and internal customers and stakeholders in IP and SoC design teams to define, evaluate, and ensure support new EDA solutions
Collaborate with vendors (Cadence, Synopsys etc) in technical tool evaluations, intersect TI methodologies with vendor roadmaps and offer best solutions to worldwide TI teams.
Interface between TI ATD teams, TI Foundry services team and external Foundry customers towards successful TI PDKs usage in Foundry design executions.


Qualifications

Minimum Requirements:


Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Electronics, Electrical Engineering or related field of study
4-10 years of relevant EDA/CAD experience combined with a passion for automation
2-3 years of having lead a team of EDA/CAD team


Preferred Qualifications


Good CMOS circuits basics, analog circuits simulation and layout experience
Demonstrated strong analytical and problem solving skills
Ability to work in teams and collaborate effectively with people in different functions
Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced and rapidly changing environment
Ability to take the initiative and drive to results
Skills to manage time, delegate and multi-task at work
Strong verbal and written communication skills
Team-work is imperative in this role

About Texas Instruments

Texas Instruments Incorporated (Nasdaq: TXN) is a global semiconductor company that designs, manufactures and sells analog and embedded processing chips for markets such as industrial, automotive, data center, personal electronics and communications equipment. At our core, we have a passion to create a better world by making electronics more affordable through semiconductors. This passion is alive today as each generation of innovation builds upon the last to make our technology more reliable, more affordable and lower power, making it possible for semiconductors to go into electronics everywhere.
