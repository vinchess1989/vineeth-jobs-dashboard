Principal Signal Integrity Engineer




Principal Signal Integrity Engineer required for a company developing high-speed communication and data compute technologies. This Principal Signal Integrity Engineer role will focus on high-speed interface design and system-level performance optimisation. The Principal Signal Integrity Engineer will work closely with cross-functional engineering teams on complex hardware platforms.




Responsibilities

Lead signal integrity design and analysis for high-speed digital systems
Perform simulation and modelling of high-speed interfaces and interconnects
Define PCB stack-ups, routing constraints, and impedance control strategies
Support board-level design, validation, and debugging activities
Work with hardware, packaging, and silicon teams to optimise system performance




Requirements

Degree in Electronics Engineering or related field
Strong experience in signal integrity for high-speed systems
Experience with simulation tools (e.g. HyperLynx, ADS, HFSS, Sigrity)
Knowledge of high-speed PCB design, DDR, SerDes, or similar interfaces
Ability to work across system, board, and component-level challenges




If you’re interested in the position of Principal Signal Integrity Engineer, please apply or contact Chris Moffatt for more details.
