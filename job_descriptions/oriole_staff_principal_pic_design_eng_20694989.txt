Key Responsibilities:




Own the design and simulation activity of key passive and active components within the Oriole PDK
Lead the design customisation of high performance key photonic building blocks within a commercial foundry PDK
Work collaboratively within the Oriole PIC team on mask layout and take the lead on tape-out cycles into a commercial foundry
Compose, analyse and deliver design specifications and other documentation within the Oriole Photonics supply chain.
Support collaborative activities between Oriole and its suppliers
Provide analysis and drive parts of Oriole's technology roadmap
Drive test development and data analysis activities







Essential Skills:




PhD in Physics with a focus on Photonics, Semiconductors or Optical Networks.
+10 years of relevant professional experience in Photonics Industry or academia
Strong simulation skills in photonics (FDTD, EME) and hands on experience with commercial relevant software
In depth knowledge of device physics and design of III-V semiconductor optical devices such as lasers, optical amplifiers, modulators and detectors
Experience on passive Si/SiN photonics design, simulation, layout and characterisation.
In depth understanding of photonic process integration and understanding of processing limitations on PIC performance (principles of design for manufacture)
Hands on experience with III-V epitaxial layer design using commercial or self-developed tools
Experience on designing high speed optical devices such as modulators and detectors for high baud rates and multi-level modulation formats.
Good understanding of Photonic device manufacturing principles, Design for manufacture, yield improvement, Design for test and reliability.
Use of ML/AI methodologies or other computational optimisation techniques in photonic design simulations
Good understanding of PIC parameter impact on system performance (e.g. such as BER, link budget, power penalties etc), any system modelling experience.







Desired Skills:




Photonic device characterisation, hands on experience with testing methodologies for wafer level and die level test
High speed RF design characterisation
Strong Layout/Mask Design skills using commercial photonic layout tools (e.g. Synopsys, GDS Factory, Nazca, Klayout)
RF simulation experience
Strong programming skills (preferably Python), and familiar with version control methodology
Good understanding of Optical networks and Applications of photonics for AI in data centres
Excellent Communication and presentation skills, team working
Good understanding of photonics industry trends for AI (new materials)
Agentic approach to photonic design, test or analysis automation.
