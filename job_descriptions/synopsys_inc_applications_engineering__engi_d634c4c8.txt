We Are:

At Synopsys, we drive the innovations that shape the way we live and connect. Our technology is central to the Era of Pervasive Intelligence, from self-driving cars to learning machines. We lead in chip design, verification, and IP integration, empowering the creation of high-performance silicon chips and software content. Join us to transform the future through continuous technological innovation.

You Are:

You are an early-career engineer passionate about solving challenging design problems in the semiconductor industry. With a strong foundation in physical implementation flows and a customer-first mindset, you’re excited to learn and contribute on advanced technology nodes using Synopsys’ industry-leading EDA tools. You take initiative, look for opportunities to improve workflows, and approach new problems with curiosity and resilience. You collaborate effectively with customers and internal teams, communicating technical concepts clearly and turning findings into actionable next steps. As a link between customers and R&D, you help translate real-world design needs into clear requirements—supporting continued improvements in performance, power, and area (PPA) and enabling customer success.

What You’ll Be Doing:


Solving complex PPA (Performance, Power, Area) challenges using Synopsys’ latest implementation technologies, including AI/ML Implementation, Physical Synthesis RTL-GDS.
Solving complex Formal verification challenges, early power estimation using latest technologies
Benchmarking Synopsys solutions against competitors to displace alternative implementation flows and demonstrate superior results.
Developing, debugging, and optimizing Synthesis implementation methodologies and flows for emerging semiconductor technologies.
Providing technical solutions by identifying design and EDA tool issues and delivering actionable resolutions for customers.
Translating technical findings into requirements for R&D, driving tool enhancements and adaptive long-term solutions.
Deploying new technology features in the latest EDA tool versions and enabling customer migration for best-in-class PPA.
Collaborating closely with Synopsys R&D and product development teams to shape future technology directions.


The Impact You Will Have:


Elevating customer success by delivering robust technical solutions and enabling optimal tool utilization.
Driving Synopsys’ competitive advantage by displacing rival implementation flows and showcasing superior PPA outcomes.
Contributing to the enhancement of Synopsys products by providing critical feedback and requirements to R&D.
Accelerating customer adoption of new technology nodes and EDA tool versions to achieve best-in-class results.
Strengthening Synopsys’ reputation as the industry leader in physical implementation and innovation.
Fostering long-term customer relationships through proactive engagement and continuous support.


What You’ll Need:


B.Tech or M.Tech in Electronics, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or related fields from a recognized institution
0-2 years of experience in synthesis, physical design, or ASIC implementation, whether from internships, academic projects, or early-career roles.
Familiar with methodology changes aimed at achieving targeted PPA metrics for complex designs.
Proficiency in synthesis and formal verification tools and flows is highly advantageous.
Knowledge in scripting skills (TCL, Unix, Perl) for automation and debugging.
Excellent communication skills, including the ability to interface effectively with customers and internal teams.


Who You Are:


Self-motivated and dedicated, with a passion for continuous learning and improvement.
Strong analytical thinker with outstanding debug skills and attention to detail.
Collaborative team player who thrives in dynamic, fast-paced environments.
Customer-centric, always advocating for the best outcomes and solutions.
Resilient and adaptable, able to manage multiple priorities and deliver results.


The Team You’ll Be A Part Of:

Join our Application Engineering team in Bangalore, where you’ll work directly with industry pioneers and innovators. The team’s mission is to solve critical design challenges, enhance quality-of-results metrics, and empower customers to achieve best-in-class performance. Collaboration with R&D and product development is central, ensuring that customer needs drive future technology advancements. Together, we set the standards for excellence in semiconductor design and implementation.

Rewards and Benefits:

We offer a comprehensive range of health, wellness, and financial benefits to cater to your needs. Our total rewards include both monetary and non-monetary offerings. Your recruiter will provide more details about the salary range and benefits during the hiring process.
