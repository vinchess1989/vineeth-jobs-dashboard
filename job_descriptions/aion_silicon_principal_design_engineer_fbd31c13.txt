Job details

Are you an experienced Principal Design Engineer looking for your next challenge?


Aion Silicon is actively building a pipeline of talented engineers for future opportunities, and we’d love to hear from skilled professionals who are passionate about Design.

With design centers across the UK, Spain, Hyderabad, and Morocco, we offer the flexibility to base this role in any of our global locations. If you’re interested in exploring future opportunities with us, please click the link below to apply and register your interest.

Purpose of role

The primary aim of this role is to lead front-end design activities within customer projects. A lead technical planner and contributor on ASIC and IP development projects, working very closely with the programmed management and senior design leads to implement and track digital IP designs and integrate these and 3rd party designs into customer ASICs and SoCs.

The role involves working directly with customers, IP vendors and architects to define innovative tailored solutions that can be translated into high quality, achievable designs.

The candidate will own and technically lead aspects of a design or ASIC/SoC integration and will lead and monitor the interactions between multi-site engineering teams to ensure efficient communication, resolve design and methodology issues and make effective decisions to ensure projects remain on track with respect to schedules, budgets, and quality commitments.  

Responsibilities

Leading a successful team

Gather creative and talented technical staff and form a team for achievement of targets

Provide technical and practical support to engineering team members as required. Provide training and documentation on methodologies and best practice.

Keep up to date with relevant engineering advances in the field and ensure that Aion Silicon is kept at the forefront of the state-of-the-art technologies, methodologies and design processes as used in the industry

Work with broader engineering teams and engineering management to define new or improved methodologies and ways of working. Effectively delegate tasks relating to technical design and flow development.

Work with programme management team to identify risks and opportunities, estimate engineering effort and update on progress against project plans.

Definition of design intent/microarchitecture and new design methodology and clear documentation thereof.

Trialling and deployment of industry-leading EDA tools for design quality assurance, power optimisation, constraints generation and synthesis/timing analysis.

Track development activities and administer milestones deliverables.

Collate engineering data and provide regular feedback to customers and programme management about plans, risks, issues, progress etc.

Ownership of design blocks and technical leadership of all frontend aspects of their development from specification through handover with physical design team. Ongoing technical support through to productization.

Represent Aion Silicon at universities, conferences and meetings and present technical papers

Qualifications

A degree/masters or PhD in either a relevant or non-relevant subject

Essential

·          Typically, 10+ years’ experience

·          Very Strong knowledge of digital design concepts and application in complex SoCs

·          Experience with High-Performance Compute (HPC) architectures, including:

o   CPU design and optimisation

o   Interconnect design

o   Inter-die or Chip-Chip high-speed data interfaces

·          Serves as a subject matter expert within a technical discipline with latitude for decision making

·          Serves as an independent individual contributor to technical project and lead the technical direction of team members

·          Solves technical problems of advanced complexity and conveys/applies solutions

·          Evaluates issues and defines solutions

·          Applies judgment in interpreting results and conducting quantitative analysis

·          May manage others on the team

·          Provides technical training

·          Contributes to technical white papers

·          Contributes to sales support as part of a team effort, such as a Statement of Work

·          Listens to customer feedback, recognizes opportunities and provides feedback for technical innovation

Use of Issue and Task tracking tools (e.g Jira)

Attributes

Excellent leadership, communication and negotiation skills and time management skills

Self-organisation and ability to respond to changing priorities quickly

Ability to work under pressure

Organisation and problem-solving skills

Self-motivated

Excellent attention to detail
