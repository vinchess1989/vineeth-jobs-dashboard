European Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (ESMC) GmbH is the joint venture between TSMC, Bosch, Infineon and NXP. For the first time, global technology leader TSMC is building a semiconductor fab in the heart of Europe, offering cutting-edge manufacturing technologies for next-generation semiconductor products. ESMC represents a shared commitment to strengthening the European semiconductor ecosystem.
For our future fab in Dresden, we are looking for a highly motivated and committed:
IT Engineer - Corporate IT Service (m/f/d)


We are looking for enthusiastic early-career engineers ready to make a real impact. If you are passionate about IT, innovation, and want to play an active part in building something truely extraordinary, we encourage you to apply.
Depending on your previous experience and avialbility you will have the opportunity for on the job training at advanced manufactring facility at TSMC in Taiwan for up to one year.
Job Responsibilities
1. Work closely with stakeholders and cross-functional teams to ensure user’s success.
2. Serve as day-to-day contact for assigned user groups, providing support, building trust and rapport while identifying areas of opportunity, highlighting best practices, and documentation.
3. Effectively gather, clarify, and prioritize requirements from users in terms of business flows, system constraints, and even pain points and provide proposals and deliver user wish day to HQ PM team / DevOps team.
4. May involved in design, assessment, and project management of local IT solution introductions if HQ cannot provide global solution or special needs triggered by local policy and rules. And sometimes support proofreading to make sure the quality of localization.
Job Qualifications
1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Information Engineering, Automation or Electrical Engineering or similar technical fields with equivalent practical experience.
2. 1-2 years IT work/ Internship experience in IT area. Concept of software engineering and product life cycle management. Strong development skills in languages such as Java, Go or Node.js.
3. A can-do attitude with mature, stable, and open-minded mindset to complete job tasks, demonstrating strong customer service orientation, teamwork spirit, project management, communication, and interpersonal skills, along with exceptional organizational abilities to prioritize and deliver assignments efficiently under tight deadlines. Views problem-solving as an opportunity to improve systems and actively embrace automation over manual effort.
4. Fluent in English (C1) AND proficiency (at least B2) in either German OR Mandarin.


Location: Dresden, Germany. 
Please apply with English resume.
Each journey starts with a first step.
Joining ESMC at this early stage will provide you with the unique opportunity to actively shape the future of the fab in Dresden, boost your career and put your true potential to the test. You will be working in a fast paced, international startup environment whilst at the same time having access to the experience, talent, and resources of the largest and most advanced semiconductor manufacturer in the world, TSMC. You have ample opportunities to learn about TSMC Operations, Digital Transformation, and various advanced Facilities related topics, both in Taiwan and other sites around the world.
Behind the world’s most advanced technology are real people with real passion. Our success is fueled by our core values: Integrity, Commitment, Innovation and Customer Trust (ICIC).
