European Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (ESMC) GmbH is the joint venture between TSMC, Bosch, Infineon and NXP. For the first time, global technology leader TSMC is building a semiconductor fab in the heart of Europe, offering cutting-edge manufacturing technologies for next-generation semiconductor products. ESMC represents a shared commitment to strengthening the European semiconductor ecosystem.
For our future fab in Dresden, we are looking for a highly motivated and committed:
Process Integration Engineer (m/f/d)


Support our customers in achieving full lifecycle management for their products by ensuring internal coordination of Process Engineering and Equipment Engineering. By utilizing state-of-the-art, agile, and intelligent operating systems you will integrate New Product Introduction (NPI), Device Performance, Yield and Reliability. Pursue your passion for Engineering leading the integration of the process flow.
Job Responsibilities
1. New product qualification and technology transfers from a mother fab.
2. Big data analysis to identify process design weaknesses and/or manufacturing tool issues.
3. Designing, executing, and analyzing experiments to improve yield and performance for each specific product.
4. Collaborating with the engineering teams to maintain process stability and provide technical solutions to customers.
5. By collaborating with Product engineering & Quality Reliability teams, you could learn yield and failure analysis skill to dig out the low yield or quality/reliability problems. Then come out the device, design, or process solution to fix the issues.
6. Scheduled evening shifts and weekend on-duty/on-call rotations in a 24/7 high-volume manufacturing environment.
Job Qualifications
1. Legally eligible to work in Germany.
2. Available for on-the-job training in Taiwan for one year.
3. Degree in Electrical, Chemical or Mechanical Engineering, Materials Science, Physics or a related technical field.
4. Fluent in English with good command of German.
5. Familiar with process change management in a manufacturing environment.
6. A strong team player that can multitask, coordinate between teams.
7. Knowledge of semiconductor manufacturing and design is a plus.
8. Willing to work in a clean room environment.
