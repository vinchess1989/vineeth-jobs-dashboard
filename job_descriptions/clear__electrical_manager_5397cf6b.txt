Electrical Package Manager (ELV) – Data Centre Construction

Hamina, Finland

€75,000 – €90,000 + Comprehensive Benefits Package

17 Days On / 4 Days Off Rotation




About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Electrical Package Manager (ELV) to join a major hyperscale data centre project in Hamina, Finland. This is an excellent opportunity to play a key role in the delivery of a large-scale, mission-critical facility while working with a leading international project team.




The successful candidate will take ownership of the Extra Low Voltage (ELV) package, ensuring the successful planning, coordination, installation, testing, and commissioning of ELV systems throughout the project lifecycle.




Key Responsibilities

Manage the delivery of all ELV systems across the data centre project.
Coordinate subcontractors, suppliers, consultants, and client representatives.
Monitor programme milestones and ensure package delivery aligns with project schedules.
Oversee quality assurance, inspections, and compliance with project specifications.
Manage testing, commissioning, and handover activities for ELV systems.
Review technical drawings, specifications, RFIs, and design changes.
Track package budgets, procurement schedules, and commercial performance.
Ensure all works are delivered in accordance with health and safety standards.
Provide regular progress reporting to senior project leadership.
Support project close-out and final documentation processes.




ELV Systems Experience

Candidates should have experience managing some or all of the following:

Structured Cabling Systems
CCTV & Security Systems
Access Control
Intruder Detection Systems
Public Address / Voice Alarm Systems
Fire Alarm Interfaces
Building Management System (BMS) Interfaces
ICT & Communications Infrastructure
Network and Fibre Installations




Requirements

Proven experience as an Electrical Package Manager, Electrical Project Manager, or Senior Electrical Engineer.
Previous data centre, mission-critical, pharmaceutical, semiconductor, or large-scale industrial project experience preferred.
Strong knowledge of ELV systems and associated installation practices.
Experience managing subcontractors and multidisciplinary teams.
Ability to drive programme, quality, and safety performance.
Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills.
EU work eligibility is advantageous.




Package & Benefits

Salary: €75,000 – €90,000

Flights provided

Accommodation provided

Monthly tax-free allowance

Travel expenses covered

17 days working away / 4 days home rotation

Opportunity to work on a landmark data centre project in Finland
