Job Summary:   

As a Senior Engineer, Layout Lead, you will contribute to the establishment of best practices in layout team knowledge, skill, analytics, workflows and business processes. Integral to your role, you will serve as authority in team deliverables and coach new hires in ramping up capability to contribute quickly to team deliverables 

 
Main Responsibilities:   

Deploy past experience in layout planning & optimization and tool movement execution excellence in contextually relevant manner 

Establish foundational and benchmark knowledge and skill base for defining team deliverable standard 

Set up workflow and business processes for team to effectively and efficiently meet deliverables 

Contribute to advancement in capability of global layout team network 

Develop training material and plan for new hires 

Other Responsibilities:  

Advocate for balanced and collaborative approach in complex cross-functional team efforts 

Deploy appropriate AI-driven solutions in achieving effective and efficient outcomes for self and teams  

Job Requirements/Qualification/ Selection Criteria : 

Minimum 5 years of relevant experience in layout planning & optimization and tool movement execution in semiconductor industry 

Bachelors degree in Industrial/Mechanical/Civil or related discipline  

Advance Proficiency in AutoCad 

Proficiency in M365 Apps (such as Microsoft Excel, Microsoft PowerPoint etc) and collaboration tools (Confluence, Microsoft Loop, Microsoft Sharepoint, etc) 

Strong criticalthinking skills, with the ability to analyze constraints, evaluate tradeoffs, and support datadriven decisionmaking in layout planning.  

Strong communication skills and a collaborative mindset to drive actions and alignment with crossfunctional stakeholders.  

Highly motivated, adaptable, and comfortable working in a fastpaced, dynamic, and rapidly changing environment.  

Ability to multi-task and organize work systematically in meeting deadlines without compromising on quality.  

Physically able to work inside a cleanroom environment, including wearing full cleanroom attire (smock, hood, gloves, boots) and performing onsite checks such as layout validations and equipmentarea walkthroughs.  

Demonstrated at least 1 year of leadership experience as project lead or team lead 

Hands-on experience in training multiple new hires 

Hands-on experience in AI (Chatbot, Agentic AI, Automation, etc) tools in augmenting work delivery in tactical or project settings
