Senior Digital ASIC Design Engineer - Rijswijk




A major US semiconductor manufacturer is looking for several Senior Digital ASIC Design Engineers to help shape the direction of cutting-edge IP for complex digital-AMS ASIC solutions.

You will be part of a global team tasked with the design and development of existing and brand new products. Taking a major hold in a niche market, and having a unique opportunity to work across the full flow.




The desired applicant for the role of senior digital designer will have strong front end digital design knowledge, as well a broad understanding of the whole digital design flow.




Requirements:

Degree / Masters / Doctorate in electronics, micro-electronics, physics or similar related field
Strong industry experience of challenging ASIC projects and their successful tape-out / delivery
Front end digital design knowledge & fundamentals - RTL coding and IP development in Verilog
Understanding of analog / mixed-signal chips or IP - ADC / DAC interfaces, PLL, DSP filters etc
Synthesis, static timing analysis (STA), timing constraints is nice to have
scripting & software coding skills - python, C, system C




Bonus / "nice-to-have" skills

DFT - scan insertion
modelling / simulation in matlab / simulink
DSP, signal-processing
UVM verification - system verilog, creating test benches, assertions, formal methods
low-power constraints, UPF




Excellent salaries, benefits, stocks, bonus, and visa sponsorship are all available for the correctly qualified candidate.
