Join Ranovus as an Analog ASIC Design Engineer

(All Experience Levels Welcome!)

Location: Nuremberg, Germany

Team: Analog & Mixed-Signal IC Design and Integration

Company: Ranovus – Innovating the Future of Data Center Connectivity

About Us

At Ranovus, we’re powering the next generation of data center infrastructure with cutting-edge fiber-optic communication technologies. With operations in Ottawa (Canada), Nuremberg (Germany), and San Jose (USA), we’re on a mission to deliver high-performance solutions that reduce environmental impact through lower power consumption.

What You’ll Do

As part of our Analog & Mixed-Signal team, you’ll help design and develop advanced SoC functions, including:


Low-power analog and mixed-signal circuits in CMOS
High-speed components like TIAs and Drivers
Lab characterization and system performance testing
Supporting system architecture and modeling
Collaborating across disciplines to bring ideas to life
Owning delivery timelines with a “get-it-done” mindset


What You Bring

We’re Hiring Across Experience Levels, So Whether You’re Just Starting Out Or a Seasoned Expert, We’d Love To Hear From You. Ideal Candidates Will Have


3+ years in analog/mixed-signal IC design (or equivalent experience)
Strong grasp of analog IC design, layout, and verification
Proven delivery of circuits like bandgaps, OpAmps, OTAs, LDOs, TIAs, Drivers
Familiarity with Verilog / Verilog-A (a plus!)
Hands-on experience with lab characterization and RF measurements
Basic understanding of IC production and foundry collaboration
Ability to guide outsourced design teams
Team spirit and excellent communication
Fluency in English
EU nationality or valid EU visa (must-have)


Why Ranovus?

Because we don’t just build chips—we build the future. Join a team that values innovation, collaboration, and impact.
