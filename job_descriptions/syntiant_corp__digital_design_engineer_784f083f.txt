Syntiant Corp., is a leader in the high-growth AI software and semiconductor solutions space. We are seeking a lead Digital Design Engineer who will be designing the digital systems and building blocks for MEMS microphones. Together with the analog/mixed-signal designers, test development engineers and other team members, you will be developing high-performance, low-power ASICs from concept to high-volume production.

Requirements

Specific Duties and Responsibilities:


Develop the digital architecture including audio signal path (DSP) and analog-digital interface with the project team
Build complex functional and behavioral models of the systems using Matlab/Simulink, Verilog-AMS, etc
Perform RTL design, synthesis, timing closure, scan pattern generation, power optimization and supervise the RTL-to-GDS flow
Define the digital verification plan
Verify the digital system and blocks in collaboration with the digital verification engineer
Develop low-power audio signal processing architectures


Qualifications, Education, and Experience Required:


Advanced Degree in electronic engineering or related field
8+ years of proven track record in developing digital blocks/systems for high-performance mixed-signal integrated circuits in a consumer or industrial R&D environment
Good understanding of the entire digital design flow and DFT
Experience with Digital Signal Processing (DSP) implementations
Hands-on experience in designing low-power digital circuits
Good understanding of most common concepts and metrics used in analog/mixed signal design (Sampling, Quantization, FFT, SNR, THD, etc.)
Experience with sigma delta ADCs or audio signal processing
Understanding of the most common limitations and issues of analog circuits (offset, gain error, distortion, etc.) is an advantage
Knowledge in EDA tools such as Cadence Digital EDA suite (Genus, Tempus, Jasper, Voltus, Modus, Conformal etc.) is an advantage
Solid knowledge of Matlab/Simulink environment
Structured and methodological approach
Strong team player
Good spoken and written communication skills in English

About Syntiant:

Founded in 2017 and headquartered in Irvine, Calif., Syntiant Corp. is a leader in delivering hardware and software solutions for edge AI deployment. The company's purpose-built silicon and hardware-agnostic models are being deployed globally to power edge AI speech, audio, sensor and vision applications across a wide range of consumer and industrial use cases, from earbuds to automobiles. Syntiant's advanced chip solutions merge deep learning with semiconductor design to produce ultra-low-power, high performance, deep neural network processors. Syntiant also provides compute-efficient software solutions with proprietary model architectures that enable world-leading inference speed and minimized memory footprint across a broad range of processors. The company is backed by several of the world's leading strategic and financial investors including Intel Capital, Microsoft's M12, Applied Ventures, Bosch Ventures, the Amazon Alexa Fund, and Atlantic Bridge Capital.
