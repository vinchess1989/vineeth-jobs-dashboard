About RTLYNX

RTLYNX is a semiconductor and intelligent systems engineering company focused on AI-driven product development across chip, edge, and cloud technologies. We deliver advanced semiconductor design and engineering solutions for global customers.

Role Overview

We are looking for highly skilled RTL Design Engineers with strong expertise in Micro-Architecture and RTL Design for complex SoC/ASIC development programs.

The ideal candidate should have hands-on experience in translating architecture specifications into high-quality RTL implementations and working closely with verification, DFT, and implementation teams.

Key Responsibilities
Develop Micro-Architecture specifications from system and architecture requirements.
Design and implement RTL using Verilog/SystemVerilog.
Perform design reviews and ensure coding best practices.
Collaborate with Verification, DFT, Physical Design, and STA teams.
Support synthesis, timing closure, and design sign-off activities.
Debug and resolve functional and integration issues.
Participate in SoC-level design integration activities.
Required Skills
5+ years of RTL Design experience.
Strong expertise in Micro-Architecture and RTL Design.
Proficiency in Verilog/SystemVerilog.
Good understanding of ASIC/SoC design flow.
Experience with synthesis and linting tools.
Knowledge of AMBA protocols (AXI/AHB/APB) is preferred.
Strong debugging and problem-solving skills.
Mandatory Requirements

✔ 5+ years of relevant RTL Design experience

✔ Strong Micro-Architecture experience
