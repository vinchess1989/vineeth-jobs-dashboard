General Summary

As a leading technology innovator, Qualcomm pushes the boundaries of what's possible to enable next-generation experiences and drives digital transformation to help create a smarter, connected future for all. As a Qualcomm Hardware Engineer, you will plan, design, optimize, verify, and test electronic systems, bring-up yield, circuits, mechanical systems, Digital/Analog/RF/optical systems, equipment and packaging, test systems, FPGA, and/or DSP systems that launch cutting-edge, world class products. Qualcomm Hardware Engineers collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop solutions and meet performance requirements.

Minimum Qualifications


Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Electrical/Electronics Engineering, Engineering, or related field and 2+ years of Hardware Engineering or related work experience.



OR

Master's degree in Computer Science, Electrical/Electronics Engineering, Engineering, or related field and 1+ year of Hardware Engineering or related work experience.

OR

PhD in Computer Science, Electrical/Electronics Engineering, Engineering, or related field.

Qualcomm Global SoC Central Physical Design team in Bangalore is actively seeking candidates for positions for developing synthetic data generation algorithms for SoC applications. These algorithms will help Qualcomm create chips that deliver the best power, performance and area, while minimizing compute and designer effort. The ideal candidate has a BS or MS in EE/ECE/CS, and 0-4 years of experience in developing complex algorithmic software in Python. Familiarity with VLSI physical design algorithms is a strong plus. The most important attributes for this role are strong inter-personal and communication skills, ability to work with a global team, a positive attitude and intellectual curiosity.

Description


Work with physical design experts to understand layout patterns in hand-designed digital blocks
Design and develop algorithms to replicate these patterns in synthetic data, to train GenAI models
Test and deploy software implementations of these algorithms, and incorporate customer feedback into improving the algorithms



Skills


The ideal candidate has a BS or MS in EE/ECE/CS, and 0-4 years of experience in developing complex algorithmic software in Python.
Strong Python skills, geometry packages such as shapely, docker
Graph algorithms, combinatorial algorithms, optimization are a strong plus
SOC domain and AI/ML background are a strong plus
