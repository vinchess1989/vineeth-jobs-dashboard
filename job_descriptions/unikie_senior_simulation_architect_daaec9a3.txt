WE ARE LOOKING FOR Senior Simulation Architect

We are looking for a Senior Simulation Architect to help our customers develop and validate the next generation of autonomous and intelligent off-road machines. In this role, you will design simulation and validation solutions that support the development of autonomous driving functions, machine control systems, advanced operator assistance features, and software-defined machines. You will work closely with customer engineering teams, helping them accelerate development through simulation, virtual validation, digital twins, and AI-assisted engineering workflows.

As a consultant, you will take a leading role in technical discussions, architecture design, and customer collaboration. You will bridge software engineering, systems engineering, and simulation technologies to create practical solutions for real-world machine development challenges.

You will help shape how future autonomous machines are developed and validated. Your work will directly influence the way our customers build safer, smarter, and more capable machines through simulation and virtual engineering.

What You Will Do


Design simulation and validation architectures for autonomous and intelligent machines
Define virtual validation environments, digital twin concepts, and simulation workflows
Design system integrations between vehicle software, control systems, sensors, simulators, and analysis tools
Create traceable validation processes linking requirements, scenarios, software versions, test executions, and findings
Support customers in simulation strategy, technology selection, and architecture decisions
Lead technical workshops and participate in customer solution planning
Guide development teams and provide technical leadership throughout project deliveries
Contribute to the growth of Unikie's simulation, autonomy, and virtual validation capabilities


Required Qualifications


Several years of experience in simulation, systems engineering, software architecture, or autonomous systems development
Experience designing complex technical systems and software-intensive products
Understanding of software verification, validation, and testing methodologies
Experience with one or more simulation platforms such as Unreal Engine, Unity, NVIDIA Isaac Sim, CARLA, Gazebo, MATLAB/Simulink, or equivalent
Understanding of autonomous systems, machine control systems, sensor technologies, and vehicle software architectures
Experience with distributed systems, middleware, APIs, and data integration
Strong communication skills and ability to work directly with customer stakeholders and engineering teams


Considered an advantage


Experience in autonomous mobile machines, mining equipment, construction machinery, agricultural machinery, robotics, or automotive systems
Experience with ROS/ROS2 ecosystems
Knowledge of LiDAR, radar, camera, GNSS, IMU, and sensor fusion technologies
Experience with digital twins and simulation-driven development
Familiarity with AI-assisted engineering and validation solutions
