Job Description



This role will work in our Device and Product Group supporting New Product Introduction (NPI) of our state-of-the-art GaN semiconductor devices as well as other engineering projects. This experienced semiconductor product engineer will work in a cross functional role that involves the ownership of new products from concept to qualification. The role requires coordinating activities across multiple departments (R&D, Design, Epitaxial, Fabrication, Package Assembly, Test and Reliability, etc.).



Lead the NPI process and coordinate the power device products definition, design, fabrication, testing and qualification, taking them from tape-out to production release.
Work closely with the marketing, sales, engineering and manufacturing teams to develop NPI plans.
Negotiate schedules to align with resources and customer requirements.
Track and communicates status, key results, risks, and problems to all stakeholders to ensure alignment of priorities. Schedule and run design review activities.
Monitor and analyzes testing and qualification results to write engineering reports.
Develop screening methods during NPI phase for qualification.
Drive root cause investigations of performance and qualification issues.
Own and support other engineering projects


Qualifications


5+ years in semiconductor manufacturing, yield engineering and power electronics is a plus.
BS in an engineering or science discipline and/or MS in engineering or science.
Experience with APQP design review processes, FMEA, NPI and SPC.
Understanding of process and product development, wafer level processing, assembly and test methods.
Exceptional time management skills.
Ability to take initiative and work effectively in teams.
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
Proficiency with MS Office.
Experience with discrete device testing and semiconductor packaging will be a plus.
Experience with engineering databases, automation and statistical analysis using software such as JMP.
