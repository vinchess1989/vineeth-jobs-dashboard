We Are: 

At Synopsys, we drive the innovations that shape the way we live and connect. Our technology is central to the Era of Pervasive Intelligence, from self-driving cars to learning machines. We lead in chip design, verification, and IP integration, empowering the creation of high-performance silicon chips and software content. Join us to transform the future through continuous technological innovation.

You Are:

You are an experienced and strategic product leader with deep expertise in semiconductor power integrity and reliability. You thrive in complex, high-impact environments, shaping product direction and translating market needs into innovative solutions. You bring a strong mix of technical depth and business acumen, connecting customer challenges with product strategy and execution.

You are comfortable operating at a senior level, influencing leadership and driving decisions that impact business growth. A natural thought leader, you anticipate industry trends and position products effectively in a competitive landscape. You communicate clearly and confidently, translating complex technical concepts into meaningful business value.

You enjoy working cross-functionally, partnering with sales, application engineering, and product teams to drive adoption and deliver impactful solutions. You are proactive, forward-thinking, and passionate about guiding products through their full lifecycle—from concept to launch and beyond.

What You’ll Be Doing:

Lead product initiatives within semiconductor power integrity and reliability for the flagship product, RedHawk-SC
Drive integration initiatives between RedHawk-SC and other Synopsys solutions
Act as a technology evangelist, providing deep use-case expertise and thought leadership within your domain
Engage directly with key customers to support roadmap planning, solution deployment, and long-term partnerships
Represent Synopsys at industry forums, including conferences, field events, and executive roundtables
Partner with ACE and sales teams to drive product adoption and business growth
Own the end-to-end product lifecycle, from functional definition through launch and eventual retirement
Translate market insights and customer feedback into product strategy and roadmap development

The Impact You Will Have:

Shape the direction of industry-leading power integrity and reliability solutions
Drive adoption and growth of Synopsys’ flagship products through strong customer engagement
Influence product strategy through deep understanding of market needs and emerging trends
Strengthen Synopsys’ position as a technology leader in the semiconductor space
Enable customers to solve complex design challenges with innovative and reliable solutions
Foster strong alignment across cross-functional teams to deliver high-impact product outcomes

What You’ll Need:

Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD in Electrical Engineering or a closely related field, with relevant industry experience across engineering, simulation, or related domains
Strong expertise in power integrity, reliability, or a related domain
Experience in engineering, computer-aided engineering (CAE) simulations, pre-sales support, sales, or customer engagement
Proven experience developing product strategies and go-to-market (GTM) initiatives based on market insights
Ability to clearly communicate the business value of complex technical innovations to senior stakeholders
Demonstrated ability to influence decisions and operate effectively in cross-functional environments

The Team You’ll Be A Part Of:

You will join a high-impact product management team focused on advancing industry-leading semiconductor solutions in power integrity and reliability. The team works closely with engineering, customers, and business stakeholders to define and deliver cutting-edge capabilities in Synopsys’ flagship products. Together, you will play a critical role in shaping product strategy, driving customer success, and maintaining Synopsys’ leadership in the semiconductor ecosystem.
