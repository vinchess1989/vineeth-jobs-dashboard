Experience in Spec extraction, Architecture/Microarchitecture definition, Digital IP/Subsystem Design/Integration, Mixed signal IP Integration, SoC Integration.

Your Role

Key responsibilities in your new role:


Architecture Definition: Must be able to extract requirements from Specs, Partition requirements, define Architecture/microarchitecture.
Digital Lead: Must be able to act as a Digital functional lead for execution lead of IP/Sub-System or an SoC.
Technical Expert: Must be able to provide Technical expertise on Digital Design, Architecture & Front-End flow.
Cross Functional Collaboration: Must be able to engage with various cross functional teams including Verification, Analog, Back-End, Marketing, Validation to ensure alignment on product objectives.


Your Profile

Qualifications And Skills To Help You Succeed


Bachelor's or Master's degree in a relevant field, such as Electronics, Digital Design, VLSI, SoC Design.
Over 15 years of experience in Spec extraction, Architecture/Microarchitecture definition, Digital IP/Subsystem Design/Integration, Mixed signal IP Integration, SoC Integration.
Strong technical knowledge of Front-End flows including Linting, CDC,RDC, Timing Constraints, Power intent, Synthesis, DFT, ATPG, Fault Grading, LEC, MVRC.
Good Understanding of Back-End flows including PNR, STA.
Strong Communication skills to interact with Cross functional teams and lead execution teams.
Good understanding of serial protocols like I2C, SPI, UART, PMBUS
Exposure to Point of Load converters, Gate driver controllers, PMICs etc.
Preferable understanding of Automotive Functional Safety standards.
