Roles and responsibility

Responsibility for Micro architecture , Spec / MRD understanding
Drive Micro architecture document including
Top level Architecture and Block Diagram
Clock/ Reset/ Power Management
SoC Fabrics, Protocol Bridges
Fuse, Boot, Security
IP planning and interfaces
Pinout, ball mapping
Systems and Test planning
Design feasibility and PPA analysis
Develop details for micro architecture for the target SoC to meet chip specifications.
Write MAS / PRD document, drive alignment for IPs with reviews to signoff.
Develop and work with the team to deliver RTL, UPF, timing constraints.
Work with SoC lead to integrate the design and setup the FPGA flows
Review DV, DFT and PD implementation plans for sign-off.




Skills:

Verilog, VHDL, High Speed SerDes protocols understanding, Bus protocols, Clock & power management etc.
Proven track record of having Micro architected, Designed a complex SoC to production
RTL design process: Specifying microarchitecture, developing RTL, linting, UPF coding, delivering synthesizable RTL, guiding Verification, DFT and PD implementations of the design.
Awareness in comprehensive validation techniques of the specific design domain.
Ability to review and signoff implementation of the design.
Hands-on experience working with version control systems.
Work with the FW development team for the design – as required.
Scripting experience for efficiency and quality.




Education & Experience:

CS/ EE/ EC Engineering Graduate or postgraduate with 15+ years of relevant experience.
