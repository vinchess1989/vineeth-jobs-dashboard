We are looking for a CAD Engineer to join our Technical team. This is a great opportunity for an experienced CAD professional to play a key role in supporting product design, development, and manufacturing through the creation and maintenance of accurate 2D and 3D models, technical drawings, and associated documentation.

At Ardent, we exist to advance people and business safety through innovative and trustworthy protection solutions for plant, public transport, and specialist vehicles. As part of our ambitious growth plans, this role offers the chance to contribute to meaningful, safety-critical work within a collaborative and forward-thinking business.

About The Role

As CAD Engineer, you will be responsible for producing detailed CAD drawings and models for components and assemblies, ensuring designs are fully defined, manufacturable, and controlled throughout the product lifecycle. You will manage CAD data, revisions, and workflows, while working closely with internal teams to support technical queries, product development, and manufacturing requirements.

You will also contribute to the preparation of technical and marketing materials to support customer engagement and internal communication. This is a office-based role.

Key Responsibilities For a CAD Engineer


Produce 2D and 3D CAD drawings and models using SolidWorks
Maintain technical drawings, BOMs, and associated documentation
Manage CAD data, revisions, and workflows using SolidWorks PDM
Support product development, prototype work, and technical queries
Work collaboratively with engineering, production, quality, and sales teams


About You


Experienced in a CAD role within manufacturing or engineering
Strong SolidWorks skills
Organised, accurate, and detail-focused
Able to work independently and as part of a wider team
Automotive industry experience would be an advantage


Qualifications

HNC, HND, or equivalent in Mechanical Engineering, Product Design, Electrical Engineering, or a related discipline.
