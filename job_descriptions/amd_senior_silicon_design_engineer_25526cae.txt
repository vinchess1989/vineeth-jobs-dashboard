SENIOR SILICON DESIGN ENGINEER 

 

About The Team

The Networking Technology & Solutions IP Group at AMD is expanding its Data Centre design teams in Edinburgh and Belfast. We architect and deliver high-performance ASIC solutions for next-generation data center networking, enabling large-scale AI training and inference clusters.

Our work spans the full development lifecycle — from architecture definition and algorithm modelling through to RTL implementation, verification, and silicon validation. We operate at the intersection of networking and AI infrastructure, solving complex system-level challenges around bandwidth, latency, scalability, and power efficiency.

We foster a collaborative, high-ownership culture where engineers are encouraged to work across domains and develop a holistic understanding of the system. Joining at this stage offers a unique opportunity to influence both technical direction and team culture as we continue to grow.

About You

You are an experienced silicon design engineer with a proven track record of delivering complex ASICs or subsystems in high-performance networking or compute environments.

You Bring


Strong technical leadership and ownership of large design blocks or subsystems
A system-level mindset with the ability to balance architecture, performance, power, and area trade-offs
Confidence in making high-impact technical decisions and influencing cross-functional teams
A collaborative approach, working effectively across geographies and disciplines



You are motivated by solving challenging problems in AI-scale networking systems and are passionate about delivering high-quality silicon that directly impacts real-world workloads.

About The Role

As a senior member of the design team, you will:


Lead the design and delivery of complex ASIC blocks for data center networking silicon
Contribute to and influence architecture definition and microarchitecture decisions
Translate system requirements into detailed specifications and high-quality RTL implementations
Work closely with verification, physical design, and validation teams to ensure first-silicon success
Drive quality, power, and area optimization across the design
Mentor junior engineers and contribute to engineering best practices
Collaborate across global teams to align on technical direction and execution



Qualifications


Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD in Electronic Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field



Strongly Preferred

Skills and Experience:


Hands on experience in ASIC/SoC design
Strong expertise in Verilog/SystemVerilog (or VHDL)
Proven experience delivering complex digital hardware systems to silicon
Solid understanding of microarchitecture and hardware/software trade-offs
Experience with industry-standard ASIC design, simulation, and synthesis flows
Strong debugging, analytical, and problem-solving skills



Preferred


Experience in high-speed networking, data center, or AI infrastructure silicon
Knowledge of networking protocols (e.g., Ethernet, RDMA, congestion control)
Experience with UVM, formal verification, or advanced verification techniques
Proficiency in scripting (Python, TCL, Perl, or C/C++)
Experience with performance modelling or architectural simulation
Familiarity with CPU/GPU architectures or heterogeneous compute systems
Exposure to AI/ML workloads and infrastructure
Experience leveraging AI-assisted design or productivity tools



Personal Attributes


Strong communicator with the ability to convey complex technical ideas clearly
Self-motivated with a high degree of ownership and accountability
Comfortable working in a fast-paced, evolving technical environment
Collaborative team player with a growth mindset
Passionate about semiconductor and digital design
