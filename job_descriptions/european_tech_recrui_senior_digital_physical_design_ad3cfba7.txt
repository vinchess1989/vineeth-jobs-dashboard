Senior Digital Physical Design Engineer – Physical Implementation / Place & Route / Timing Closure




We are currently partnered with a leading semiconductor company developing advanced SoCs and ASIC solutions for high-performance applications. As part of their expanding silicon engineering team, they are looking to hire a Senior Digital Physical Design Engineer to drive full-chip and block-level implementation activities across complex semiconductor programmes.




This role offers the opportunity to work on advanced-node designs, owning the complete physical design flow from floorplanning through sign-off while collaborating closely with RTL, DFT, verification, and architecture teams to deliver world-class silicon products.




**This is a contracting role**




Key Responsibilities

Drive complete physical implementation flows including floorplanning, power planning, placement, clock tree synthesis (CTS), routing, and physical optimisation
Perform block-level and full-chip physical design implementation for complex ASIC and SoC designs
Own timing closure activities across multiple operating corners and modes using advanced Static Timing Analysis (STA) methodologies
Analyse and resolve timing, signal integrity (SI), IR drop, electromigration (EM), and power-related issues




Key Requirements

10+ years of industry experience in digital physical design, ASIC implementation, or SoC physical design
Extensive hands-on experience across the complete Place & Route (P&R) flow
Strong expertise with Cadence implementation tools including Innovus and Tempus
Strong expertise with Synopsys implementation tools including ICC2 and PrimeTime
Proven experience using Calibre for physical verification and sign-off







Keywords

Physical Design / Digital Physical Design / ASIC Implementation / SoC Physical Design / Place and Route / P&R / Floorplanning / Power Planning / CTS / Clock Tree Synthesis / Routing / Timing Closure / STA / Static Timing Analysis / PrimeTime / Tempus / Innovus / ICC2 / Calibre / DRC / LVS / ERC / Signal Integrity / SI / IR Drop / Electromigration / EM / PPA / Low Power Design / UPF / CPF / Advanced Nodes / Tape-Out / Semiconductor Engineering / ASIC Design




If you are interested in this position, please send a CV to Alistair@eu-recruit.com
