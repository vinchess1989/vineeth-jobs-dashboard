Spara
Nexperia is a world-class company in semiconductor development and in-house production. A proven global player with an entrepreneurial mentality. At our core is an 13,000+ strong international network with a singular focus. Built on passion and commitment to our work, belief in our goals and a drive to succeed regardless of the challenges we face. We support, reward and challenge individuals equally, in a dynamic and energetic environment.

At Nexperia Manchester, we are strengthening our R&D organisation with the addition of a Senior Engineer – Power MOSFET Platform Development. This is an opportunity to shape next generation power semiconductor technologies within a global manufacturer producing over 100 billion devices every year.

In this role, you will contribute across the entire product development lifecycle from early concept and device architecture through design, prototyping, validation, and industrialisation. Your expertise will directly influence the performance, reliability, and manufacturability of future MOSFET platforms.

We are looking for a methodical problem solver who can conceptualise, design, and develop innovative semiconductor power MOSFET technologies. If you enjoy working hands‑on in a collaborative R&D environment where your technical insight is genuinely valued, this role offers the scope and impact you’re looking for.

What You Will Do

As a Platform Development Engineer at Nexperia, you will join a large and highly collaborative R&D organisation with direct exposure to teams across the business. You will work closely with colleagues in R&D, Manufacturing, and Supply Chain to ensure alignment on specifications, technology roadmaps, and long‑term product strategy. You will also interact with external customers, global suppliers, and our assembly and test operations, giving you broad visibility and influence across the full development chain.

Key Responsibilities


Understanding and interpreting the Customer Requirement Specification for new products
Contributing to technology platform and product design using tools such as TCAD, Cadence, and Klayout
Performing data analysis across wafer test, assembly, and qualification stages
Preparing clear technical summaries and communicating complex information to team members and wider stakeholders



What You Will Need

Essential Requirements:


Degree in Electronics Engineering, Semiconductor Physics, Electrical Engineering, or a related discipline
Several years of experience in power semiconductor technology development (Power MOSFETs, ideally Silicon), with strong understanding of device physics, process interactions, manufacturability, and failure mechanisms
Excellent communication skills, with the ability to translate complex technical information for stakeholders, senior management, and cross‑functional teams
Ability to work on site based in Stockport on a Hybrid basis (3 days per week in office)



Desirable Skills & Experience


Project management experience with the ability to multitask across development streams
Demonstrated experience using design tools like TCAD, Cadence or Klayout
Experience with statistical analysis tools such as Minitab, Statistica or SPSS
Hands‑on experience with root cause analysis, including 8D, 5‑Why, Fishbone diagrams and containment strategies



Interview process


Stage 1: Recruiter Review
Stage 2: 30-60‑minute Technical Interview
Stage 3: 60‑minute Behavioural & Competency Interview
