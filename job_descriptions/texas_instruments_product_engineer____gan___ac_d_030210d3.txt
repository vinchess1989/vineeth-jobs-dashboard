⚡ Power the Future — Be the Engineer Behind World-Class High Voltage Power ICs at Texas Instruments




At Texas Instruments, our High Voltage Power (HVP) team develops some of the world's most advanced power solutions — AC/DC controllers, isolated DC/DC converters, GaN-integrated power stages, and modules capable of withstanding several hundreds of volts. Now, we're expanding this capability to Bangalore — and we're looking for a talented Product Engineer to be part of this exciting journey from the very beginning.




🔎 About the Opportunity




This is a rare startup-like opportunity within the safety of TI's global umbrella. You won't just join a team — you'll help build one. As part of TI's new HVP investment in India, you'll work in a dynamic, entrepreneurial environment with access to TI's world-class tools, global manufacturing network, and deep technical expertise.

We are looking for a driven Product Engineer (3–10 years of experience) to join the HVP team in Bangalore — responsible for product qualification, reliability, yield management, characterization, and production release of high-voltage industrial and automotive power controller ICs and GaN power stages.







📌 Key Responsibilities




Collaborate cross-functionally with Design, Applications, Test, Marketing, Manufacturing, Quality & Reliability teams in all aspects of new product development and production release
Work with process and package technology development to qualify technologies for new products
Partner with global manufacturing facilities to address product quality, yield management, and cost reduction on mature products — including product spins and derivatives
Develop and implement Qualification plans — including sHTOL, dHTOL, ELFR, BHAST, and PTC board design for analog product qualification
Perform evaluation, debug, and characterization of integrated circuits to ensure they meet product requirement specs and manufacturing requirements
Work with Failure Analysis (FA) teams to determine root causes of reliability and qualification failures
Coordinate with planning teams to ensure smooth device ramp post Release to Manufacturing (RTM)
Qualify second-source wafer fab, Assembly/Test (A/T) facilities, and BOM to ensure device continuity of supply
Drive test conversion, test time reduction, and yield improvements to achieve device entitlement and operational transfer cost (OTC)
Partner with wafer fabrication and A/T sites to guarantee schedule, cost, and quality targets
Communicate weekly to management on project status and key milestones




🛠️ Minimum Requirements




Bachelor's/Master's degree in Electrical/Electronics Engineering or related field
3–10 years of experience in product engineering, IC qualification, or semiconductor product development
Strong understanding of analog IC product development processes — qualification, characterization, yield, and reliability
Experience designing qualification boards (sHTOL, dHTOL, ELFR, BHAST, PTC) for analog products
Proven ability to debug and characterize integrated circuits against product requirement specifications
Experience working with wafer fab and assembly/test manufacturing sites
Strong communication, cross-functional collaboration, and project management skills
Ability to own deliverables and schedules independently with minimal supervision




🌟 Preferred Qualifications




Experience in high-voltage power IC product engineering — AC/DC controllers, DC/DC converters, or GaN power stages
Familiarity with GaN semiconductor technology and wide-bandgap power device qualification
Experience in second-source qualification of wafer fab, A/T facilities, and BOM management
Knowledge of test conversion, test time reduction, and OTC optimization
Prior experience working with global manufacturing networks across fab, assembly, and test sites
Understanding of failure analysis (FA) methods for power IC reliability and qualification
Experience with device ramp planning and RTM coordination
Strong analytical skills in yield management and cost reduction for mature products




💡 Why Texas Instruments HVP Bangalore?




🚀 Startup Energy: Join a new team being built from scratch — your contributions define the culture and technical capability

🌍 Global Impact: Work on TI's world-class HVP portfolio — AC/DC, DC/DC, GaN, and high-voltage modules

🏭 End-to-End Ownership: Own the full product lifecycle — from qualification to manufacturing release to yield entitlement

🤝 Cross-Functional Exposure: Work with Design, Test, FA, Manufacturing, Marketing, and Quality teams globally

💰 Compensation: Competitive salary, comprehensive benefits & long-term incentives

🔬 Technology : Work on GaN, high-voltage industrial and automotive power controller ICs — one of the fastest-growing domains in semiconductors




📣 Ready to Build Something Extraordinary at TI's Newest HVP Team in Bangalore?




If you're passionate about power IC product engineering, qualification, and yield excellence — and excited by the rare opportunity to build a team and technical capability from the ground up — we want to hear from you.
