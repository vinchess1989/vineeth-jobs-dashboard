Our vision is to transform how the world uses information to enrich life for all.

Micron Technology is a world leader in innovating memory and storage solutions that accelerate the transformation of information into intelligence, inspiring the world to learn, communicate and advance faster than ever.

Key Responsibilities


Contribute to the development of new product opportunities by assisting with the overall design, layout, and optimization of DRAM Memory array/Peripheral Logic/Analog circuits
Actively leverage AI‑based tools, agentic workflows, and automation frameworks to:
Accelerate schematic optimization and design‑space exploration
Assist in parasitic analysis, sensitivity studies, and corner coverage
Improve debug efficiency across simulation and silicon learning loops
Define semicustom methodology for memory design
Collaborate with CAD, EDA, and software teams to integrate AI‑driven flows into simulation, verification, and design sign‑off environments.
Oversee and manage the layout process including floor-planning, placement, and routing
Perform circuit-level verification including functional, timing, power and reliability analysis using industry standard and Micron-specific simulators and tools.
Contribute to cross-group communication to work towards standardization and group success
Drive innovation into the future memory generations and design methodology within a dynamic work environment


Minimum Qualifications


Master’s degree in electrical or computer engineering with 4-8 years of experience in memory design.
Strong fundamentals in CMOS circuit design, device physics and memory operation.
Experience with SPICE-based simulation and parasitic extraction.


Preferred Qualifications


Domain knowledge in DRAM design (DDR5, LPDDR5 or HBM or in any other memory related field)
Domain knowledge in NAND memory design
Exposure to or strong interest in AI/ML-assisted design flows, agentic AI or advanced automation in circuit design and verification.
Experience on SoC / ASIC semicustom flow
Strong communication and collaboration skills.
