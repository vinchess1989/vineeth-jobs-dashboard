About SiTime

SiTime is the Precision Timing company.

Timing is the heartbeat of all electronics, ensuring performance, resilience and scalability. For decades, quartz devices, non-silicon technology, have kept systems in sync, but they struggle in harsher, more demanding environments. MEMS-based Precision Timing delivers greater accuracy, smaller size and resilience. Today, MEMS timing powers over 400 applications, including high-growth ones in AI datacenters, automated driving, industrial and humanoid robots, wearables and IoT.

Our semiconductor MEMS programmable solutions offer a rich feature set that enables customers to differentiate their products with higher performance, smaller size, lower power, and better reliability. With more than 4 billion devices shipped, SiTime is changing the timing industry. For more information, visit: www.sitime.com .

Job Summary

The Engineer II, Post-Silicon Validation is responsible for productizing SiTime’s revolutionary MEMS-based timing products. Responsibilities include mixed-signal post-silicon validation and characterization, system algorithms, firmware development, support to hardware development for test automation, test functions software development, execution of Failure Analysis, conduct experiments to prototype new system architectures for future products, and finally cross-functional interaction with MEMS, CMOS, NPI, Operations, Marketing organizations.

It is not necessary to meet all job requirements to be a qualified candidate for the position.

Responsibilities


Validation, characterization, and release-to-production of MEMS-based timing products.
Work with MEMS and circuit design teams to evaluate new silicon at system and building block level, recommend design improvements for enhanced performance.
Develop and carry out experiments to prototype new system architectures and technologies.
Design, develop, and validate PCB test platforms and firmware to enable automated data acquisition & analysis.
Develop and transfer algorithms to final test and production environments.
Perform engineering failure analysis to root cause product issues.


Qualifications & Requirement


MS or PhD in Electrical or Mechanical Engineering.
2+ years of experience in the productization of semiconductor sensor-based devices.
Fundamental knowledge and understanding of analog and mixed-signal circuits.
Hands-on experience with electronics lab instrumentation and measurements setups. Prior experience with metrology systems for evaluating timing parameters is preferred but not required.
Very good programming skills with hands-on experience in MATLAB, Python, C, C++, including object-oriented programming.
Very good statistical analysis skills.


Desired Characteristics & Attributes


High level of self-motivation and drive to make an impact.
Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
Clear and concise written and verbal communication skills.
Ability to work in a highly collaborative, fast-paced team environment.
