Process Engineer - Semiconductors - Etch, Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) or Epitaxy - Greenock




Bluegate are proud to partner with a global giant in the field of design and manufacture of electronic components.




We have a requirement for experienced Process Engineer that has worked on split gate MOSFETs, to assist with continued growth within their manufacturing facility based in Greenock.




You will come from a semiconductor background and have hands on knowledge and experience of Wet Etch, Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) or Epitaxy.




You will be offered a competitive salary, regular pay reviews, relocation assistance (if necessary) and promotional opportunities.







Key Skills / responsibilities / Background – Process Engineer – Greenock




- Experience within the semiconductor technology industry

- Expert on Wet Etch, Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) or Epitaxy processes.

- Qualified ideally to degree level minimum in related field

- Ongoing control of Static Process Control (SPC)

- Controlling process performance, maintain efficient workflow and implementing improvements




This is a great job opportunity to join a growing company who can offer career progression and personal development.







APPLY NOW! Or for more information on the Process Engineer role, based in Greenock, please contact Tommy Beazley quoting job reference 414TRB.
