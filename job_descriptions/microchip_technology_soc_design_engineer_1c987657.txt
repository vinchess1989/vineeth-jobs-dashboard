Job Description

As a SoC Design Engineer in the MPU group, you will work with our multi-sited global Silicon Development Team in design and development of complex SoCs. The work will include SoC design integration, microarchitecture, design verification, as well as supporting post silicon bring up.

Responsibilities


Design and integration of complex IPs and subsystems into SoC
Understanding SoC requirements and architecture specifications
Understand IP specifications and work with IP/architecture/application teams to correctly integrate the IPs into SOCs, meeting the chip level requirements.
Perform RTL coding
Run RTL checks – Lint, CDC, RDC
Develop verification plan for subsystems/SoC
Collaborate with cross functional teams in architecture and IP evaluations, performance, power analysis, pre and post silicon validation
Overcome design challenges, support achievement of verification goals, provide debug support
Hold spec reviews, code reviews, coverage analysis, etc. to meet quality goals
Support development of timing constraints and synthesis
Power Estimation and Analysis


Requirements/Qualifications

Qualifications/Requirements


Good understanding of SoC architecture fundamentals and digital design
Working experience in RTL coding using Verilog/SystemVerilog
Understanding of ARM AMBA bus protocols
Knowledge and exposure of complete SOC RTL to GDS to silicon release flow
Proficiency in any one of commonly used scripting languages (Perl, Python, TCL)
Knowledge of revision control tools such as SVN, GIT etc.
Familiarity with AI-assisted development tools (e.g., Claude Code, Codex) for code generation/review, with understanding of responsible use (verification, IP/confidentiality, and compliance with company policies)
Superior Written and Verbal Communication skills


Preferred Skills


Experience in ARM or RISC-V CPU based SoC development
Hands on experience of C/System-C
Experience/Understanding of verification using UVM


Required Education


BSEE minimum, MSEE preferred.
