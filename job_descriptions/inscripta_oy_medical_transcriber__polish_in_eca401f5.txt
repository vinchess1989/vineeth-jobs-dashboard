Medical Transcriber (Polish)
Inscripta Oy  Remote FreelanceFull-time
Salary €1,600 - €2,200

We are looking for a detail-oriented Polish Medical Transcriber to support the development and quality assurance of our speech recognition solutions. The ideal candidate is a native Polish speaker with a strong command of Polish grammar and linguistics, combined with professional-level English skills. This role requires great precision, effective collaboration with cross-functional teams, and independent task management. This role is a starting point with opportunities to expand responsibilities as we grow.




Key qualifications & skills

Native proficiency in Polish, with excellent knowledge of grammar, syntax, and linguistic nuance

Professional English skills (written and spoken)

Experience in medical transcription. Reviewing and validating medical speech data for AI training is highly valued.

Strong attention to detail and commitment to linguistic accuracy

Technical confidence and ability to quickly adapt to new tools and systems

Ability to work both independently and as part of a collaborative team

Willingness to travel to Finland, especially at the beginning of employment




Traits we are looking for

Systematic, careful, and quality-driven

Strong sense of responsibility and ownership of work

Open to feedback and continuous improvement

Comfortable working in a fast-paced, technology-driven environment




What we offer

Work for a young and dynamic company, as a contractor or employee

Opportunities for professional growth, both within the role and across the company

The possibility for the role to evolve based on your strengths and interests, potentially including customer-facing responsibilities

Starting salary: €1,600–€2,200 (7000-9200 PLN) per month, depending on experience and qualifications




This role is ideal for someone who enjoys working at the intersection of language, technology, and healthcare, and who takes pride in linguistic precision.

Inscripta is a Finnish pioneer in speech recognition for the healthcare and social care sectors. We make documentation faster, smoother, and higher quality.

Our solution is powered by our proprietary, ever-evolving speech recognition AI, specifically designed for the demands of professional medical documentation.

Inscripta Direct enables professionals to dictate directly into patient record systems. On average, our users save 45 minutes every day: time that can be reinvested into better care, lighter workdays, and more efficient use of resources.

At Inscripta, we build technology that serves people. Our values: Balance, Collaboration, Trust, and Growth guide how we work and what we create. We help healthcare professionals spend less time on documentation and more time with people.

We envision a future where documentation supports, not slows down, professional work. A future where advanced speech recognition AI works accurately, and reliably to make everyday tasks lighter and more meaningful.

By combining our cutting-edge technology with deep understanding of clinical workflows, we strive to improve working life in healthcare, one dictated note at a time.

We’re building the best team in the industry. We're looking for curious and ambitious people who want to shape the future of healthcare with meaningful technology. If you're ready to make a real impact on healthcare — join our growing team!
