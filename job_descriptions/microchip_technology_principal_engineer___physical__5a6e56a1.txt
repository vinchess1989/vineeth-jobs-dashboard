Job Description

This role supports Physical Design responsibilities for 32-bit microcontrollers and wireless solutions in collaboration with MCU32 and WSG Business Units at Microchip.

Responsibilities


Execute standard Physical Design implementation practices.
Conduct die size estimations and manage bond-out approvals.
Achieve timing closure to optimize process node performance.
Expertise in timing closure and debugging.
Setup, debug, and synthesize clock trees for high-quality QoR.
Design and support multi-voltage island-based floorplans.
Develop and automate design flows.
Deliver Physical Verification (PV)-compliant designs.
Collaborate with external vendors and IP providers to resolve issues.
Work closely with international design and implementation teams.


Requirements/Qualifications

Qualifications:


Minimum B.Tech/M.Tech in Electronics or related field.
10+ years of experience in Physical design and timing aspects of IC design, with leadership capability.


Key Skills


Expertise in VLSI logic design, clock tree synthesis/debugging, and timing closure.
Proficiency in place-and-route and low-power design methodologies.
Experience in system-level floorplanning, power network planning, and power integrity/reliability (EM/IR) analysis.
Strong knowledge of Innovus, Redhawk, and Calibre tools.
Proficiency in Tcl and Perl scripting.
Excellent debugging, analytical, and leadership skills.
Strong communication skills and interpersonal abilities.


Travel Time

0% - 25%
