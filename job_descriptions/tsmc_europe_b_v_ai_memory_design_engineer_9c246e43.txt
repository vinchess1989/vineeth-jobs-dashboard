Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company Ltd. (“TSMC”) is the world’s leading semiconductor foundry. Headquartered in Taiwan, the Company has operation facilities worldwide and the largest logic chip manufacturing capacity in global semiconductor industry. The Company employs a global workforce of over 89.000 and recorded revenue over US$122 billion in 2025. TSMC produces the chips that are found in smart phones, tablets, computers, smart TVs, cars, laptops, games consoles, data centers and many more applications. TSMC sells to a wide variety of customers, including most leading semiconductor companies.
At the TSMC EU Design Center, you’ll work in an exceptional technology and design team to refine your technical and leadership skills within the world's most advanced design service ecosystem. You'll be contributing to the forefront of semiconductor technology, including cutting-edge nonvolatile memory solutions like MRAM and RRAM. Your main focus will be to assist our leading customers in delivering state-of-the-art products that have a transformative impact on people's lives.
Job Responsibilities
Your work will enable chip innovation possible by developing and optimizing all aspects in the pre-silicon design flow, including:


1. Develop memory IP and compilers targeting AI applications optimized with TSMC process.
2. Develop test chips to validate the memory IP.


(1) Conduct rigorous testing to ensure reliability and scalability of developed solutions.


Responsibilities for developing memory test chips and IP include:


1. Conduct circuit design or RTL coding.
2. Perform design verification.
3. Review and verifying layout reports.
4. Prepare design reports.
5. Collaborate on test chip implementation.
6. Assist with silicon characterization and reliability qualification.
7. Contribute to front-end and back-end design kit generation.
8. Analyze and debug silicon data.
9. Provide technical support to customers.
Job Qualifications
1. A Master’s degree or higher in Electrical Engineering or a related field.
2. Over 5 years of experience in circuit and logic design for embedded memory technologies. Experience in compute-in-memory or AI algorithms is considered a strong advantage.
3. Proficiency with EDA tools and expertise in design methodologies for custom or digital design in the field of non-volatile memory.


Only the applications relevant to the criteria above will be taken for further consideration.
