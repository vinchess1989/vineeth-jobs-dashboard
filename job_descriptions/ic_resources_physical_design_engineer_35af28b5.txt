Physical Design Engineer - Eindhoven




An ambitious deep-tech startup developing next-generation AI computing technologies is looking for a Physical Design Engineer to join the team.




Responsibilities

Support RTL-to-GDS implementation activities for advanced semiconductor designs
Perform synthesis and analyse timing, area and power results
Contribute to floorplanning and physical implementation planning
Generate and evaluate PPA metrics to support architectural and design decisions
Develop and maintain implementation constraints and design collateral
Work closely with RTL, architecture and verification teams to optimise design quality
Collaborate with external implementation partners and provide technical guidance throughout the physical design flow
Assist with design closure activities and implementation reviews
