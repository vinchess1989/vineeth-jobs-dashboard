Staff Physical Design Engineer (CPU Cores)

The Role

We are seeking a seasoned Physical Design Leader with expertise in Microprocessor Design and Design Techniques. You are meticulous about Power, Performance and Area while balancing the business needs. This senior role will stretch you as you lead the physical design team to help create new design for AMD.

The Person


You have excellent communication and presentation skills, demonstrated through technical publications, presentations, trainings, executive briefings, etc.
You are highly adept at collaboration among top-thinkers and engineers alike, ready to mentor and guide, and help to elevate the knowledge and skills of the team around you.
You are experienced in leading teams of senior engineers



Key Responsibilities


Primary technical contact for your focus area
Pro-actively works with peers in technology and design reviews
Quantify impacts of technology issues in design reviews
Represent the CPU Cores to executive management from a technical perspective.
Work cross functionally across CPU Cores team to identify and assess complex technical issues/risks, and develop solutions
Work closely with Design teams for Area and Floorplan refinement, Timing targets, Power targets



Preferred Experience


Experience leading technical teams
Experienced in high-speed microprocessor designs
Ability to create new design ideas and flow
Knowledge of current sub-micro technologies
Solid understanding Physical Design tools and flows or custom circuit design



Preferred Education And Experience


Electrical Engineering Degree with 5+ years of experience preferred
