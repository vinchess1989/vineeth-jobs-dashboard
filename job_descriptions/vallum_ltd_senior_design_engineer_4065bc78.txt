We're currently looking for an experienced Design Engineer to join our client's High Power division. In this role, you'll be part of a team responsible for the design and development of main circuits and power electronics for next-generation frequency converters.

This is an excellent opportunity to work on technically challenging product development projects in an international engineering environment, collaborating with multidisciplinary teams and contributing to innovative high-power solutions.




You will:

Design, dimension, and verify the main circuit and power electronics of frequency converters
Specify verification tests and analyze results
Create and maintain design documentation
Research and evaluate new components and topologies
Collaborate with subcontractors and cross-functional engineering teams, including mechanical, electronics, and verification teams
Mentor and support junior engineers
Participate in product development projects within the High Power division




We're looking for someone with:

M.Sc. in Power Electronics or a related field
Relevant experience in product development projects
Strong knowledge of electrical drives' main circuit topologies
Deep understanding of power semiconductors and inductive components
Proven experience in filter design
Proficiency with simulation tools such as Simulink
Fluent English communication skills, both written and spoken




It would be considered an advantage if you also have:

Finnish language skills
Experience mentoring junior engineers




We're primarily looking for professionals with 5+ years of relevant industry experience. However, candidates with a strong academic background and exceptional technical potential may also be considered, even with fewer years of experience.

Assignment details:

Start: August–September (according to agreement)
Duration: Initial 12-month contract
Location: Helsinki, Pitäjänmäki (50% hybrid)
Allocation: 100%
Language: English is required; Finnish is considered an advantage
