We Are:

At Synopsys, we drive the innovations that shape the way we live and connect. Our technology is central to the Era of Pervasive Intelligence, from self-driving cars to learning machines. We lead in chip design, verification, and IP integration, empowering the creation of high-performance silicon chips and software content. Join us to transform the future through continuous technological innovation.

You Are:

You are an accomplished technical leader with a deep passion for driving excellence in electronics simulation. You possess a strong academic background (MS/PhD) in Electrical or Electronics Engineering, specializing in Electromagnetics, and have spent over a decade advancing your expertise in high frequency domains. Your hands-on experience with electronics simulation tools such as HFSS, SIwave, Q3D, and EMC Plus enables you to tackle complex challenges and provide innovative solutions. You thrive in customer-facing environments, adept at translating intricate technical concepts into clear, actionable guidance that empowers clients and colleagues alike.

Your leadership journey has equipped you with the ability to mentor and guide multidisciplinary teams, fostering a culture of continuous learning and technical mastery. You are strategic, collaborative, and highly adaptive, with a proven track record of building domain expertise across SI/PI, EMI/EMC, and RF/Antenna applications. You are driven by a desire to make a tangible impact—both within your team and across the broader organization—by championing customer success, shaping product development, and positioning India as a global center of technical excellence. Your professionalism, business acumen, and organizational skills ensure you deliver results in fast-paced, dynamic environments. Fluent in English and eager to embrace cross-cultural collaboration, you are ready to lead, inspire, and advance the future of electronics simulation at Synopsys.

What You’ll Be Doing:


Leading pre-sales technical activities for Ansys Electronics high frequency solutions, including HFSS, SIwave, Q3D, EMC Plus, and Charge Plus.
Partner with India and APAC sales and channel teams to support account planning and opportunity execution.
Acting as the senior technical escalation point for complex customer challenges in India and select APAC accounts.
Supporting proof-of-concepts, benchmarks, competitive evaluations, and executive technical discussions to drive customer engagement.
Guiding customers through end-to-end simulation workflows spanning package, board, and system levels.
Mentoring and developing a team of Application Engineers, defining skill development plans and career pathways aligned with global standards.
Building and sustaining strong domain expertise across SI/PI, EMI/EMC, and RF/Antenna domains.
Driving customer onboarding, enablement, adoption, renewals, and technical expansion initiatives.
Collaborating with APAC and global application engineering leaders to share technical assets, best practices, and customer learnings.
Providing structured customer feedback to influence product improvements, roadmap priorities, and beta initiatives.
Representing Ansys India at technical seminars, webinars, user conferences, and ecosystem events.
Developing reusable demos, reference workflows, and technical enablement material to strengthen India’s technical delivery hub.


The Impact You Will Have:


Positioning India as a global center of technical excellence within Ansys’ ecosystem.
Accelerating customer adoption and technical success of Ansys Electronics high frequency solutions across India and APAC.
Enhancing the capabilities and expertise of the Application Engineering team through mentorship and skill development.
Driving innovation and customer satisfaction by resolving complex technical challenges and influencing product development.
Building strong partnerships with sales, channel, and global teams to maximize business opportunities and customer outcomes.
Contributing to the creation and dissemination of best practices, technical assets, and enablement materials that benefit both customers and internal teams.
Shaping the future direction of Ansys Electronics products through structured feedback and active participation in roadmap initiatives.
Representing Ansys India as a thought leader at industry events, strengthening brand reputation and technical community engagement.


What You’ll Need:


MS/PhD in Electrical/Electronics Engineering or equivalent, specialized in Electromagnetics.
10+ years of experience in the high frequency domain, with deep hands-on expertise in electronics simulation.
5+ years of experience using electronics simulation tools or multiphysics engineering solutions.
5+ years of experience leading or mentoring technical teams in a customer-facing environment.
Strong knowledge of SI/PI, EMI/EMC, RF, or power electronics simulation domains.
Proven ability to communicate complex technical concepts clearly and effectively to diverse audiences.
Logical problem-solving skills, excellent interpersonal and communication abilities, and fluency in English.
Strong organizational and time management skills, with a sense of urgency and drive for high performance.
Ability to travel up to 25% of the time as needed.
Leadership presence, cross-cultural collaboration skills, and a strategic mindset.
Must have passion and drive for personal technical excellence and high performance.
Must have customer service aptitude and maintain customer focus.
Must have skills to demonstrates business acumen, driven to succeed


Who You Are:


A collaborative leader and mentor who inspires technical excellence and teamwork.
Customer-focused, with a passion for delivering impactful solutions and building strong relationships.
Adaptable and resilient, thriving in fast-paced environments and navigating ambiguity with confidence.
Strategic thinker, capable of aligning technical initiatives with broader business goals.
Professional, organized, and driven to succeed with a high degree of integrity and business acumen.
Effective communicator—both written and verbal—with the ability to influence stakeholders at all levels.


The Team You’ll Be A Part Of:

You will lead the Electronics High Frequency Application Engineering team in India, collaborating closely with multi-disciplinary teams across APAC and global Ansys organizations. The team is dedicated to advancing technical excellence, driving customer success, and fostering innovation through domain expertise in SI/PI, EMI/EMC, and RF/Antenna simulation. Together, you will shape the future of electronics simulation and reinforce India’s role as a key technical delivery hub within Ansys’ global ecosystem.

Rewards and Benefits:

We offer a comprehensive range of health, wellness, and financial benefits to cater to your needs. Our total rewards include both monetary and non-monetary offerings. Your recruiter will provide more details about the salary range and benefits during the hiring process.
