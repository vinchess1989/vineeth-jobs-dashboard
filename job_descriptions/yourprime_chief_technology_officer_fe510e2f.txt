Chief Technology Officer

AI SaaS Scale-Up

Ireland (Predominantly Remote)

€130,000 - €150,000 + Benefits







Whether you're an experienced CTO looking for a business where you can genuinely shape the future, or an ambitious Head of Engineering, VP Engineering or Technical Leader ready for your first CTO opportunity, we'd love to hear from you.




This is a rare opportunity to join a privately-owned SaaS company at a pivotal stage of growth as it launches an exciting new generation of AI-native products.




Having partnered with the business on a number of successful hires, we've seen first-hand the quality of the leadership team, the culture they've built and the ambition behind the product roadmap.




The engineering foundations are already in place.

Now they're looking for someone to lead the next chapter.







The Opportunity




As CTO, you'll work closely with the founders to define the company's long-term technology strategy while remaining close enough to engineering to influence architecture, technical direction and product innovation.




You'll inherit a talented development team and have the opportunity to shape both the technology function and engineering culture as the business continues to grow.




This role would suit someone who enjoys building, improving and innovating, rather than simply maintaining existing systems.




Whether you've already held a CTO title or you're currently leading engineering within a scaling SaaS business and feel ready for the next step, this could be the opportunity you've been waiting for.




You'll Have The Opportunity To




Define the company's long-term technology strategy.
Continue developing a high-performing engineering team and culture.
Help shape the next generation of AI-native products.
Introduce modern engineering practices that improve quality and delivery.
Work alongside the founders on product and commercial strategy.
Drive innovation through practical adoption of AI across both products and engineering.




Technology

Current technologies include:

Backend

TypeScript
Node.js
NestJS

Frontend

Angular

Cloud

Microsoft Azure
Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS)
Kubernetes
Microservices
CI/CD




Whilst experience with this stack is beneficial, we're far more interested in your ability to lead engineering teams, make sound technical decisions and build scalable software businesses.







We'd Love To Speak To People Who




Have experience leading software engineering teams.
Have worked within SaaS, software or technology businesses.
Enjoy balancing technical leadership with commercial thinking.
Are passionate about AI and modern software engineering practices.
Have experience building or scaling software products.
Enjoy mentoring engineers and developing future technical leaders.
Want to play a key role in shaping a growing technology business.
