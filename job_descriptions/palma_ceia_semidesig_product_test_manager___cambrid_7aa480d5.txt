Palma Ceia Semi-Design (PCS), is a fast-growing, fabless semiconductor company, focused on complete integrated Wi-Fi and LTE solutions for the IoT marketplace. We require a product/test manager to join their Silicon Operations team in Cambridge, UK. Successful candidates will have extensive knowledge and experience of semiconductors.

Essential Duties And Responsibilities

include the following. Other duties may be assigned.

Product/Test Engineering


Develop Product Yield Enhancement & Quality improvement methods as products ramp to production.
Interface with suppliers to resolve issues in a timely manner.
Review of customers and supplier specifications to ensure that customer needs will be met by Palma Ceia Semi Design products.
Ensure the smooth transition of products from early-stage sampling to HVM on time and within the quality and cost expectations.
Interface with all relevant departments from R&D through sales and marketing to ensure product offering meets time and cost deadlines.
Define & drive product cost reduction programs.


Test Development


For each new product or product variant requiring a new test solution:


I. Develop a test plan with written definitions how to test each block.

II. Agree what characterisation and reliability testing will be required.


Work with R&D to ensure that best DFT techniques are employed from an early stage.
Working with R&D devise optimum test solutions such as BIST etc. to minimize cost.


Processes and Procedures


Create suitable product and test development procedures. These procedures should cover all aspects of vendor and subcontractor control and management.
Procedures should include all aspects of product and test development, test control, product handling, yield targets, quality targets, etc.


Skills And Experience

Essential Experience:


BS/MS degree Physics, EE or equivalent industrial experience
Minimum 4 years of direct experience high tech manufacturing environment
Familiarity with electronic manufacturing systems particularly in the Semiconductor industry
Knowledge of product qualification methods incl. FA
Product and test engineering knowledge of wireless products
Familiarity with the design, NPI & YE processes involved in a fabless semiconductor company
Working with external suppliers and partners
Silicon Foundry and OSAT experience
Statistical analysis – e.g. JMP


Desirable Experience


Good understanding of analog/RF and digital IC design methodologies and circuit architectures
Familiarity with fab tape-out process
Experience with quality management systems
Package substrate design
Knowledge of BT, Wi-Fi, Zigbee or nb-IoT
Effective use of Microsoft Office presentation tools – Word, Power Point, and Excel plus Outlook and Teams


Attributes

Essential:


Strong communication skills (written, verbal, and presentation)
Strong team-player
Can-do attitude
Ability to work independent of management, yet within a team environment of constantly changing priorities
Ability to work on multiple quality projects simultaneously
Strong attention to detail
Willingness to get involved in whatever needs doing


Desirable


Knowledge of Mandarin


Additional Information


Job Site: Cambridge, UK
Email resumes to: careers@pcsemi.com.
No calls. EOE.
