We are partnering with an innovative space technology company developing next-generation Earth observation satellites capable of detecting and responding to critical changes anywhere on Earth with unprecedented speed and accuracy. Their capabilities support mission-critical applications across defence, maritime, intelligence, and security sectors.




They are seeking a Senior Electronics Engineer to lead the design and delivery of high-reliability mixed-signal electronics for satellite platforms. This is a hands-on technical leadership role, with ownership of critical hardware systems and influence over long-term engineering direction and best practices.




Responsibilities:




Lead the design, development, validation, and qualification of mixed-signal electronics for space applications
Own hardware systems end-to-end, from concept through testing, manufacturing, and in-orbit performance
Drive system architecture decisions to ensure scalability, reliability, and mission success
Coordinate cross-functional development activities across engineering disciplines
Establish and promote engineering best practices in hardware design, testing, and documentation
Troubleshoot and resolve issues across the lifecycle, including development, production, and on-orbit anomalies
Use data and industry insights to inform design decisions and improve system performance
Mentor engineers and contribute to building a high-performing hardware team




Essential Skills & Experience:




10+ years’ experience in electronics engineering, ideally in aerospace, embedded, or other mission-critical environments
Strong background in board-level design, including schematics, PCB layout, bring-up, and validation
Experience leading complex, cross-functional projects with system-level impact
Proven ability to solve complex technical problems using advanced design and measurement techniques
Expertise in lab testing and system-level validation, with confident use of standard test equipment
Strong skills in debugging and root-cause analysis at board and system level
Ability to balance technical performance with cost, manufacturability, and schedule
Clear communicator, able to work effectively across teams and stakeholders




Desirable Skills:




Degree in Electrical Engineering or related field (BSc/MSc/PhD)
Experience in the aerospace or space industry
Familiarity with radiation effects, thermal constraints, and in-orbit operations
Experience with Python or scripting in Linux environments




We welcome applications from international candidates and are able to provide visa sponsorship for highly qualified individuals.




Please contact Yasmin Hamdy at yasmin.h@auxotalent.com for more information.
